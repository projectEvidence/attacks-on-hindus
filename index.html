Date of Incident: January 21, 2025
District: 18
Upazilla: 145
Police Station: 538
Specific Location: Jalkhabar Restaurant, Rd Number 2, Dhaka 1212. Google Maps: Q9JX+8MW

Involved Crimes

  1. Loot
  2. Robbery
  3. Shop vandalism
  4. Physical assault
  5. Extortion
Miscreants attacked, vandalized and looted a snack restaurant and a dessert shop in the capital's Mohakhali after the extortion was not paid. The incident took place on Tuesday (January 21) evening. The snack shop operators said that 40-50 unknown people suddenly attacked and vandalized the restaurant after the extortion was not paid. During this, the attackers looted 20-30 thousand taka from the cash counter. The snack shop operators said that they had never seen the attackers before. A case has been filed with Banani Police Station in this incident. However, the police refused to comment on the matter. Description source: https://ajkerdesh.com/archives/129602
