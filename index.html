Hefazat-e-Islam Chandina, Comilla Branch brought out a Procession on February 07, 2025 Threatening to Slaughter ISKCON Members and Demolishing ISKCON places
Date of Incident: February 7, 2025
Division: Chattogram
District: Comilla
Upazilla: Chandina
Police Station: Chandina
Details
On Friday, Feb 7, 2025 after Jumu'ah prayer, extremists under the leadership of the secretary of the Chandina Pourashava Branch of Hefazat-e-Islam brought out a procession today after Jumu'ah prayer to stop the anniversary celebration of ISKCON and their temple's foundation preparation work in Chandina, Comilla.

The terrorists shouted slogans calling for the killing of Hindus and ISKCON members. They shouted: "Grab ISKCON members one by one and slaughter". They shouted to demolish ISKCON's places.

As shown in the video, the secretary of Hefazat-e-Islam Chandina Pourashava Branch thanked the local adminstration and police station OC to let them hold this hate speech procession. The video shows that the secretary delivered his hate speech at the gate of the police station. He also thanked Hazrat Maulana Sadek Mahmud Binnuri (spelling of name transcribed from his speech), the Grand Mufti of Ulama-e-Deoband oikya parishad "Motua" (spelling review needed for "Motua") board and Senior Vice President of Hefazat-e-Islam, Chandina Pourashava Branch.

Attack, vandalism and looting at Jalkhabar Restaurant and Mistanna Bhandar "জলখাবার রেস্টুরেন্ট ও মিষ্টান্ন ভান্ডার" in Mohakhali for not getting demanded money
Date of Incident: January 21, 2025
Division: Dhaka
District: Dhaka
Upazilla: Dhaka City
Police Station: Banani
Specific Location: Jalkhabar Restaurant, Rd Number 2, Dhaka 1212. Google Maps: Q9JX+8MW
Details
Miscreants attacked, vandalized and looted a snack restaurant and a dessert shop in the capital's Mohakhali after the extortion was not paid.
The incident took place on Tuesday (January 21) evening. The snack shop operators said that 40-50 unknown people suddenly attacked and vandalized the restaurant after the extortion was not paid. During this, the attackers looted 20-30 thousand taka from the cash counter.
The snack shop operators said that they had never seen the attackers before. A case has been filed with Banani Police Station in this incident. However, the police refused to comment on the matter.

Hindu Youth lynched by Islamist mob in Chittagong
Date of Incident: December 31, 2024
Division: Chattogram
District: Chattogram
Police Station: Patenga
Specific Location: Kathgar, Patenga
Details
A young Hindu man called Pranto Talukder was forcefully abducted by a radical islamist mob and lynched. He was brutally beaten.
The whole incident was captured in CCTV footage.
This incident happened in Kathgar, Patenga, Chittagong on January 01, 2025.
Later he was admitted to chittagong medical college.
The islamist mob claimed that Pranto Talukder wrote something blasphemous on facebook. However, nobody could corroborate such claim.
Such incidents is increasing day by day in Bangladesh. Police have neither filed any case nor arrested anyone.
