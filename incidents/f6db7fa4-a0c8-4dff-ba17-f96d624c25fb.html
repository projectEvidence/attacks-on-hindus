23. Muslim goons Abu Saleh (30) and Tamim (25) physically assaulted and then tried to kill a Hindu man Koel Krishna Gain (কোয়েল কৃষ্ণ গাইন) by Stabbing because victim refused to pay 'jizya' tax (জিজিয়া কর) in Kazirabad (কাজিরাবাদ) village, Betagi, Barguna
Date of Incident:
March 6, 2025
Division: Barishal
District: Barguna
Upazilla: Betagi
Specific Location: Kazirabad village, Ward No. 2 of Kazirabad union
Details
Muslims physically assaulted and then tried to kill a Hindu man Koel Krishna Gain (কোয়েল কৃষ্ণ গাইন) by Stabbing because victim refused to pay 'Jizya' tax (জিজিয়া কর - https://bn.wikipedia.org/wiki/জিজিয়া_কর) in Kazirabad (কাজিরাবাদ) village, Betagi, Barguna.

On Friday, March 7, 2025 around 8 pm, Abu Saleh (30), son of Abdul Karim, and Tamim (25), son of Abdul Hamed of the same village tried to kill Koel Krishna Gain (30), son of Naren Chandra Gain of Kazirabad village, Ward No. 2 of Kazirabad union, Betagi upazila, Barguna district by stabbing in front of Sameer Sikder's house.

Abu Saleh and Tamim first asked Koel Krishna Gain for money (jizya tax). When the victim asked why they'd have to pay the money (jizya tax), the criminal replied that they'd have to pay the tax to live in that neighborhood. The perpetrators got angry and punched Koel when he did not pay the jizya tax. At one point, they tried to hack him to death by stabbinb.

Village resident Sameer Chandra Sikadar, who is more than 60 years old, said that the Hindus of the village are suffering from insecurity due to threats and persecution.
