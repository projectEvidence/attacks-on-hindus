46. Premier University VC Dr. Anupam Sen Resigns Amid Protests by Muslim Students, Allegations of Religious Bias Surface!
Date of Incident:
December 6, 2024
Division: Chattogram
District: Chattogram
Police Station: Pachlaish
Details
Amid a protest by a group of Muslim students, Dr. Anupam Sen, the Vice-Chancellor of the private Premier University in Chittagong and a renowned sociologist, has resigned. On Friday night, he submitted his resignation letter to the university’s Chancellor, the President of Bangladesh. Since Wednesday, a group of students had been staging demonstrations demanding the resignation of the Vice-Chancellor, Pro-Vice-Chancellor, and Treasurer of the university.
Dr. Anupam was a student-friendly teacher. Many believe that he was treated this way because he belongs to the Hindu religion.
