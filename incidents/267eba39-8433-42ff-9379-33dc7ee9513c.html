36. Shuvo Roy was beaten by islamist mob and then handed over to Police in Gazipur for Alleged Derogatory Remarks About Prophet Muhammad
Date of Incident:
March 13, 2025
Division: Dhaka
District: Gazipur
Upazilla: Gazipur Sadar-Joydebpur
Specific Location: Zaruner Namapara Konabari(জরুনের নামাপাড়া কোনাবাড়ী)
Details
A young man named Shuvo Roy (23) was beaten by a mob for allegedly making derogatory remarks about Prophet Muhammad (PBUH). The incident took place in the Namapara area of Konabari Jarun in Gazipur city on Friday (March 14).

Shuvo Roy is the son of Dhiren Chandra Roy from the Magura Baishyapara area under Kishoreganj Police Station in Nilphamari district. He works at a local garment factory.

According to police and local sources, the young man made derogatory remarks about the Prophet (PBUH) on Friday. Locals then caught him, called 999, and handed him over to the police.

Local resident Md Zahirul Islam Zahir said the youth confessed to making the offensive remarks. He was then handed over to law enforcement.

Sub-Inspector (SI) Mohammad Rafiqul Islam of Konabari Police Station under Gazipur Metropolitan Police said the youth was brought to the station after receiving the report. Further action will be determined and announced later.
