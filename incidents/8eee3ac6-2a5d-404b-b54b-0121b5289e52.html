53. Saraswati Puja Mandap was vandalised by Md. Badsha and his companion, also attacked women and child in front of Daffodil, Ishwardi School Para, Pabna
Date of Incident:
February 2, 2025
Division: Rajshahi
District: Pabna
Upazilla: Ishwardi
Specific Location: Ward 02
Details
The students of the school and college of the Sanatan community of this village were celebrating Saraswati Puja and listening to music in the box, at that time the defendants 1. Md. Badsha (65), father-deceased Tofazzal Hossain, 2. Md. Soleman Mandal Fela (60), father-deceased Huzur Ali Mandal, 3. Md. Swapan (57), father-deceased Tofazzal Hossain, 4. Md. Shajahan Master (55), father-Addat, 5. Md. Suman (40), father-unknown, Sarvasang-Schoolpara Hajir Mor, Post Office-Ishwardi, Police Station-Ishwardi, District-Pavanya People abused us in foul and obscene language. When we forbade them from abusing the defendants, they got angry and pushed us, kicked us, punched us and caused painful injuries. They kicked the sound box in the Saraswati Puja Mandap in front of Daffodil, Ishwardi School Para, Pabna, lifted chairs and threw them at the women and threw the puja prasad. They threatened to kill us, injure us and showed various kinds of intimidation. Hearing our cries, witnesses from the surrounding area 1. Shri Ratan Kumar Roy (27), father-Shri Badal Roy, 2. Shri Ratan Kumar Das (37), father-deceased Ravi Das, 3. Shri Shuvo Ravi Das (22), father Shri Dilip Ravi Das, Sarva Sang School Para Hajir Mor, Post Office-Takhyani, Thana-Taishwardi, District-Pabna people and many others saw and know about the incident. After consulting with local dignitaries and relatives about the matter, there was a slight delay in coming to the house and filing a complaint.
