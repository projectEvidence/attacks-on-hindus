96. Arun Das a victim of land grabbing from Awami League leader Hasnat Abdullah, Hasnat grabbed a lot of land in the 16 years of the previous Awami League government by using influence and fear
Date of Incident:
September 25, 2024
Division: Barishal
District: Barishal
Upazilla: Gaurnadi
Specific Location: Dakhsin Palardi
Details
It is alleged that Hasanat Abdullah grabbed a lot of land in the 16 years of the previous Awami League government by using influence and fear, sometimes at a nominal price, sometimes by force. Abul Hasanat Abdullah is the President of Barisal District Awami League.

There are also allegations against Hasanat of grabbing land from minority communities. Arun Das (72), the owner of the traditional 'The Laxman Das Circus', alleged that Hasanat Abdullah had his eye on the land of their under-construction 'Leela Cinema Hall' in Dakshin Palradi Mouza, next to the Dhaka-Barisal highway. He put pressure on him to get the land deed. When he did not agree, Awami League leader Abul Hossain Mollah and local Selim Hawladar offered to sell the land, but he refused.

Later, Hasanat Abdullah used the administration to stop their circus show. This caused them financial losses. Since the circus was closed for a long time, it became difficult to bear the cost of food for the artists and animals. Then he was forced to deed 34 percent of the land, including the building under construction. Hasanat Abdullah gave only 3.6 million taka for the property worth about 1.5 crore taka. Arun Das lamented, "It didn't end here, Hasanat Abdullah again pressured me to write down the dighi on 96 percent of my land. But when I refused, Lutfar Rahman, the general secretary of Gournadi Upazila Chhatra League, threatened to kill me. After that, I stopped going to Gournadi out of fear for my life."
