51. Prof. Dr. Shubhamoy Dutta was forced to resign amidst protests by a faction of radical muslim students
Date of Incident:
December 20, 2024
Division: Dhaka
District: Dhaka
Police Station: Banani
Specific Location: Star Tower, 12 Kemal Ataturk Avenue
Details
Amid student protests, Acting Vice-Chancellor of the private Prime Asia University, Prof. Dr. Shubhamoy Dutta, has resigned and stepped down from all university activities. Many believe he is an honest and student-friendly teacher. However, the protesters have brought various allegations of corruption and irregularities against Dr. Dutta. They staged a demonstration by blocking Kamal Atatürk Avenue in Banani, Dhaka, to prevent him from entering the campus.
