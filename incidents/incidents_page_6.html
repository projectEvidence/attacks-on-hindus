76. Hindu Widow Arati Mondol’s Home in Madaripur Demolished and Seized by Force
Date of Incident:
December 1, 2024
Division: Dhaka
District: Madaripur
Upazilla: Shibchar
Police Station: Shivchar
Specific Location: Bablatala area
Details
On Monday, December 2, at around 11:00 AM, Raihan Molla and his hired goon Biplob attacked the home of poor and helpless widow Arati Mondol. During the attack, they vandalized the house—breaking doors and windows, cutting down trees, and even removing the water tap from the premises.

Arati Mondol, a 55-year-old widow, has been living on this land since her marriage to her late husband, Gouranga Mondol. With tears in her voice, she said, “Raihan Molla and his family had threatened us several times before. They tried to demolish the house and attack us earlier too. Even though the police had come and removed some of their encroachments, today they have forcefully taken over my home again.”
Arati Mondol’s son, Swapan Mondol, added, “This land belongs to us. Today a large group of people attacked our house, broke down our doors, and destroyed our trees. We are now living in great fear. The attackers could launch a bigger assault at any moment.”
Local residents believe that despite owning the land legally, the family is being subjected to such oppression simply because they belong to a minority community.

81. Around 15/20 people attacked, looted and vandalism the ISKCON 'Shri Sri Hare Krishna Namahatta Sangha' temple in Bhairab Ranibazar Haludpatti, Kishoreganj.
Date of Incident:
November 28, 2024
Division: Dhaka
District: Kishoreganj
Upazilla: Bhairab
Specific Location: Haludpatti, Ranibazar
Details
An attack, looting and vandalism took place at the ISKCON temple 'Shri Sri Hare Krishna Namahatta Sangha' in Bhairab Ranibazar Haludpatti, Kishoreganj. Police have arrested three leaders of the banned Chhatra League in this incident.

The brief statement of the case states that there is a temple called 'Shri Sri Hare Krishna Namahatta Sangha' in Ranibazar Haludpatti. The plaintiff is a member of the Sangha. Last Wednesday at around 8:30 am, some members including Pranay went to clean the temple. They returned after finishing their work at around 9:30 am. On Friday, around 5 pm, after hearing about the attack, they went to the temple and found that the door and lock were broken. When they went inside, they found that six pictures of the Lord, conch, achamanpatra, mridangam, sofa, chair, table, clock and Thakur's throne kept in the temple had been vandalized. Everything was lying on the ground. Bells, gongs, cymbals, and drums kept in a box at the temple were stolen. The complaint mentions a loss of 4 million taka.

Plaintiff Pranay Karmakar told Prothom Alo that ISKCON has many branch associations. Sri Sri Hare Krishna Namahatta Sangha is the propaganda branch of ISKCON. The Bhairav ​​Sangha was established in 2009. Temple activities are also conducted here. Regular prayers are held on Sundays. Bhairav ​​activities are being conducted from the ISKCON Narsingdi branch.

After investigation, it was learned that the activities of the Sangha are conducted in a one-story building of a businessman in Ranibazar Haludpatti. Prayers and kirtan are held in the Sangha every Sunday. Apart from that, the Sangha office is not open much on other days. It was closed yesterday as well. Around 5 pm, some people came there and started shouting slogans like 'ISKCON has no place in Bhairav'. Almost all of them had sticks in their hands. Later, they vandalized and left. Most of the attackers were young people. There were 15 to 20 people in number.

Division: Dhaka

Details
During the clash between Chinmoy followers and law enforcement, Assistant Public Prosecutor (APP) Saiful Islam Alif (35) of the Chittagong court was killed. At least 20 people were injured. Police lobbed tear gas, sound grenades and baton-charge to control the situation.
