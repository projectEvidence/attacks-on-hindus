77. Business owned by Barun Kumar Ghosh looted during broad daylight in Satkhira
Date of Incident: August 5, 2024
Division: Khulna
District: Satkhira
Upazilla: Kaliganj
Specific Location: Bharashimla Union
Details
On August 6, at around 10:30 am, more than a hundred armed people returning from the BNP procession on the south side of Kankshiali Bridge broke the shutters of “Ma Lakshmi Bhandar” and “Ma Lakshmi Muri Bhandar” and entered. First, they vandalized 10 CCTV cameras inside the mill. The attackers broke open the electric meter and took various items, including wires.
