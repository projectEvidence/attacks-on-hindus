11. Mob of Islamist extremists attacked Jitu Karmakar and his family with the motive to kill him for his activism to secure human rights for Hindus and minorities
Date of Incident:
April 10, 2025
Division: Chattogram
District: Chattogram
Upazilla: Sitakunda
Specific Location: কেদারখীল village of জাফরনগর-৪৩১০ ইউনিয়ন
Details
Jitu Karmakar is a member of Bangladesh United Sanatani Awakening Alliance (বাংলাদেশ সম্মিলিত সনাতনী জাগরণ জোট). He participated in the protests on August 09-10, 2024. He was vocal against atrocities on Hindus and joined Chinmoy Krishna Das Brahmachari and other Sanatanis who were demonstrating for the rights of Hindus. Since some days before the incident, he received death threats on Dacebook. Following this, a mob of Islamic extremists attacked him and his family on Friday, April 11, 2024 around 9 PM with the intention to kill him. The mob critically injured his family members, mainly Jitu Karmakar and his elder brother. Although he escaped the death narrowly, he along with his brother are admitted to hospital with life-threatening injuries.
