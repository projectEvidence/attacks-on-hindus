79. Idols Vandalized at Kali Temple in Sherpur Cremation Ground, Bogura by Anti-Hindu Extremist Group
Date of Incident:
December 17, 2024
Division: Rajshahi
District: Bogura
Upazilla: Sherpur
Specific Location: Union: Bhabanipur
Details
In Shikhar village of Bhabanipur Union, Bogura, idols at the cremation ground’s Kali temple were found vandalized. The cremation ground is established on nearly one acre of land. Indigenous communities from Shikhar village and neighboring villages such as Aminpur, Chandipur, Amboil, Gomorta, and Bhadra perform funeral rites there. Every year, Kali Puja and devotional kirtans are held at the site. After the puja, oil lamps are lit at the temple every evening. On Wednesday afternoon, some local residents discovered the Kali idol in a broken state. Three of its hands and several other parts were damaged. This incident has caused concern and unrest among the villagers. Locals and nearby residents believe that some anti-Hindu extremist groups are behind this act.
