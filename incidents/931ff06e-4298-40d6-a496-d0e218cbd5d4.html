51. Multiple harassment case have been filed after Hindus protested outside the Chittagong court premises against the arrest and imprisonment of Chinmoy Krishna Das Brahmachari
Date of Incident:
November 29, 2024
Division: Chattogram
District: Chattogram
Police Station: Kotwali, CMP
Details
A harassment case has been filed after Hindus protested outside the Chittagong court premises against the arrest and imprisonment of Chinmoy Krishna Das Brahmachari. The case names 116 people and names 500 others as anonymous defendants. A total of four cases have been filed, including three for assaulting police in the incident.
Involved Crimes
  • • Systematic Bias against Non-Muslims
  • • Suppression of Civil Rights/Free Speech
  • • Misuse of Power
