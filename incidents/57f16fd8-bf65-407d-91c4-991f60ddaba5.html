125. Md. Abu Kalam and his gang attacked and attempted to occupy the Durga Temple in Badarganj
Date of Incident:
September 14, 2024
Division: Rangpur
District: Rangpur
Upazilla: Badarganj
Details
Md. Abu Kalam and his gang attacked and attempted to occupy the Baryari Durga Temple in Badarganj Baluabhat, Rangpur district. In retaliation, local Hindus protested in front of the police station in the middle of the night.
