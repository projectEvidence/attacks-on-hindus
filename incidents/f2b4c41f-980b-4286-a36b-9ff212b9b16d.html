59. Muslims attack and injure peaceful Hindu protester demanding release of Chinmoy Krishna Das in Rangpur
Date of Incident:
November 26, 2024
Division: Rangpur
District: Rangpur
Upazilla: Rangpur Sadar
Specific Location: জাহাজ কোম্পানি মোড়
Details
Protests were held in Rangpur to protest the arrest of Chinmoy Brahmachari. At 8:30 pm, a protest procession started from "ধর্মসভা" near "জাহাজ কোম্পানি মোড়" area of the city. The procession went around the town hall and returned to "জাহাজ কোম্পানি মোড়" area where a clash occurred. Several people were injured. Two army vehicles and Metropolitan Police personnel arrived at the scene and calmed the situation. A protester named Jiban Kumar Roy said, "Our peaceful procession was attacked, injuring 4-5 people. We demand the unconditional release of Chinmoy Krishna Das Brahmachari and justice for the attack on us."
