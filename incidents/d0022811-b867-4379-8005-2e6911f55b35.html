75. Narayan Das from Meghna Upazilla of Comilla was arrested by Police without any investigation on alleged charge of hurting religious sentiments
Date of Incident:
December 2, 2024
Division: Chattogram
District: Comilla
Upazilla: Meghna
Specific Location: Govindapur Union
Details
Police have arrested a young man named Narayan Das (25) on charges of allegedly insulting the Quran in Joynagar village of Gobindapur union in Meghna upazila of Comilla. The police arrested Narayan Das without any investigation. He was arrested on Tuesday (December 3) afternoon. The accused Narayan Das is the son of Nowresh Das of the village.

When the news of the alleged insulting of the Quran spread, intense tension spread among the Muslim locals. Later, based on the complaint of the locals, the police led by SI Saimum conducted a quick drive and arrested him.

After the arrest of the accused, the locals demanded strict punishment for him. The locals said, “Exemplary punishment must be ensured for this crime, so that no one dares to commit such crimes in the future. Hurting religious sentiments will not be tolerated. We demand speedy and strict justice.”

Meghna Police Station Officer-in-Charge (OC) Abdul Jalil said, "The accused has been arrested. He is being interrogated. The Circle Sir is coming, we will discuss with him and take the next decision." He added, "Everyone should be very careful before posting any religious matter on social media. The administration will take strict steps to curb such crimes."
