130. Former Awami League parliamentarian Sadeq Khan from Dhaka-13 constituency built an empire by by occupying the land of the Hindu community
Date of Incident:
September 15, 2024
Division: Dhaka
District: Dhaka
Upazilla: Dhaka City
Specific Location: Mohammadpur
Details
Former MP from Dhaka-13 constituency Sadeq Khan has established a huge market and petrol pump by occupying the land of the Hindu community. At various times, by exerting influence and intimidation, he has occupied their land and constructed various establishments in Mohammadpur area including Agricultural Market, Sadeq Khan Chicken Market, Sadeq Khan Dried Market, Sadeq Khan Brick Market, Sadeq Khan Bali Market, Sadeq Khan Basti and petrol pump. He has established a kingdom in the area by freely occupying land. ACC sources have given this information.
Information about huge wealth in the name and anonymity of Sadeq Khan has been found in a secret investigation by the ACC's Intelligence Branch. He has built wealth in his own name and in the names of his family members and relatives through various irregularities and corruption. The legal source of these assets could not be found.

Sources said that the Anti-Corruption Commission (ACC) has decided to investigate allegations of abuse of power, irregularities, and amassing wealth beyond known sources of income through corruption against Sadeq Khan and another MP and the Vice-Chancellor of Bangabandhu Sheikh Mujibur Rahman Digital University in Gazipur. The commission took this decision yesterday, Sunday. A public investigation will begin against them soon.
Sadeq Khan became an Awami League MP from Dhaka-13 constituency in the 11th National Parliament election. He had been carrying out coercive activities including encroachment since before. After becoming an MP, he became reckless in grabbing land belonging to the Hindu community.
