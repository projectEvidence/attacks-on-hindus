146. Hindu girl Tama Chakraborty was abducted in broad daylight in Baliakandi of Rajbari District
Date of Incident:
September 8, 2024
Division: Dhaka
District: Rajbari
Upazilla: Baliakandi
Details
Baliakandi is an upazila under Rajbari district of Dhaka division. The abducted girl's home is in Jadavpur village of Madhukhali police station of Faridpur district. In a complaint filed with Baliakandi police station, the abducted Tama Chakraborty's father Parimal Chakraborty told the police, "My daughter Tama Chakraborty (18) studies at Jamalpur News College. Today, on 09/09/2024, at approximately 10.15 am, my daughter, on her way to Jamalpur Degree College, informed my wife through her mobile phone (01775-385945) from Jamalpur Bazar area that someone or something had followed my daughter for malicious purposes and later when I tried to contact her on the mobile phone, the mobile phone was switched off. Immediately, I along with my family members came to Jamalpur Bazar and searched for my daughter in the market and surroundings. But I could not find my daughter anywhere. In this situation, there is a risk of serious harm to my daughter.' He gave detailed information about his daughter in the charge sheet.
