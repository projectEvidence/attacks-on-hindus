76. Idols Vandalized at Radha-Govinda Temple in Haluaghat
Date of Incident:
December 18, 2024
Division: Mymensingh
District: Mymensingh
Upazilla: Haluaghat
Specific Location: Bonderpara Radha-Gobinda Temple
Details
Unidentified assailants vandalized idols at the Radha-Govinda Temple in Banderpara village of Shakuai Union, Haluaghat Upazila, late Thursday night. The damage was discovered by local Hindu community members on Friday morning as they passed by the temple and found several deities broken and desecrated.

Upon receiving the report, a team of law enforcement officials, including Superintendent of Police Md. Azizul Islam, Assistant Police Superintendent Sagar Sarkar (Haluaghat-Dhobaura Circle), and Officer-in-Charge of Haluaghat Police Station Abul Khair, visited the site and launched an investigation.

OC Abul Khair confirmed the incident and assured that a full investigation is underway to identify and bring the perpetrators to justice.

Political leaders and community activists have condemned the attack, calling for the protection of religious minorities and swift action against those responsible.
