64. Muslim students at Begum Rokeya University in Rangpur particaped in smear campaign against ISKCON after the murder of Assistant Public Prosecutor Saiful Islam Alif
Date of Incident:
November 26, 2024
Division: Rangpur
District: Rangpur
Upazilla: Rangpur Sadar
Specific Location: Begum Rokeya University
Details
After the murder of Assistant Public Prosecutor Saiful Islam Alif's murder, Islamic parties reinforced smear campaign against ISKCON demanding ban on ISKCON. Students at Begum Rokeya University (formerly Rangpur University) in Rangpur protested demanding a ban on ISKCON in the wake of the attack.

The date of the incident is either November 26 or November 27, 2024 (the news was published on Nov 27) Source: https://samakal.com/bangladesh/article/267302/চিন্ময়ের-গ্রেপ্তার-ঘিরে-সংঘাত-চট্টগ্রামে-আইনজীবী-নিহত
