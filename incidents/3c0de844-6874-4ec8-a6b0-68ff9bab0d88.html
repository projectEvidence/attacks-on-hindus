70. Vandalism and looting in attempt to occupy Hindu homes in Mohanganj, Netrokona
Date of Incident:
November 15, 2024
Division: Mymensingh
District: Netrokona
Upazilla: Mohanganj Upazilla
Specific Location: ​​Khurshimul, Maghan-Siyadar union
Details
There have been allegations of vandalism and looting by a Muslim family in Manarkandi area of ​​Khurshimul village in Maghan-Siyadar union of Mohanganj upazila of Netrokona in an attempt to occupy the house of a Hindu family.
The victim family filed a written complaint with the Netrokona Police Superintendent on Saturday (November 16) afternoon. On Sunday (November 17) morning, Rajan Ravidas, a member of the victim family, confirmed the truth of the incident and said, "Kukila Begum and her children have been torturing my family for a long time. Even after the learned court issued Section 145 in this regard, they attacked my family, vandalized, looted and took everything. I demand fair justice from the administration." According to a written complaint to the Superintendent of Police, a group of masked miscreants, including Kukila Begum, wife of deceased Akkas Mia of the same village, and her children Enamul Mia, Alimul Mia, Ashiq Mia, and others, attacked and looted the house of Babul Rabidas, son of Chowkidar Rajan Rabidas, son of deceased Ganesh Rabidas, in the Manarkandi area of ​​Khurshimul village in Maghan-Siyadar union of Mohanganj upazila of Netrokona district, on Friday afternoon.
