38. Advance bookings for Hindu, Buddhist, and non-Muslim weddings were forced to cancel on the occasion of a waz mahfil by pro-Jamaat Islamic speaker Mizanur Rahman Azhari. This affected families who had already printed wedding cards and sent out invitations.
Date of Incident:
January 31, 2025
Division: Chattogram
District: Chattogram
Police Station: Kotwali, CMP
Specific Location: parade ground in Chittagong
Details
It was noted in https://bddigest.com/news/16773/ (saved pdf: https://drive.google.com/file/d/1fzmxzAIltWTd9kSk4TMpoAMP3I2zPqpP/view?usp=drive_link) that in late January of 2025, all community centers around the parade ground in Chittagong were forced to cancel advance bookings for Hindu, Buddhist, and non-Muslim weddings on the occasion of a waz mahfil by pro-Jamaat Islamic speaker Mizanur Rahman Azhari. As a result, families who had already printed wedding cards and sent out invitations were affected.
The youtube video of Mizanur Rahman Azhari's Waz is available at https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=uCJorr5f-Ag (as of March 01, 2025) which was published by Cox Islamic Media (Youtube Channel: https://www.youtube.com/@CoxIslamicMedia Facebook Page: https://www.facebook.com/coxislamicmedia) Phone: 01796-669848
Exact date was not available. So the date of January 31 was used here because the youtube video of the waz was published on that date and the online news mentioned late January.
