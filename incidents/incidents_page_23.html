August 4, 2024
Division: Khulna

Details

The miscreants looted computers, laptops, ACs, routers and imported components from the shop and three warehouses.

But the looting could not be stopped. My father spent a lot of money fighting the national parliament and upazila elections against the boat symbol. We owe crores of taka to the bank. We are now unemployed. We are sitting at home. I have become destitute and have sat on the streets."

339. Hindu households at Andharkota and Bollamukh village were attacked and looted by Islamist mob
Date of Incident:
August 4, 2024
Division: Khulna
District: Jashore
Upazilla: Bagherpara
Specific Location: Andharkota village, Bollamukh village
Details
Several Hindu houses were attacked and looted at Andharkota village. Most of them were Hindu farmers or day labourer.
Golak Chandra Biswas, one of the many victims, claimed that the assailants had vandalized the family home that day. They also looted gold, 50,000 taka in cash, and a music box. They then took out a motorcycle and a bicycle that were kept on the balcony of the house and placed them on one side of the yard. They then took out blankets, pillows, and clothes from the house, placed them on the motorcycle and bicycle, and set them on fire.
