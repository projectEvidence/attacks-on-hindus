334. A group of people with covered face, robbed half a dozen Hindu house in Shalborat
Date of Incident:
August 4, 2024
Division: Khulna
District: Jashore
Upazilla: Bagherpara
Specific Location: Shalborat
Details
On the night of August 5, 2024, a group of 10 to 12 people attacked the Hindu neighborhood of Shalborat village in the upazila. 65 Hindu families live in this neighborhood. They went to five to six houses with black cloths tied around their faces and ramadas in their hands. The people of the neighborhood said that out of fear, everyone in the neighborhood somehow spent the night in the neighboring garden. On Tuesday morning, the men left the neighborhood and went elsewhere.

341. Miscreants looted all the belongings of Lakshmi Rani Pal of Bejpara, Jessore city
Date of Incident:
August 4, 2024
Division: Khulna
District: Jashore
Upazilla: Jessore Sadar
Specific Location: Banani Road, Bejpara
Details
Lakshmi Rani Pal lives with her parents on the ground floor of Paresh Basu's house on Banani Road in Bejpara, Jessore town. Her only source of income is a sewing machine. After Sheikh Hasina's resignation, miscreants broke into the main gate of the house at around 9:15 pm last Monday and looted Lakshmi Rani's sewing machine, cooking stove, gas cylinder and 10 baht of gold jewelry belonging to her brother's newly married wife kept in the cupboard. Lakshmi Rani's family members have been leaving the area fearing for their lives. They have taken shelter in a relative's house in another area.
