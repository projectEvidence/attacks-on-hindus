85. Assistant Professor of Chittagong University, Rontu Das was forced to resign
Date of Incident:
October 22, 2024
Division: Chattogram
District: Chattogram
Upazilla: Hathazari
Details
Rontu Das, an assistant professor of the history department of Chittagong University, has resigned under pressure from students. The incident took place in the room of the chairman of the history department of the university on Wednesday. At that time, Rontu Das was standing behind some students protesting under the banner of general students demanding his resignation. The students later handed over the resignation letter written by him to the chairman of the department.
He was allegedly involved in Awami League politics.
