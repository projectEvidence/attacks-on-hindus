99. Miscreants vandalized Durga idol in Bakerganj, Barisal
Date of Incident:
October 3, 2024
Division: Barishal
District: Barishal
Upazilla: Bakerganj
Specific Location: Deuribari, Paschim Shyampur
Details
Miscreants vandalized a Durga idol in Bakerganj, Barisal. The incident took place at the Deuribari Public Durga Temple in West Shyampur, Barisal, around 3 am on Friday (October 4).

Although the temple has been celebrating puja for 11 years, there has never been a communal attack. However, this year, miscreants broke the throats of three idols in their temple.
