100. On the night of October 1, idol was vandalized at the Palpara temple in Manikdi, Sujanagar
Date of Incident:
September 30, 2024
Division: Rajshahi
District: Pabna
Upazilla: Sujanagar
Specific Location: Palpara, Manikdi
Details
Five idols, including a Durga idol, were vandalized at the Manikadi Palpara Barwari Puja Mandap in the Sujanagar Municipality area.

Bijan Pal, president of the Manikdi Palpara Barwari Puja Mandap, told Prothom Alo that the work of making the idols in the temple for the occasion of Durga Puja is nearing completion. Now only the painting work is left. On Tuesday night, while leaving the temple, they saw the idols intact. Then, when they came in the morning, they found the heads of five idols, including the Durga idol, broken.
