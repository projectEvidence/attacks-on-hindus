205. Muslim mob vandalizes Hindu residence while hurling communal slurs
Date of Incident:
August 4, 2024
Division: Dhaka
District: Dhaka
Upazilla: Dhamrai
Details
In Dhamrai, Dhaka district, along with the attack on a Hindu residence, an attempt was made to vandalise the Shri Maa Durga Temple. On August 5, 2024, around 6 pm, a mob of 100-150 people strong came and vandalised the house hurling communal abuses.
