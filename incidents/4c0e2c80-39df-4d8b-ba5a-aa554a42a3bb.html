71. Muslim family encroached on the land of the 200 years old temple in of Manoharganj Upazila of Comilla, police remained silent
Date of Incident:
November 23, 2024
Division: Chattogram
District: Comilla
Upazilla: Monohorgonj
Specific Location: Boro kachi Bosu Bari, (বড় কাঁচি বসু বাড়ি )Ward No. 5, Bipulasar Union No. 11
Details
A local Muslim family has encroached on the land of the 200 years old Sri Sri Harikamal Seva Ashram and temple in Bipulasar Union No. 11 of Manoharganj Upazila of Comilla and built a road. The minority Hindu community has not received any redress after complaining to the police station or the administration in this regard.
