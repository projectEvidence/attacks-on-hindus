61. After extremist Muslims killed Assistant Public Prosecutor (APP) Saiful Islam Alif, Islamic Parties calumniated ISKCON members accusing them of this killing in order to agitate people against ISKCON and Hindus
Date of Incident:
November 27, 2024
Division: Dhaka
District: Dhaka
Upazilla: Dhaka City
Specific Location: Statement sent to the media by Hefazat-e-Islam Ameer and Secretary General
Details
During the clash between Chinmoy followers and law enforcement, Assistant Public Prosecutor (APP) Saiful Islam Alif (35) of the Chittagong court was killed. At least 20 people were injured. Police lobbed tear gas, sound grenades and baton-charge to control the situation.

Jamaat-e-Islami Ameer Dr. Shafiqur Rahman condemned and protested the killing. In a statement, he called Alif a "dear party colleague" and demanded legal action against those involved in the killing. In another statement, Hefazat-e-Islam Ameer Muhibullah Babunagari and Secretary General Sajedur Rahman demanded the immediate arrest of ISKCON activists and supporters involved in the killing and exemplary punishment.

From https://www.greenbanglaonline24.com/en/dont-fall-into-any-trap-of-india:
Hefazat-e-Islam Ameer Allama Muhibullah Babunagari and Secretary General Maulana Sajedur Rahman made this demand in a statement sent to the media on Wednesday.

In the statement, they said, ‘Focusing on the arrest of anti-national traitor Chinmoy Krishna Das, the activists and supporters of the extremist Hindutva ISKCON launched a terrorist attack on the Chittagong court premises and brutally hacked to death the state’s lawyer Saiful Islam Alif in broad daylight. We believe that this conspiratorial anarchy has been created with the instigation and support of India. The government must ensure justice for the murder of Shaheed Alif by quickly arresting the ISKCON activists and supporters involved in this conspiracy. The Hindutva ISKCON must be banned immediately for its terrorist activities.’

They also said, ‘The Hindutva ruling class of India, which is bloodthirsty against Muslims, wants to maintain its hegemony in this country by playing the ‘Hindu card’. India has gone mad since the fall of their servant, the fascist Hasina. That is why the country is carrying out one conspiracy after another.’

65. Police and BGB attacked Chinmoy's followers peacefully staying on the ground floor of the court building
Date of Incident:
November 26, 2024
Division: Chattogram
District: Chattogram
Police Station: Kotwali, CMP
Details
Chinmoy Krishna's lawyer Swarup Kanti Nath said, "After the magistrate court rejected bail, we filed a case and applied for bail in the Metropolitan Sessions Judge's Court." The court has put the application for hearing on Wednesday (today). Chinmoy's followers had been peacefully staying on the ground floor of the court building since morning. In the afternoon, the police and BGB attacked ordinary Sanatanis without provocation. Such an attack under the court building is unprecedented.

It was seen on the spot that tension was seen when the police tried to remove Chinmoy's followers around 2 pm. Then, an old mini truck and a microbus from Malkhana were brought to the ground floor of the court premises and a blockade was created in front of the prison van. Many of the blockaders were then seen lying on the ground. As tension increased in the court premises, Metropolitan Sessions Judge Md. Saiful Islam's chamber, Metropolitan PP Mofizul Haque Bhuiyan and General Secretary of the Chittagong Bar Association Ashraf Hossain Chowdhury Razzaj were seen entering. At that time, Chinmoy's lawyer was waiting outside the Metropolitan Sessions Judge's court to hear the case. At that time, several hundred police, SWAT and BGB members stationed on the ground floor of the court building blew whistles simultaneously and first exploded sound grenades. After that, they dispersed the blockaders by beating them with batons. Everyone dispersed after 15 to 20 minutes of action by the three forces.

Six Chinmoy followers were admitted to the hospital after being injured in the police baton-charge. They are Srivas Das, Sharku Das, Choton, Sujit Ghosh, Utpal and Enamul Haque.
