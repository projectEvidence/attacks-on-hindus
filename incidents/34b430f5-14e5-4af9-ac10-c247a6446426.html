311. Rajfulbaria Bazar Temple Committee President Monar's grocery shop was looted and the house was attacked
Date of Incident:
August 4, 2024
Division: Dhaka
District: Dhaka
Upazilla: Savar
Details
Savar Upazila Rajfulbaria Bazar Temple Committee President Monar's grocery shop was looted and the house was attacked by a group under the orders of Tentuljhora Union Ward 2 BNP President Qutub Ali (member). The group was led by his son Sabbir and nephew Manju. Another group under the leadership of Tentuljhora Union Sramik Dal (President) Md. Ayub Ali Kuthi and his son AL-Amin joined the attack.
