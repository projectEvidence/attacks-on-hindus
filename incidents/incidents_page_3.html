Upazilla: Kalapara
Description: The residence of Awami League Joint General Secretary Ananta Mukherjee and a temple in the nearby area of ​​Kuakata Municipality in Patuakhali were attacked. Nothing in my house was saved. Then the attackers attacked the temple next to my house. The doors and windows of the temple were damaged."
