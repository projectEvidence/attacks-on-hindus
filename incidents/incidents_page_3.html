35. Jihadi Muslims in Bangladesh destroy ISKCON’s Govinda's Kitchen and halt its services. The attack on ISKCON's restaurant was allegedly because it was seen as a centre for spreading Hindu ideology.
Date of Incident:
January 16, 2025
Division: Dhaka
District: Dhaka
Upazilla: Dhaka City
Details
Amid country wide forceful and unlawful religious policing by Islamist fundamentalist to serve beef in every hotel, a mob targeted a Hindu hotel in Malibagh, Dhaka. The name of the hotel is "Govindas Kitchen". The hotel serves vegetarian food.

No mainstream media reported the incident.

Quoted from the source https://sanatanprabhat.org/english/125243.html:

1. According to eyewitnesses, a post circulated on social media led to the attack on the restaurant’s staff, manager and owner by jihadi Muslims. Several people were injured.

2. The attack on the restaurant was mainly due to a provocative post spread by a Facebook account named ‘Paradoxical Sajid’, which was about beef. The post called for a boycott of vegetarian restaurants run by ‘ISKCON’, claiming that these restaurants are centres for spreading Hindutva ideology.

3. The post called on people to ‘not rent out property to ISKCON to run restaurants’, and also alleged that ISKCON restaurants mix cow urine and cow dung in their recipes. The restaurant was attacked because of this post.

4. ‘Govinda’s Kitchen’ was a popular restaurant, known for its affordable vegetarian food. The customers here were not only Hindus, but also Muslims from low-income groups.
False propaganda that Hindu restaurants are hide-outs for Hindu terrorists !

It is alleged that various provocative posts were spread to create an atmosphere for attacking Govinda’s Hotel. It says that ‘conspiracies are being hatched through Hindu restaurants in Bangladesh. Hindu terrorists have set up hideouts in these restaurants and these restaurants are working as branches of Indian intelligence agencies’.
‘Rajdhani Hotel’ of a pro-Hindu Muslim also attacked

In the same way that ‘Govinda’s Kitchen’ was attacked, the ‘Rajdhani Hotel’ in Dhaka was also attacked. This restaurant is owned by a Muslim and was sympathetic towards Hindus and this restaurant was attacked because it did not serve beef.

43. Around 15/20 people attacked, looted and vandalism the ISKCON 'Shri Sri Hare Krishna Namahatta Sangha' temple in Bhairab Ranibazar Haludpatti, Kishoreganj.
Date of Incident:
November 28, 2024
Division: Dhaka
District: Kishoreganj
Upazilla: Bhairab
Specific Location: Haludpatti, Ranibazar
Details
An attack, looting and vandalism took place at the ISKCON temple 'Shri Sri Hare Krishna Namahatta Sangha' in Bhairab Ranibazar Haludpatti, Kishoreganj. Police have arrested three leaders of the banned Chhatra League in this incident.

The brief statement of the case states that there is a temple called 'Shri Sri Hare Krishna Namahatta Sangha' in Ranibazar Haludpatti. The plaintiff is a member of the Sangha. Last Wednesday at around 8:30 am, some members including Pranay went to clean the temple. They returned after finishing their work at around 9:30 am. On Friday, around 5 pm, after hearing about the attack, they went to the temple and found that the door and lock were broken. When they went inside, they found that six pictures of the Lord, conch, achamanpatra, mridangam, sofa, chair, table, clock and Thakur's throne kept in the temple had been vandalized. Everything was lying on the ground. Bells, gongs, cymbals, and drums kept in a box at the temple were stolen. The complaint mentions a loss of 4 million taka.

Plaintiff Pranay Karmakar told Prothom Alo that ISKCON has many branch associations. Sri Sri Hare Krishna Namahatta Sangha is the propaganda branch of ISKCON. The Bhairav ​​Sangha was established in 2009. Temple activities are also conducted here. Regular prayers are held on Sundays. Bhairav ​​activities are being conducted from the ISKCON Narsingdi branch.

After investigation, it was learned that the activities of the Sangha are conducted in a one-story building of a businessman in Ranibazar Haludpatti. Prayers and kirtan are held in the Sangha every Sunday. Apart from that, the Sangha office is not open much on other days. It was closed yesterday as well. Around 5 pm, some people came there and started shouting slogans like 'ISKCON has no place in Bhairav'. Almost all of them had sticks in their hands. Later, they vandalized and left. Most of the attackers were young people. There were 15 to 20 people in number.
