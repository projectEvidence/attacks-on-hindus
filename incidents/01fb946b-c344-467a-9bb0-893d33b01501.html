46. Hefazat-e-Islam Islamists vandalized Temple and Murti of Kali Mata in Dilalpur area of Pabna Sadar on the night of 2025/02/21
Date of Incident:
February 20, 2025
Division: Rajshahi
District: Pabna
Upazilla: Pabna Sadar
Specific Location: Dilalpur
Details
On the night of 21/02/2025, Islamists attacked a Kali Mata temple (শ্রী শ্রী রক্ষা কালি মাতার মন্দির) in #Dilalpur area of Pabna Sadar. The Murti of Kali Mata has been vandalised. Locals alleged that members of the radical organisation ‘Hefazat-e-Islam’ are behind this attack.
