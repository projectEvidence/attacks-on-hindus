83. Sampa Rani Das commited suicide after being harassed by Chhatra Dal leader Hridoy Raihan,Bauphal, Patuakhal
Date of Incident:
February 3, 2025
Division: Barishal
District: Patuakhali
Upazilla: Bauphal
Details
A first-year undergraduate student named Sampa Rani alias Iti Das (19) was harassed by a group of miscreants led by a Chhatra Dal leader while having dinner at a hotel with her friend in Bauphal, Patuakhali. It is alleged that the student committed suicide by hanging herself from the ceiling of her house. She was found hanging from Iti Das's house in Daspara village around 9 pm on Monday and taken to the Upazila Health Complex, where the doctor on duty declared her dead. The police recovered the body and sent it to the Patuakhali Medical College Hospital morgue for an autopsy on Tuesday morning. A case of accidental death has been registered at the police station in this regard. The young woman is the daughter of Sameer Das alias Niranjan Das, a resident of Daspara village. She was a first-year undergraduate student of Barisal Government Hatem Ali College. The name of the harassing Chhatra Dal leader is Hridoy Raihan. The general secretary of Ward 4 of Baufal Municipality Chhatra Dal. He is known to be very close to the member secretary of Baufal Municipality Chhatra Dal, Sakibuzzaman Rakib. Sakibuzzaman Rakib claims that he (Hridoy) was expelled from the party a few days ago. According to Eti's friends, relatives and eyewitnesses, Eti had gone out for a walk on the occasion of Saraswati Puja on Monday. At around 12 noon, Eti went to a restaurant on the eastern side of the public field of the upazila headquarters with a friend. At that time, Hridoy Raihan harassed them in various ways. At one stage, Hridoy was taking the friend who was with the young woman outside and some miscreants who were with her insulted the young woman's friend in various ways. And when the young woman resisted, the miscreants also fought with her.When one of the young woman's friends called 999 to inform him of the matter, Assistant Sub-Inspector (ASI) of Baufal Police Station Md. Shahin came and took the girl's friend to the police station on a motorcycle. Eti also went to the police station. Both of them were released around 4 pm. Upon reaching home, the girl went to the second floor of the house. After that, no one in the family saw her again. When her mother called her for dinner around 9 pm, she saw her hanging and screamed. Then people came and took her to the Upazila Health Complex and the doctor on duty declared her dead. A relative of the girl's family said that while hanging out with her friend, she could not bear it and chose suicide. He also said that the scoundrels involved in the incident are afraid to seek justice because they are under the umbrella of influential people. Their entire family is in panic. In this regard, Hridoy Raihan said that he did not harass her. However, when he saw them (the girl and her friend) in an objectionable state, he informed their family and asked them to take them away. When the family did not come and send the police, the matter came to light and the girl went home crying. A person who did not want to be named said that the miscreants were standing in front of the police station when the girl was leaving. The girl was saying to them, "You did not let me live. "Two eyewitnesses said that the girl and her friend were sitting in a restaurant and talking normally. The objectionable matter is baseless. Assistant Sub-Inspector (ASI) of Bauphal Police Station Md. Shahin said that Hridoy is the mastermind of this incident. On Monday, a boy came to the police station and said that their motorcycle was stopped. He went to the spot. Then Hridoy told him that it was their internal matter. He did not know about the matter related to the girl there. He asked to file a complaint with the police station in this regard; but they did not agree to file a complaint. Officer-in-Charge (OC) of Bauphal Police Station Md. Kamal Hossain said that a case of accidental death has been filed in this regard. The body of the girl was recovered and handed over to the family after autopsy at the Patuakhali Medical College Hospital morgue. Necessary action will be taken in this regard.
