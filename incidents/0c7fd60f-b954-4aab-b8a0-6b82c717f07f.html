2. Fajr Ali, a local muslim breaks into the house and rapes a Hindu married woman in Muradnagar, Comilla.
Date of Incident:
June 25, 2025
Division: Chattogram
District: Comilla
Upazilla: Muradnagar
Specific Location: Pachkitta (পাঁচকিত্তা), Ramchandrapur Dakshin Union (রামচন্দ্রপুর দক্ষিণ ইউনিয়নে)
Details
A 25-year-old Hindu married woman was raped by a local Muslim politician Fajr Ali in Muradnagar, Comilla. He breaks into the house and raped the helpless woman. She filed a case with Muradnagar Police Station.

She stated that about 15 days ago, she came to her father's house from her husband's home with her children. On Thursday, around 10 p.m., a man named Fajr Ali (38)came to her father's house and asked her to open the door. She refused. At one point, the man broke down the door and raped her.

A neighbour said, "There was a lot of noise in the house on Thursday night. I ran out in fear and called others. When people arrived, they found the door broken. Later, we rescued the victim."

A local resident, who asked not to be named, said that during the incident Fajr Ali was caught and beaten by locals. He was later taken to a hospital in Comilla city.


Muradnagar Police Station Officer-in-Charge Zahidur Rahman told Prothom Alo that a drive is underway to arrest the accused. He expressed hope that the arrest would be made soon. Anik, Suman, Ramzan and Babu from the same village were arrested on charges of spreading the video of rape.
