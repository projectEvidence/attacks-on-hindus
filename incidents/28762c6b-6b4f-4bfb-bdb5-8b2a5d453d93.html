55. Eleven year old Hindu girl Purnima Reli's mutilated dead body with severed neck and chopped wrist found in a tea garden the day after she went missing
Date of Incident:
February 5, 2025
Division: Sylhet
District: Moulvibazar
Upazilla: Kamalganj
Specific Location: Shamshernagar Tea Garden
Details
Quoted from https://www.opindia.com/news-updates/hindu-girl-purnima-reli-murdered-body-mutilated-kamalganj-moulvibazar-sylhet-bangladesh-details :

The mutilated body of an 11-year-old Hindu girl was found in the Shamshernagar Union in Kamalganj upazila in the Moulvibazar district of Bangladesh. The matter came to light on Thursday (6th February).

According to reports, the victim was identified as Purnima Reli. She was a student of Class IV. The Hindu girl had been missing since Wednesday (5th February). She had reportedly gone out to bring the cattle from the field.

Purnima was covered in blood. Her neck was severed and her wrist was chopped off. The mutilated condition of her body shocked the locals, who have now demanded the immediate arrest of those involved in the brutal murder.
