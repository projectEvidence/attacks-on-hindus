299. Many Hindus, including Uttam Kumar Ghosh, were attacked in Kalaroa upazila of Satkhira
Date of Incident:
August 4, 2024
Division: Khulna
District: Satkhira
Upazilla: Kalaroa
Specific Location: Tulsidanga
Details
Many Hindus, including Uttam Kumar Ghosh, were attacked in Kalaroa and Shyamnagar upazilas of Satkhira.
Uttam Kumar Ghosh of Tulsidanga village of Kalaroa said, "I know the people who attacked my shop. But I can't name them. They don't have any party."
