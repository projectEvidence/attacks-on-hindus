7. Islamists vandalized Durga Mandir in Maslandpur, Pirganj, Thakurgaon
Date of Incident:
March 22, 2025
Division: Rangpur
District: Thakurgaon
Upazilla: Pirganj
Specific Location: মছলন্দপুর মাশেনাতলী
Details
On the night of March 22nd, Islamists vandalized the idols at Shree Shree Durga Mandap in Maslandpur, Pirganj. The idols of Kartik and Lakshmi were broken and thrown on the ground, and the nose of the Ganesh idol was damaged.
