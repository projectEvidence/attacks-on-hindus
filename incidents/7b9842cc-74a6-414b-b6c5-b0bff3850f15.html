129. Sri Sri Jut Madan Gopal Jiu Vigraha Temple in Rishikesh Das Lane in the Sutrapur area of ​​Dhaka was vandalized and looted
Date of Incident:
September 11, 2024
Division: Dhaka
District: Dhaka
Upazilla: Dhaka City
Police Station: Sutrapur
Specific Location: Rishikesh Das Lane
Details
The idol of the Sri Sri Yut Madan Gopal Jiu Vigraha Temple in Rishikesh Das Lane in the Sutrapur area of ​​Dhaka was vandalized and the gold ornaments of the idol were looted. They beat up the two daughters of the current sevayat of the temple, Kartik Roy, and his wife for obstructing them. On the afternoon of September 12, 2024, 20-25 people armed with sticks and sharp weapons broke into the temple and attacked it.
