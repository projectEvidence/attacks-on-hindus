35. Hefazat-e-Islam Chandina, Comilla Branch brought out a Procession on February 07, 2025 Threatening to Slaughter ISKCON Members and Demolish ISKCON places
Date of Incident:
February 7, 2025
Division: Chattogram
District: Comilla
Upazilla: Chandina
Police Station: Chandina
Details
On Friday, Feb 7, 2025 after Jumu'ah prayer, extremists under the leadership of the secretary of the Chandina Pourashava Branch of Hefazat-e-Islam brought out a procession today after Jumu'ah prayer to stop the anniversary celebration of ISKCON and their temple's foundation preparation work in Chandina, Comilla.

The terrorists shouted slogans calling for the killing of Hindus and ISKCON members. They shouted: "Grab ISKCON members one by one and slaughter". They shouted to demolish ISKCON's places.

As shown in the video, the secretary of Hefazat-e-Islam Chandina Pourashava Branch thanked the local adminstration and police station OC to let them hold this hate speech procession. The video shows that the secretary delivered his hate speech at the gate of the police station. He also thanked Hazrat Maulana Sadek Mahmud Binnuri (spelling of name transcribed from his speech), the Grand Mufti of Ulama-e-Deoband oikya parishad "Motua" (spelling review needed for "Motua") board and Senior Vice President of Hefazat-e-Islam, Chandina Pourashava Branch.

Note:
- Bengali word "Pourashava" means City Corporation.
