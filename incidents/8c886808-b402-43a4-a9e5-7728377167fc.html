67. Chinmoy Krishna Das, spokesperson of "সনাতনী জাগরণ জোট" was arrested on false charges
Date of Incident:
November 25, 2024
Division: Dhaka
District: Dhaka
Upazilla: Dhaka City
Specific Location: Airport
Details
Bangladesh Sammilit Sanatani Jagran Jote spokesperson Chinmoy Krishna Das Brahmachari has been arrested by the Dhaka Metropolitan Police's Intelligence Department (DB). He was arrested from the capital's airport area on Monday. He was preparing to fly from Dhaka to Chittagong. At that time, a DB team brought him to the DB office on Minto Road in the capital.

In a press release sent to the media yesterday, leaders of "হিন্দু বৌদ্ধ খ্রিষ্টান ঐক্য পরিষদ" (Bangladesh Hindu Buddhist Christian Unity Council) said that there was a risk that this arrest would tarnish international image of Bangladesh about freedom of expression and human rights issues. The leaders demanded the immediate release of Chinmoy Krishna Das Brahmachari.
