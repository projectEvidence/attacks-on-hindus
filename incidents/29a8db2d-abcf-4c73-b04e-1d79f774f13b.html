155. Various parts of several idols were vandalized on the occasion of Durga Puja at the house of Ramcharan Dey in Changirgaon
Date of Incident:
September 19, 2024
Division: Chattogram
District: Lakshmipur
Upazilla: Ramganj
Specific Location: Changirgaon, Chandipur Union
Details
Various parts of several idols in a temple on the occasion of Durga Puja at the house of Ramcharan Dey in Changirgaon, Chandipur Union, Ramganj Upazila, Lakshmipur have been vandalized.

On Friday (September 20), during the Friday prayer sermon at Baitul Aman Jame Mosque in South Changirgaon of the upazila, 7 people including Munna (13), Rifat (11), Zahid (10), Minhaj (7), Parabi (8), Salman (8) vandalized the head of Ganesh, both hands of Lakshmi and the left hand of Durga. These radicals vandalised the idols to earn mercy from Allah.

At that time, 4 people were arrested and handed over to the police by the locals.
