50. Minority houses vandalized and looted in Pubail
Date of Incident:
October 25, 2024
Division: Dhaka
District: Gazipur
Upazilla: Gazipur Sadar-Joydebpur
Details
An incident of attack and vandalism occurred at the homes of minorities in Pubail, Gazipur city. In this regard, on Saturday, October 26, the victim, Nirmal Mallick, filed a written complaint with Pubail Police Station against Uttam Kumar Mallick, Shahjalal, Sanjay Sikder, Shinikda Rani Mallick, Deepali Rani Mallick and 15-20 unidentified people.
