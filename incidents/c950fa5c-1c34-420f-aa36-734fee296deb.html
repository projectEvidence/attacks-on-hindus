55. Hindu minor (14) girl was gang raped by 10-15 local Muslim youth.
Date of Incident:
February 26, 2025
Division: Rangpur
District: Nilphamari
Upazilla: Dimla
Details
Hindu minor (14) girl was abducted and gang raped by 10-15 local Muslim youth. The victim is a resident of Balapara village of Dimla Upazilla of Nilphamari district. On the night of February 27, 2025, the criminals forcibly took her to a cornfield and gang-raped her.

However, only Abdul Kuddus and Saminur Rahman were arrested by police. Others are still roaming freely.
