124. Anik Chakma (17) was beaten to death by Muslim Bengali settlers within 200 yards of the DC office
Date of Incident:
September 20, 2024
Division: Chattogram
District: Rangamati
Upazilla: Rangamati Sadar
Details
On September 20, clashes broke out between tribals and Muslim settler Bengalis in Rangamati. In this, Anik Chakma (17) was beaten to death while sitting in a tea shop within 200 yards of the DC office. Although the killers' clear faces and identities were confirmed in the video footage, they have not been arrested yet. Vandalism was carried out in Banarupa Maitree Vihar during the incident. The office of the Chittagong Hill Tracts Regional Council was burnt down. No one has been charged or arrested in these incidents as of the publishing date of https://drive.google.com/file/d/1WATrOCOBYteGnKWZgBM548VAVJCwBe_3/view?usp=drive_link.
