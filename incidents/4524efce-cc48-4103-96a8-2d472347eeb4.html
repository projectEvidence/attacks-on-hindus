25. Hindu Youth lynched by Islamist mob in Chittagong
Date of Incident:
December 31, 2024
Division: Chattogram
District: Chattogram
Police Station: Patenga
Specific Location: Kathgar, Patenga
Details
A young Hindu man called Pranto Talukder was forcefully abducted by a radical islamist mob and lynched. He was brutally beaten.
The whole incident was captured in CCTV footage.
This incident happened in Kathgar, Patenga, Chittagong on January 01, 2025.
Later he was admitted to chittagong medical college.
The islamist mob claimed that Pranto Talukder wrote something blasphemous on facebook. However, nobody could corroborate such claim.
Such incidents is increasing day by day in Bangladesh. Police have neither filed any case nor arrested anyone.
