315. House and business establishments of Dilip Singh looted, arsoned and vandalised
Date of Incident:
August 4, 2024
Division: Khulna
District: Jashore
Upazilla: Manirampur
Specific Location: Amrojhuta
Details
A group of people attacked the house of Dilip Singh in Amrajhuta village of Monirampur upazila on Monday night. At that time, they poured petrol on Dilip Singh's two-storey house and set it on fire. Earlier on Monday evening, a group of people attacked two business establishments of Dilip Singh in Khatuadanga Bazar of the upazila, vandalized them and took away the goods in the shops.
