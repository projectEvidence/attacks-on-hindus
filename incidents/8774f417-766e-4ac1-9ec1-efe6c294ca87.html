168. In Handyal Bhadra Kali temple of Chatmohar upazila of Pabna, 3 idols including Mahadev idol were vandalized on Monday (September 9) sometime during the night
Date of Incident:
September 8, 2024
Division: Rajshahi
District: Pabna
Upazilla: Chatmohar
Specific Location: Handial Paschimpara, Handial Union Parishad
Details
Three idols, including a Mahadev idol, have been vandalized at the Handial Bhadra Kali Temple in Chatmohar Upazila of Pabna. On Monday (September 9), miscreants vandalized three idols of the temple sometime during the night, said Kaliswar Das, general secretary of the temple committee.

He said that on the occasion of the upcoming Durga Puja, work was underway to make a Durga idol in the courtyard of the Bhadra Kali Temple in Handial Paschimpara, adjacent to Handial Union Parishad, until around 11 pm on Monday. After that, everyone left. After that, the miscreants tried to break the iron grill and lock but failed and vandalized the idol from outside with a large iron rod or bamboo. The people of the neighborhood saw the matter in the morning.
