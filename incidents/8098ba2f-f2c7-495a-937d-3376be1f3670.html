113. In Chittagong temple donation box of the Sri Sri Sarvajanin Brajdham Puja Temple in Enayet Bazar Paschim Goalparawas broken and stolen
Date of Incident:
October 16, 2024
Division: Chattogram
District: Chattogram
Police Station: Kotwali, CMP
Specific Location: Paschim Goalpara, Enayet Bazar
Details
According to the case report, on October 17, at around 7:30 am, Rana Das, the general secretary of the Sri Sri Sarvajanin Brajdham Puja Temple in Enayet Bazar Paschim Goalpara under Kotwali Police Station in Chittagong City, came to the temple to offer prayers and found that the tin gate of the temple was open and two donation boxes inside the temple were broken. At one stage, he entered the temple and searched the items and found that the cash inside the two donation boxes inside the temple, which was approximately 70 thousand taka, was missing.

The Kotwali Police have arrested two people for breaking into the donation box of the Sri Sri Sarvajanin Brajdham Puja Temple in Enayet Bazar Goalpara under Kotwali Police Station in Chittagong. The stolen money was also recovered from the donation box.
