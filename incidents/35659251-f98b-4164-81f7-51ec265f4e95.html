1. Muslims attack Hindu gold shop owner in Tangail.
Date of Incident:
April 5, 2025
Division: Dhaka
District: Tangail
Upazilla: Delduar
Police Station: Delduar
Details
In the Delduar South Bazaar area under Delduar Police Station in Tangail district, a gold shop owner named Akhil Karmakar was physically assaulted and injured after being accused of blasphemy and making offensive remarks. He was later taken into custody by the police. It is still unclear whether any looting occurred at his shop. While the police have detained Akhil Karmakar, no arrests have been reported yet regarding those who carried out the attack. The local Hindu minority community is currently living in extreme fear and insecurity.
