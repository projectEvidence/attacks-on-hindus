54. Human chain formed against Supta Saha in Kalmakanda Upazila of Netrokona to protest for insulting of Prophet Muhammad (PBUH)
Date of Incident:
February 27, 2025
Division: Mymensingh
District: Netrokona
Upazilla: Kalmakanda Upazilla
Details
A human chain and protest rally have been held in Mohanganj to protest against the youth's alleged insults to the Prophet Muhammad and to demand protection of the sanctity of the month of Ramadan. The Qawmi Aliya Oikya student organization has formed this human chain demanding the arrest of the notorious insulter Supta Saha as soon as possible and exemplary punishment. The human chain was held in front of the Mohanganj Big Mosque after Jumma prayers on Friday (February 28). The insulter Supta Saha is from Kalmakanda Upazila of Netrokona. The speakers said in their speeches, ''Muslims will not tolerate the insult of the world prophet, let the weapon of Badr fall into the abyss once again, this religion is mine, this land is my Prophet, we want justice for the insult. Naraye Takbir Allahu Akbar" and Bangladesh is a non-communal country. Such incidents happen in this country. We will demand from the government and administration that Supta Saha should be brought under the law and hanged." The human chain was presided over by the convener of the organization, Niyamul Haque Lyon. Mufti Amir Uddin, Imam and Khatib of Mohanganj Big Mosque, spoke as the chief guest. Maulana Ruhul Amin Nagari, Organizing Secretary of Hefazat-e-Islam Mohanganj Upazila, spoke as the special guest.Among others present were Ulama Dal General Secretary Maulana Nuruzzaman, Jamiat Ulama-e-Islam leader Hafez Qari Masum Billah, Maulana Sheikh Mohammad Abdullah Jalali, Maulana Sakibul Hasan, Upazila Youth Jamiat President Maulana Tofail Ahmed Habibi, Youth leader Maulana Arman Hossain Bakki, Hafez Ariful Islam Emon, Chhatra Jamiat Mohanganj Upazila Secretary Hafez Nazmul Hasan Shanto, Municipal Chhatra Jamiat Organizing Secretary Hafez Ruhul Amin Tushar, Islamic Students Movement Vice President Mahdi Hasan Shafayat, Hafez Abdullah Bin Nur Mohammad, Qari Khairul Islam, Hafez Muzakkir Hossain, Jabir Mahmud Abdul Momin, Rabiul Awal, Mujahidul Islam Jihadi, Tanvir Ahmed, Abid Hasan, Rahul Mia, Mehdi Hasan Abdullah Al Mashud, Abdul Basir, Mizanur Rahman, Iman Mia, Rakibul Islam, Delwar Hossain Akram Hossain and others.
