130. A group of land grabbers is trying to seize a record amount of land belonging to 21 Hindu families in the Kalikapur area of ​​Patuakhali city
Date of Incident:
October 20, 2024
Division: Barishal
District: Patuakhali
Upazilla: Patuakhali Sadar
Specific Location: Kalikapur
Details
A group of land grabbers is trying to seize the land of 21 Hindu families in the Kalikapur area of ​​Patuakhali city. The Hindu families have filed a written complaint with Patuakhali Sadar Police Station on Monday, October 21, seeking redress. However, the Hindu families claim that the land grabbers are threatening to kill and injure them and drive them out of the country, claiming that they are BNP workers.
