17. Seven talented Hindu students were expelled from Pabna University of Science and Technology for protesting attacks on Hindus on Facebook
Date of Incident:
March 15, 2025
Division: Rajshahi
District: Pabna
Upazilla: Pabna Sadar
Specific Location: Dhaka - Pabna Hwy, Pabna 6600, Bangladesh
Details
These seven talented Hindu students were expelled from Pabna University of Science and Technology for protesting attacks on Hindus on Facebook:
Biddut Chandra paul
Subarna Sarkar
Dipu Biswas
Tanay Sarkar
Otri saha
Ankon Ghosh
Parmita Chakrabarty
