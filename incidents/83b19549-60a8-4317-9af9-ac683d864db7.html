18. A sixth-grade Hindu student was raped on her way home from school in Rangpur
Date of Incident:
March 17, 2025
Division: Rangpur
District: Rangpur
Upazilla: Mithapukur
Specific Location: বড় মির্জাপুর ইউনিয়ন/বড় হযরতপুর ইউনিয়নের চিথলী পশ্চিমপাড়া এলাকা
Details
A sixth-grade student was raped on her way home from private school in Mithapukur, Rangpur. The student's father has filed a case with the police station in this incident. The incident took place on Tuesday (March 18) afternoon. The police could not arrest the rapist.

According to police and local sources, the student, the daughter of a farmer from a village in Boro Mirzapur Union of the upazila, is studying in sixth grade at a local high school. The school is now closed. However, the girl used to go to school every day to study privately. She was returning home after finishing her private studies in the morning. Alam Mia (40), son of the deceased Abu Mia of the area, who was lying in wait in a corn field on the way, grabbed the girl and took her inside the corn field and raped her.

Later, when the girl heard her crying, people around the spot rescued her and admitted her to the hospital. The rapist fled. Mithapukur Police Station OC Abu Bakkar Siddique said that a case has been filed with the police station in this incident. The girl has been rescued and admitted to the hospital for a health check-up.
