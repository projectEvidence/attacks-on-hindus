40. Khalid Mahmud Alias Hridoy Khan Arrested for Harassing Women and Inciting Hindu-Muslim Discord Through Offensive Social Media Comments in Savar, Dhaka
Date of Incident:
March 9, 2025
Division: Dhaka
District: Dhaka
Upazilla: Savar
Details
Accused of harassing women through indecent gestures. Savar police arrested a man named Khalid Mahmud alias Hridoy Khan.

He was arrested from Savar's Aminbazar on Monday afternoon. AIG (Media and PR) of Police Headquarters Enamul Haque Sagar confirmed the matter.


He said that on a Facebook page, Khalid Mahmud Hridoy Khan was seen making indecent comments to different girls for not going out without hijab. She is spreading it through social media and humiliating all the girls in the society. She also says that she will be raped if she doesn't wear the hijab and that her comments towards women are offensive. He also made indecent comments towards young girls in the video.

The statement of the accused youth is an attempt to create discord between the Hindu and Muslim communities, the police headquarters said in the message. Legal action is being taken against him as a result of such acts that have created an unstable environment in the society.
