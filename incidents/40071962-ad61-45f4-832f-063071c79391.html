171. House vandalised and ransom was demanded from Shimul Saha
Date of Incident:
August 5, 2024
Division: Khulna
District: Jashore
Upazilla: Bagherpara
Specific Location: Narikelbaria
Details
On Tuesday morning (August 6), a group of people attacked and vandalized the house of Shimul Saha in Narikelbaria village. At that time, they demanded a ransom of one lakh taka from the people of the house.
