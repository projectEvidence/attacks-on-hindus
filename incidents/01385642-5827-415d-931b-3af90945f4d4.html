100. A gold crown given by Indian Prime Minister Narendra Modi to the idol of Jashoreshwari Temple in Shyamnagar, Satkhira, has been stolen
Date of Incident:
October 9, 2024
Division: Khulna
District: Satkhira
Upazilla: Shyamnagar
Details
A gold crown given by Indian Prime Minister Narendra Modi to the idol of Jashoreshwari Temple in Shyamnagar, Satkhira, has been stolen. The temple's CCTV footage shows a young man stealing the crown. On the afternoon of October 10, 2024, on March 27, 2021, Indian Prime Minister Narendra Modi personally gave this crown to the temple. The crown has not been recovered so far.
