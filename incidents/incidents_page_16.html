239. Computer business of Partha Pratim Nath was looted by armed miscreants in Jessore
Date of Incident:
August 4, 2024
Division: Khulna
District: Jashore
Upazilla: Jessore Sadar
Specific Location: Railway road
Details
A group of miscreants armed with sticks attacked and looted the Nath Computer business of Partha Pratim Nath, son of Jessore Sadar Upazila Awami League President Mohit Kumar Nath, located on the city's railway road. The miscreants looted computers, laptops, ACs, routers and imported components from the shop and three warehouses.

Partha Pratim Nath told Prothom Alo, "I have been running the business for 24 years. The miscreants, carrying the national flag on their heads and sticks in their hands, openly looted goods worth more than four crore taka. When the army was informed, a car finally arrived. But the looting could not be stopped. My father spent a lot of money fighting the national parliament and upazila elections against the boat symbol. We owe crores of taka to the bank. We are now unemployed. We are sitting at home. I have become destitute and have sat on the streets."
Involved Crimes

  • • Loot
