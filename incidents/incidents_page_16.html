226. Lina Bhattacharya's house on Rabindranath Road in the Hotel Bazar area of ​​Meherpur Municipality was attacked and looted
Date of Incident:
August 4, 2024
Division: Khulna
District: Meherpur
Upazilla: Meherpur-S
Specific Location: Rabindranath Sarak, Hotel Bazar
Details
Lina Bhattacharya's house on Rabindranath Road in the Hotel Bazar area of ​​Meherpur Municipality was attacked and looted. Four people in the house were beaten up. Lina Bhattacharya said, "10-12 boys broke the lock of the main gate of the house, went to the second floor and beat us up. Later, they opened the cupboard of the house and took away gold jewelry and valuables. On the way, they set fire to the furniture in the house and left."
