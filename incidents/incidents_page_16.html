226. A group of Muslim fundamentalist youth attacked four Hindu temples
Date of Incident:
August 4, 2024
Division: Khulna
District: Jashore
Upazilla: Bagherpara
Specific Location: Rishipalli, Sultannagar
Details
Four temples in Sultannagar Rishipalli, Bagharpara upazila of Jessore have been attacked. The Basanti Temple, Harichand Temple, Kali Temple and Manasa Temple in Rishipalli were attacked on the evening of August 5, after the fall of the government. A group of Muslim fundamentalist youth from Dhalgram Bazar entered their village carrying sticks, machete and other indigenous sharp weapons.
