98. Hindu houses were looted in Gobindpur, Kushtia while Hindus were busy in Durga Puja
Date of Incident:
October 12, 2024
Division: Khulna
District: Kushtia
Upazilla: Mirpur
Specific Location: Malakarpara, Gobindapur
Details
Hindu homes were looted while Hindus were busy in Durga Puja in Malakar Para, Gobindpur, Kushtia. During this time, several families' homes were entered and everything was looted.
