100. Sanjit Sen, principal of Inatganj Degree College in Nabiganj Upazila of Habiganj, has resigned under pressure after the political situation changed
Date of Incident:
October 9, 2024
Division: Sylhet
District: Habiganj
Upazilla: Nabiganj
Details
Sanjit Sen, the principal of Inatganj Degree College in Habiganj's Nabiganj Upazila, has resigned after protests by students and locals.

On Thursday (October 10) afternoon, Inatganj Union Parishad Chairman Noman Hossain confirmed the principal's resignation and said that students and locals have been holding various protests demanding his resignation for a long time, as a result of which the college had become dysfunctional.
