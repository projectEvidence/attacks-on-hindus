313. Mrinal Kanti Chatterjee of Rakhalgachi Union of Bagerhat was hacked to death
Date of Incident:
August 4, 2024
Division: Khulna
District: Bagerhat
Upazilla: Bagerhat Sadar
Specific Location: Rakhalgachi Union
Details
Mrinal Kanti Chatterjee of Rakhalgachi Union of Bagerhat Sadar Thana was hacked to death by miscreants. His wife and daughter were admitted to the hospital with serious injuries after being hacked. The deceased, Mrinal Kanti Chatterjee (65), is the son of the late Keshab Lal Chatterjee of Chhota Paikpara village. He was a former assistant teacher at the nearby Madhudia Government Primary School. His injured wife Shefali Chatterjee (60) and daughter Jhuma Rani Chatterjee (35) have been admitted to the Bagerhat District Hospital.
