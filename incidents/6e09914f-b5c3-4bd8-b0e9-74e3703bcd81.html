81. Muslims attacked bus carrying Hindus on their way to peaceful protest gathering in Rangpur and seriously injured two people. Miscreants then fled into a mosque.
Date of Incident:
November 22, 2024
Division: Rangpur
District: Rangpur
Upazilla: Kaunia
Specific Location: Kaunia bus stand area
Details
On the way to the rally venue in Rangpur, the bus carrying Hindus was stopped and then attacked at the Kaunia bus stand by Islamic extremists shouting 'Indian agents' and other anti ISKCON slogans. Hindus got off the bus in fear and fled to various places for shelter. Two people were seriously injured in the attack. They are Nil Mohan, a resident of Durakuti area of ​​Lalmonihat, and Shipan Karmakar, a worker from Satharpara area of ​​Rajarhat upazila of Kurigram. They were admitted to the hospital. After attacking Hindus miscreants entered a mosque.
Hindus alleged that bus services were stopped on several routes, including Dinajpur (https://drive.google.com/file/d/1QfbetL4g3TtHV-YxJMrLmCPddrbB7zpk/view?usp=drive_link).
