75. Ananta Kumar Dhar was arrested for hurting religious sentiment in an alleged facebook post according to the filed case of Faisal Sarkar Rifat in Narsingdi
Date of Incident:
February 10, 2025
Division: Dhaka
District: Narsingdi
Upazilla: Palash
Details
Police have arrested a young man named Ananta Kumar Dhar (20) on charges of insulting the Prophet (PBUH) on social media Facebook, hurting religious sentiments. He was sent to jail by a court on Tuesday (February 11) afternoon and Sunday afternoon. Earlier on the same day, a team of district police led by Narsingdi Additional Superintendent of Police (Sadar Circle) Md. Anwar Hossain Shamim conducted a raid in the Rampur area of ​​Monohardi upazila and arrested him. The arrested Ananta is the son of Nirmal Kumar Dhar, a resident of Balia Bakultala village under Palash upazila of Narsingdi. Police and locals said that on February 10, a post titled 'Simple living was the Prophet's choice' was posted on the social media Facebook page titled 'Bangla Citizens Expatriate'. Arrested Ananta Kumar made scathing comments there from a fake ID he operated. Tension spread when hundreds of locals took out a procession on the evening of February 10 demanding Ananta's arrest and trial, claiming that he had disrespected and insulted the Prophet (PBUH) through his comments. During this time, many people went to Ananta's house and started searching for him. Later, the situation calmed down with the presence of the police and the assurance of arresting the culprit.Later, at 4 am on Tuesday, the police arrested Ananta while he was hiding in a hole in the bushes in the Rampur area under Monohardi police station in Narsingdi district. With the help of information technology, a police team surrounded the entire area and conducted a combing operation. Sensing the presence of the police, the police and the local people chased and arrested him when he tried to escape. This morning, a person named Faisal Sarkar Rifat filed a case with Palash police station, making Ananta Kumar Dhar the sole accused, and he was arrested in this case. In this regard, Narsingdi Additional Superintendent of Police (Sadar Circle) Md. Anwar Hossain Shamim said that a young man named Anant Kumar Dhar has been arrested on charges of making religiously provocative comments on Facebook. He admitted the charges against him and gave a statement to the court under Section 164.
