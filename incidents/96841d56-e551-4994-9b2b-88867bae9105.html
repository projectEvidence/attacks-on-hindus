82. 13-Year-Old Hindu Girl Pragya Mallick was Abducted in Broad Daylight in Barishal; Police Inaction Fuels Family’s Despair
Date of Incident:
December 4, 2024
Division: Barishal
District: Barishal
Police Station: Kotwali Barishal
Details
A gang of teenagers lead by Md. Riaz kidnapped 13-year-old Hindu minor Pragya Mallick from Barisal in the afternoon! Her father Pankaj Kumar Mallick filed a complaint at the Barisal Kotwali Model Police Station in this regard. But due to the silence of the police, the girl could not be found! On behalf of the family, a complaint was filed with the Home Affairs Advisor on December 5.
