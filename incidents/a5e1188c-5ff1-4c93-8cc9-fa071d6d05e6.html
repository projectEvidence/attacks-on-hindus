62. Attack, vandalism and looting at Jalkhabar Restaurant and Mistanna Bhandar "জলখাবার রেস্টুরেন্ট ও মিষ্টান্ন ভান্ডার" in Mohakhali for not getting demanded money
Date of Incident:
January 20, 2025
Division: Dhaka
District: Dhaka
Upazilla: Dhaka City
Police Station: Banani
Specific Location: Jalkhabar Restaurant, Rd Number 2, Dhaka 1212. Google Maps: Q9JX+8MW
Details
Miscreants attacked, vandalized and looted a snack restaurant and a dessert shop in the capital's Mohakhali after the extortion was not paid.
The incident took place on Tuesday (January 21) evening. The snack shop operators said that 40-50 unknown people suddenly attacked and vandalized the restaurant after the extortion was not paid. During this, the attackers looted 20-30 thousand taka from the cash counter.
The snack shop operators said that they had never seen the attackers before. A case has been filed with Banani Police Station in this incident. However, the police refused to comment on the matter.
