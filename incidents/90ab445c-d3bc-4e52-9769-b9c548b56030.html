44. Attack and vandalism on office room at Khulna Shitala Bari Temple under the guise of student coordinator
Date of Incident:
January 24, 2025
Division: Khulna
District: Khulna
Police Station: Khulna
Specific Location: Shitala Bari Temple (শীতলাবাড়ী মন্দির) , Maulvi Para Rd, Khulna, Bangladesh
Details
Two culprits attacked and vandalized office room at Khulna Shitala Bari Temple (শীতলাবাড়ী মন্দির) under the guise of student coordinator. The temple authority nabbed two perpetrators.

As of March 13, 2025, the source Facebook video post of the proof https://youtu.be/L4wJ2aXI3Po seems to have been removed. This post on x.com has a similar/same video available: https://x.com/HinduVoice_in/status/1883014360718590207 which shows the incident date as January 24, 2025.
