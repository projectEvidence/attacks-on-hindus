187. House of retired Hindu school teacher Kartik Chandra was attacked and looted in Bhatpara village of Lalpur, Natore
Date of Incident:
August 5, 2024
Division: Rajshahi
District: Natore
Upazilla: Lalpur
Details
The house of Kartik Chandra, a retired school teacher in Bhatpara village, was attacked and looted. He was known as a popular teacher and philanthropist in the area.
