149. House of retired Hindu school teacher was attacked and looted
Date of Incident:
August 4, 2024
Division: Rajshahi
District: Natore
Upazilla: Lalpur
Details
The house of Kartik Chandra, a retired school teacher in Bhatpara village, was attacked and looted. He was known as a popular teacher and philanthropist in the area.
