66. ইসকনকে সন্ত্রাসী সংগঠন বলা হলে হিন্দুরা যখন তাদের আওয়াজ তুলেছিল, তখন বাংলাদেশ পুলিশ এবং সেনাবাহিনী চট্টগ্রামে হিন্দুদের গ্রেপ্তার অব্যাহত রেখেছিল।
Date of Incident:
November 6, 2024
Division: Chattogram
District: Chattogram
Police Station: Kotwali, CMP
Details
Hindus protested in Chittagong after ISKCON was called a terrorist organization. Then the administration, police and army started filing harassment cases against Hindus selectively. Regular arrests are being made in this incident. Like many others, four more people have been arrested in the cases filed in this incident. They are Arjit Khastagir, Tipu Nandi, Mishu Kar and Balai Chakraborty. The joint forces conducted raids in different parts of the city on Wednesday night and arrested them. A total of 53 people have been arrested in this incident.
These 53 people were produced before the Chittagong Metropolitan Magistrate Court under the guard of the joint forces on Thursday afternoon. The court rejected their bail application and ordered them to be sent to jail. They were later sent to jail, said Fazlul Quader Chowdhury, Officer-in-Charge (OC) of Kotwali Police Station.
