105. Two Hindus Monks Shyamal Das and Ranganath Das were arrested by police when they went to visit Chinmoy Krishna Das in Jail. No reason was provided.
Date of Incident:
November 29, 2024
Division: Chattogram
District: Chattogram
Police Station: Kotwali, CMP
Details
Chinmoy Krishna Das is currently in jail on the orders of the court. It is alleged that two more monks have been arrested while trying to give him medicine. Two of Chinmoy Krishna Das's assistants - Shyamal Das and Ranganath Das Brahmachari - had taken medicine to the jail for Chinmoy Krishna Das. At that time, the two monks were arrested. The police did not say anything about the reason for their arrest. There was no case against these two monks.
