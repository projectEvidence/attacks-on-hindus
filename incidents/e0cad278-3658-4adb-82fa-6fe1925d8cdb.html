140. A man named Utsav Mandal was beaten up by a mob for allegedly posting a status on social media that insulted the Prophet of Islam.
Date of Incident:
September 4, 2024
Division: Khulna
District: Khulna
Police Station: Sonadanga
Details
A man named Utsav Mandal was beaten up by a mob for allegedly posting a status on social media that insulted the Prophet of Islam.
Although it was initially reported that the teenager, who was seriously injured in the beating, had died, a statement from the army's spokesperson, the Inter-Services Public Relations (ISPR), said that he was alive.
The ISPR said in a statement that the injured person is 22 years old. The police had earlier said that he was 15 years old. The incident took place at around 12:15 am on Wednesday in the Sonadanga residential area of ​​Khulna.
