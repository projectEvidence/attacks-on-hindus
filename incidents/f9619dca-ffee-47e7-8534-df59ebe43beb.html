228. A group of attackers vandalized and set fire to the house of Bishnupada Dutta
Date of Incident:
August 4, 2024
Division: Khulna
District: Jashore
Upazilla: Abhaynagar
Specific Location: Payra Union
Details
On the evening of August 5, a group of attackers vandalized and set fire to the house of Vishnupada Dutta, former chairman of Payra Union Parishad (UP) in Barandi village.
