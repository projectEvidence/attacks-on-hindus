43. Mozammel Haque alias Manik (50) raped a fifth-grader Hindu child in Thakurgaon
Date of Incident:
March 8, 2025
Division: Rangpur
District: Thakurgaon
Upazilla: Thakurgaon Sadar
Details
Police have arrested a teacher of a school for allegedly raping a fifth-grade student in Sadar upazila of Thakurgaon. He was arrested from a village in the upazila on Saturday afternoon. The arrestee has been identified as Mozammel Haque alias Manik, 50. He is an assistant teacher of a local government primary school.

According to the case statement, Mozammel used to teach mathematics and English to 10 to 12 fifth-grade students of the school during the holy month of Ramadan. Yesterday, the victim went to the school for private studies at around 10 am. Mozammel gave them leave around 11:15 am. But when he came home, Mozammel called the victim back to school. He then raped the child in the school room. Threaten not to tell anyone about the incident. At home, the child told his mother everything.


When the incident came to light, police arrested the accused Mozammel Haque. Later, the victim's father filed a case with Bhulli Police Station under the Women and Children Repression Prevention Act. Mozammel was taken to court on Sunday morning after being shown arrested in the case. Seeing Mozammel in court, angry students attacked him. Later, additional police arrived and brought the situation under control. The court sent him to jail.

Students of different educational institutions demonstrated and formed a human chain in Thakurgaon town at 11 am demanding the highest punishment for the rapist. Later, they took position in front of the District Judge and Sessions Judge Court and shouted slogans like 'We want justice, we want justice', 'There is no place for rapists in my Sonar Bangla', 'black hands of rapists, break it, crush it'.
Bhulli Police Station Officer-in-Charge (OC) Saiful Islam Sarkar said the accused was sent to the district jail through a court this morning.
