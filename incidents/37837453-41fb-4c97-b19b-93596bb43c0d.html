56. Nishan Debnath (20) from Patiya, Chittagong was arrested by police on alleged blasphemy post: The islamist blasphemz mob tried to lynch him.
Date of Incident:
December 5, 2024
Division: Chattogram
District: Chattogram
Upazilla: Patiya
Specific Location: Ward No. 05, Jiri Union ( জিরি ইউনিয়নের ৫নং ওয়ার্ডে )
Details
In the name of hurting religious sentiments, targeting Hindus or religious minorities in Bangladesh to harass or file false cases is nothing new. Recently, on Friday (December 6), the police arrested a Hindu youth named Nishan Debnath without any evidence on the same pretext.

He was arrested from the Shantir Hat area of ​​the upazila at around 10:30 am. The matter was confirmed by the Officer-in-Charge (OC) of Sadar Model Police Station Abu Zayed Md. Nazmun Noor.

The arrested student Nishan Debnath (20) is the son of Tapan Nath of Sudhir Doctor's house in Ward No. 5 of Jiri Union of the upazila. Nishan Debnath is said to be a class 12 student of Kusumpura School and College.

According to Patia Police Station sources, Nishan Debnath, who was arrested 6 days ago, insulted the Holy Prophet Hazrat Muhammad (PBUH) in a post on his Facebook page; some students in the Shantir Hat area tried to kill him on such allegations. However, the police arrested him without waiting for or paying attention to the evidence-

On receiving the news, the patrolling police team immediately reached the spot and rescued Nishan Debnath from the hands of the public and arrested him and confiscated his mobile phone. Later, Sajjad Hossain of Kusumpura Union filed a written complaint with Patiya Police Station on Friday for insulting the Holy Prophet Hazrat Muhammad (PBUH).

After registering the complaint as a case under the Cyber ​​Security Act of Patiya Police Station, the Patiya Police Station arrested Nishan Debnath as the sole accused in that case and sent him to jail through the court on Friday afternoon.

Patiya Police Station Officer-in-Charge (OC) Abu Zayed Md. Nazmun Noor said that the arrested student Nishan Debnath had posted a controversial post on Facebook about the Holy Prophet Hazrat Muhammad (PBUH) on social media, which spread on Facebook. The law and order situation in the upazila is normal in this incident. Intelligence surveillance continues.
