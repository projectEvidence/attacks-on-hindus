64. Md. Bakul and Shikha Begum Accused of forceful grabbing of Hindu Temples in Pakhaliya Para, Keraniganj
Date of Incident:
December 28, 2024
Division: Dhaka
District: Dhaka
Upazilla: Keraniganj
Specific Location: Pakhalia Para
Details
On Sunday, December 29, 2024, around 4:00 PM, a deeply disturbing incident unfolded in Pakhaliya Para, Shubadda, Keraniganj, within the Dhaka District, where Md. Bakul Sarkar and Shikha Begum have been accused of leading an attempted forceful occupation of two historic Hindu temples—the Shree Shree Raksha Kali Temple and the Radha Govinda Temple.

Local residents allege that the group tried to assert physical control over the temple premises without legal authority, sparking alarm and panic among the minority Hindu community. The temples have long served as centres of worship and heritage for the local Hindu population.

A heart-wrenching video recorded by a resident captured villagers crying out for help, as women desperately appealed:

“Brother, please save us. Our husbands are being beaten while trying to protect the temple. We earnestly request everyone—Hindu, Muslim, students, the general public—please help save this temple.”

The footage, now circulating in local networks, illustrates the raw fear, helplessness, and unity of a community under threat. Despite clear signs of communal aggression and physical violence, locals report inaction from law enforcement, raising concerns over the state's ability—or willingness—to protect minority religious rights.
