243. The house of Bhajan Karmakar and idols in temple were vandalized in Sonarai Union of Gabtoli Upazila
Date of Incident:
August 4, 2024
Division: Rajshahi
District: Bogura
Upazilla: Gabtali
Specific Location: Sonarai Union
Details
The house of Bhajan Karmakar and idols in temple were vandalized in Sonarai Union of Gabtoli Upazila. They were robbed of 5 bharis of gold and beaten up mercilessly.
