213. Homeopathic doctor Manoj Kumar Biswas was beaten up, his shop was looted, house temple was vandalized
Date of Incident:
August 4, 2024
Division: Dhaka
District: Faridpur
Upazilla: Madhukhali
Specific Location: Bamundi Bazar, Meghchami Union
Details
The attackers attacked and vandalized the shop of homeopathic doctor Manoj Kumar Biswas in Bamundi Bazar. Manoj was beaten up. Later, he went into hiding with his family. The miscreants attacked the house, vandalized and looted it. They broke the idols of Hari Thakur and Shanti Mata in the temple of the house. They looted his medicine shop in the market.
