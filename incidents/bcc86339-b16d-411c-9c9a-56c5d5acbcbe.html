245. The house of Hindu Parishad President Sahadev Saha vandalized, women beaten up.
Date of Incident:
August 4, 2024
Division: Chattogram
District: Noakhali
Upazilla: Hatia
Details
The house of Hindu Parishad President Sahadev Saha in Sonadia, Hatia Upazila of Noakhali, was attacked and vandalized, along with women being beaten up. The victim's family informed the Hatia Police Station and the Coast Guard over the phone, but no one went to the scene.
