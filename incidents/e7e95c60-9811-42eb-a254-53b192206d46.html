11. Hindu Family in Cumilla’s Lalmai Upazila Resists Violent Land-Grabbing Attempt Despite Court Ban
Date of Incident:
May 15, 2025
Division: Chattogram
District: Comilla
Upazilla: Lalmai
Specific Location: West Ashwathtala, Bagmara South Union
Details
In Paschim Ashwath Tala, Bagmara Dakshin Union, Lalmai Upazila, Cumilla, a Hindu family has accused local resident Nazrul Islam of repeatedly attempting to seize their land and block their access road, despite a court order prohibiting such actions. The latest attempt occurred on the morning of Friday, 16 May 2025.

According to victim Gopal Pal, Nazrul Islam has long been trying to evict the family from their property by demolishing their shops and obstructing their private road. A legal case regarding the land dispute is already pending in court, and the court has issued a prohibition order in favor of the Hindu family. However, defying this order, Nazrul allegedly arrived on Friday morning with over 200 hired men to forcibly occupy the family’s road and shops.

Local residents helped the Pal family resist the attackers. Shortly afterward, personnel from the Lalmai Army Camp and Lalmai Police Station arrived at the scene, prompting the hired men to flee. Despite the family’s legal victory in securing a court injunction, they say they continue to live in fear of further attacks and harassment from the land grabbers.

Gopal Pal has appealed to the authorities to ensure his family’s safety and protection of their ancestral property, emphasizing that the intimidation and violence are part of ongoing efforts to displace them from their land solely because they are a Hindu minority family in the area.
