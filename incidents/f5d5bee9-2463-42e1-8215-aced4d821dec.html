76. Dev Dulal Guha, Lalmonirhat Government College teacher, had been taken into Detective Branch custody for making allegedly controversial posts on social media Facebook,Lalmonirhat
Date of Incident:
February 7, 2025
Division: Rangpur
District: Lalmonirhat
Upazilla: Lalmanirhat Sadar
Details
Lalmonirhat Government College teacher Dev Dulal Guha had been taken into DB custody for making allegedly controversial posts on social media Facebook. On Saturday afternoon, February 8, the students handed over Deb Dulal, a teacher of the Physics Department of Lalmonirhat Government College, to the police. Eggs were also thrown at him while he was being taken to the car. The students against discrimination in the district said that Dev Dulal had made provocative posts on his Facebook ID that were in conflict with the current government and some of the posts hurt religious sentiments. That is why the students took a stand since noon. Later, after being questioned by the OC of Lalmonirhat Sadar Police Station and police personnel, he was quickly taken away from there in a white microbus. After taking him away, the students against discrimination went to take a stand in front of the Deputy Commissioner's office. Later, in front of the Deputy Commissioner's office, Superintendent of Police Tariqul Islam and Deputy Commissioner HM Rakib Haider heard the complaint verbally from the students against discrimination. After hearing the complaint, both the Deputy Commissioner and the Superintendent of Police assured that if the allegations against Dev Dulal are proven, they will be tried according to the prevailing laws of the country. They also assured that he will not be able to enter Lalmonirhat Government College from now on. In addition, the college principal and rescue authorities have also been informed about Dev Dulal.
