158. Temple was broken and everything necessary was looted in Dhaka college
Date of Incident:
August 16, 2024
Division: Dhaka
District: Dhaka
Upazilla: Dhaka City
Specific Location: Dhaka college
Details
Hindu students live in the West Dormitory of Dhaka College in the capital. On August 17, 2024, in the name of a weapons search, the door of the worship room in front of the Nat Mandir on the 3rd floor was broken and everything necessary was looted, and the idol of Goddess Saraswati kept under the dormitory was vandalized.
