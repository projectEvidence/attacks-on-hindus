202. Hindu college teacher was attacked with sharp weapon and house was looted
Date of Incident:
August 4, 2024
Division: Rajshahi
District: Natore
Upazilla: Lalpur
Specific Location: Pal para, Madhabpur
Details
The houses of college teacher Digendranath Pal and Ranendranath Pal were attacked in Madhabpur Palpara of the upazila. Their belongings were looted. The attackers hit Ranendranath on the hand with a sharp weapon, causing bloody injuries.
