5. Horrific Night of Looting, Gang Rape and Physical Assault at Hindu Home in Magura’s Sripur
Date of Incident:
May 27, 2025
Division: Khulna
District: Magura
Upazilla: Sreepur
Details
A brutal and targeted attack unfolded in Harinandi village of Sripur upazila, Magura district, where a Hindu family was subjected to a terrifying night of violence. On the night of [insert date if known], an armed gang of assailants broke into the home of Kishore Kumar Ray, also known locally as Madhu Thakur, and looted the house before committing heinous acts of physical assault and gang rape.

The attackers, suspected to be part of an organized and communally motivated gang, forcibly entered the house, ransacked it, and stole valuable belongings. In an act of unimaginable cruelty, they gang-raped Kishore Kumar’s wife and brutally beat both husband and wife, leaving them gravely injured and unconscious.

It was only the next morning that neighbors, hearing no activity from the house, discovered the couple lying in critical condition. They were rushed to Magura Sadar Hospital, where they are currently undergoing treatment.

This barbaric assault has shaken the entire Hindu community in the area, who now live in fear and insecurity. Locals strongly suspect a communal motive behind the attack, as Hindu homes in remote villages are often seen as soft targets. The community is demanding an immediate and impartial investigation, arrest of the perpetrators, and ensured protection for minority Hindu families, who continue to face violence, intimidation, and injustice.
