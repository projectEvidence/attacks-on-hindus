63. Former associate of Awami League leader has been accused of forcibly occupying a temple and cremation ground in Manikganj
Date of Incident:
October 22, 2024
Division: Dhaka
District: Manikganj
Upazilla: Shibalaya
Specific Location: Kasadoho, Goalkhali
Details
Swapan Kumar Debnath, the private secretary or associate of the recently defunct MP from Barisal 4 constituency Pankaj Kumar Debnath, has been accused of forcibly occupying the temple, cremation ground and the only public road in Kasadoho village of Shivaloy upazila of Manikganj to construct a state-of-the-art bungalow. We want the government to take action in this regard and rescue the temple and cremation ground.
