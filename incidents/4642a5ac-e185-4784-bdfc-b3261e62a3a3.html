148. An attempt to make Hijab mandatory for Hindu students at Muslim Uddin Girls High School in Daspara, Rangpur city
Date of Incident:
September 25, 2024
Division: Rangpur
District: Rangpur
Upazilla: Rangpur Sadar
Specific Location: Daspara
Details
Students protested at Muslim Uddin Girls High School in Daspara, Rangpur city, alleging an attempt to make Hijab mandatory for Hindu students. The District Commissioner has formed an investigation committee in response to the protest.

  • • Religious Policing
