298. Shankar Banerjee's house in Kirtipasha, Jhalokati Sadar Upazila, was attacked and looted on the afternoon of August 5 by radical mob
Date of Incident:
August 4, 2024
Division: Barishal
District: Jhalokati
Upazilla: Jhalokati Sadar
Specific Location: Kirtipasha
Details
Shankar Banerjee's house in Kirtipasha, Jhalokati Sadar Upazila, was attacked and looted on the afternoon of August 5. In addition to taking goods, they also slaughtered a goat and two cows in public in the market and cooked them for consumption.
