155. Three tribal people killed by Muslim Bengali Settlers while some of the security personnel encouraged and supported the attackers instead of controlling a clash between the settlers and local tribes
Date of Incident:
September 18, 2024
Division: Chattogram
District: Khagrachari
Upazilla: Dighinala
Details
On September 19, clashes broke out between Bengali settlers and local hill tribes. It is alleged that 37 tribal houses and shops were burnt down in Dighinala of Khagrachari district in the wake of the clashes. The local tribals alleged that some of the security personnel, instead of bringing the situation under control, encouraged and supported the attackers. Junan Chakma (20), Dhananjay Chakma (50) and Rubel Chakma (20) were killed in the firing.
