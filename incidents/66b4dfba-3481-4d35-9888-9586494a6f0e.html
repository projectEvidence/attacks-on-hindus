1. Primeasia University Acting VC Dr. Shubhamoy Dutta Resigns Following Student Protests in Banani, Dhaka
Date of Incident:
December 20, 2025
Division: Dhaka
District: Dhaka
Police Station: Banani
Specific Location: Primeasia University Campus, Kamal Ataturk Avenue, Banani, Dhaka, Bangladesh
Details
Dr. Suvamoy Datta, the Acting Vice-Chancellor of Primeasia University and a respected Hindu academic, was compelled to resign amidst intense student agitation. On Saturday evening, he submitted his resignation to the university registrar, citing the student demands as the reason for stepping down.

While the official reason referenced student protests, the resignation came after days of aggressive demonstrations in Dhaka’s Banani area, where students blocked roads and chanted hostile slogans against university authorities. The protests accused the administration of corruption and failure to establish a permanent campus — issues for which Dr. Dutta was unfairly blamed.
