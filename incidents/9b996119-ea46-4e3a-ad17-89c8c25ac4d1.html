184. Land grabbers grab 22 Bighas of a Hindu family in Satkhira and Continuously threaten to kill them
Date of Incident:
August 14, 2024
Division: Khulna
District: Satkhira
Upazilla: Assasuni
Specific Location: Ward no 6, Assasuni Sadar Union
Details
Members of the persecuted family of Bolabaria, Assasuni, Satkhira at the press conference of Asashuni Press Club described their ordeal that started on August 15, 2024; whereas the press conference was held around March 01, 2025. Bishakha Rani Mondol of the victim family describes that the land grabbers don't let them stay in their house at night. The land grabbers occupied 22 bighas of land. Criminal also looted their business and took fish from their farm. Criminals continuously threaten to murder them wherever possible. Criminal threaten to tie them inside home and then set fire to their home. Victim Tushar Kanti Mondol named these perpetrators: Ohidul Islam, Majid, Hadi, Khokon Chandra Mondol. Khokon is their neighbour and the others live in Asashuni.
