198. Hindu households at Andharkota and Bollamukh village were attacked and looted by Islamist mob
Date of Incident:
August 4, 2024
Division: Khulna
District: Jashore
Upazilla: Bagherpara
Specific Location: Andharkota village, Bollamukh village
Details
Several Hindu houses were attacked and looted at Andharkota village. Most of them were Hindu farmers or day labourer.
Golak Chandra Biswas, one of the many victims, claimed that the assailants had vandalized the family home that day. They also looted gold, 50,000 taka in cash, and a music box. They then took out a motorcycle and a bicycle that were kept on the balcony of the house and placed them on one side of the yard. They then took out blankets, pillows, and clothes from the house, placed them on the motorcycle and bicycle, and set them on fire.

  • • Arson
  • • House Vandalism

199. Hindu temple was demolished and looted
Date of Incident:
August 4, 2024
Division: Dhaka
District: Shariatpur
Upazilla: Shariatpur Sadar -Palong
Specific Location: Dhanuka Manasa Bari
Details
A temple at Dhanuka Mansa Bari in Shariatpur district town has been demolished. On the night of August 5, a Muslim mob demolished the temple and looted the furniture. The Krishna temple was constructed in March. The Krishna idol in the temple was vandalised and destroyed. The attackers had surrounded nearby Hindu houses to vandalise them. Later in the night, when the army members went there, the miscreants fled.

204. Rich Hindu businessman house and business establishment was looted and vandalized
Date of Incident:
August 4, 2024
Division: Rangpur
District: Gaibandha
Upazilla: Palashbari
Specific Location: Gopinathpur (Jundah) in Barisal Union
Details
On the night of August 5, the home and business of businessman Shri Gopal Chandra Roy and his younger brother Gokul Chandra Roy of Palashbari Upazila were attacked. The houses were vandalized and looted. Five tractors of the brick kiln, a joint business of the two brothers, were burned. Businessman Shri Gopal Chandra Roy said that on the night of the incident, 30 to 35 youths wearing masks attacked the home with Ram Da, sticks and sticks. To save their lives, he and his family took shelter in the darkness next to the house.
