196. Extremist Muslims attacked and looted the houses of local Hindu temple committee president and his Hindu neighbours in Lalpur, Natore
Date of Incident:
August 5, 2024
Division: Rajshahi
District: Natore
Upazilla: Lalpur
Details
A group of people attacked the house of Dwipendranath Saha, the president of the Upazila Puja Celebration Council (পূজা উদ্‌যাপন পরিষদ) and the temple, who is also a school teacher, around 7:30 PM yesterday. The attackers broke down one of the gates of his house but failed to break the second one, so they shattered the windows from outside. Following this, they attacked the houses of his brother, Naresh Chandra Saha, and their neighbor, Shimul Sarkar. The attackers broke the doors, entered the houses, and carried out extensive vandalism.

Naresh Chandra claimed that the assailants looted over a hundred thousand taka, four tolas of gold jewelry, a laptop, mobile phones, and sacks of rice from his house.

Earlier, the attackers vandalized eight CCTV cameras at the Jotdaivaki Shiva and Kali Temple. Dwipendranath Saha told Prothom Alo over the phone that his house was located next to the temple. The attackers targeted his house right after attacking the temple. At that time, he informed the Lalpur police station, but an officer there said they would not be able to send police.

  • • Loot
  • • Robbery

201. Seven Hindu temples were attacked and set on fire at night on August 5, 2024
Date of Incident:
August 4, 2024
Division: Rangpur
District: Thakurgaon
Upazilla: Pirganj
Specific Location: Kosharaniganj Union, No. 2Kasha Raniganj Union
Details
On Monday night, August 5, 2024, 7 Hindu temples in Pirganj were attacked and set on fire. Satirghat Sri Sri Kali Temple, Sri Shiv Temple, Sri Sri Saraswati Temple in Kosharaniganj Union of Pirganj Upazila were attacked and set on fire. Elua Pukur Sri Kali Temple in Saidpur Union, Sri Sri Chandi Thakurani Kali Temple in Shivpur Village, Senua Tilipukur Sri Kali Temple, Sati Dah Cremation Ghat and Temple in No. 2 Kasha Raniganj Union were attacked and vandalized by the Vijaya procession. The iron stove for cremation of the body of the crematorium, tube well, tin ceiling of the temple and even the motor for bathing the body were looted. The place for chanting Krishna's name in the crematorium field was heinously damaged.

  • • Temple Vandalism
