196. House of Ananta Mukherjee and a temple in the nearby area of ​​Kuakata Municipality in Patuakhali were attacked
Date of Incident:
August 4, 2024
Division: Barishal
District: Patuakhali
Upazilla: Kalapara
Details
The residence of Awami League Joint General Secretary Ananta Mukherjee and a temple in the nearby area of ​​Kuakata Municipality in Patuakhali were attacked. The incident took place around 4:30 pm.

Ananta Mukherjee alleged, "After hearing the news of the government's resignation, BNP-Jamaat leaders and activists took out a procession in Kuakata Municipality. The procession came towards my house. At one stage, people from the procession attacked my house. Many of the attackers entered my house. They broke and smashed every thing in the house. Nothing in my house was saved. Then the attackers attacked the temple next to my house. The doors and windows of the temple were damaged."

  • • House Vandalism

202. Hindu households at Andharkota and Bollamukh village were attacked and looted by Islamist mob
Date of Incident:
August 4, 2024
Division: Khulna
District: Jashore
Upazilla: Bagherpara
Specific Location: Andharkota village, Bollamukh village
Details
Several Hindu houses were attacked and looted at Andharkota village. Most of them were Hindu farmers or day labourer.
Golak Chandra Biswas, one of the many victims, claimed that the assailants had vandalized the family home that day. They also looted gold, 50,000 taka in cash, and a music box. They then took out a motorcycle and a bicycle that were kept on the balcony of the house and placed them on one side of the yard. They then took out blankets, pillows, and clothes from the house, placed them on the motorcycle and bicycle, and set them on fire.

206. Hindu temple was demolished and looted
Date of Incident:
August 4, 2024
Division: Dhaka
District: Shariatpur
Upazilla: Shariatpur Sadar -Palong
Specific Location: Dhanuka Manasa Bari
Details
A temple at Dhanuka Mansa Bari in Shariatpur district town has been demolished. On the night of August 5, a Muslim mob demolished the temple and looted the furniture. The Krishna temple was constructed in March. The Krishna idol in the temple was vandalised and destroyed. The attackers had surrounded nearby Hindu houses to vandalise them. Later in the night, when the army members went there, the miscreants fled.
