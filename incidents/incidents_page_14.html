205. 1971 war crime witness Ajit Sarkar was murdered on 05 August 2024
Date of Incident:
August 4, 2024
Division: Mymensingh
District: Mymensingh
Upazilla: Phulpur
Specific Location: Balia Union
Details
After the fall of Sheikh Hasina, on August 5, the house of Ajit Sarkar, a witness in a war crimes case, was attacked by the son and relatives of the accused in Domkona village of Balia Union in Phulpur, Mymensingh.
In 2016, Ajit Sarkar Penu testified in court in a war crimes case against Ghiyasuddin, a member of the 1971 Peace Committee.
The attack was led by Ghiyasuddin's son Lavlu on the afternoon of August 5. Ajit Sarkar sought refuge in the house of his neighbor Mortuza Mia to save his life. He was taken from there and dragged around Mahishaunda Bazar and forced to wear a garland made of shoes for being a witness in the case. Then, four or five people, including Lavlu, killed him on the river bank and dumped his body. On the evening of August 8, when the body was found floating downstream, Lavlu's nephew Moizuddin chopped it into pieces and dumped it a few kilometers away.

  • • House Vandalism

Date of Incident:
August 4, 2024
Division: Barishal
District: Jhalokati
Upazilla: Kathalia
Specific Location: Uttarpar, Satani Bazar, Aurabunia Union
Details
On August 5, 2024, an attack was carried out on the Sri Sri Sarbajanin Durga Temple, north of Satani Bazar, Paschim Chitki village, Aurabunia union, Kanthalia upazila, Jhalokathi. Several hundreds islamist fundamentalists led by local Manjurul Sikder (50) took part in the attack. During this time, they vandalised and looted various items. The people of the minority Hindu community were unable to resist the sudden preplanned attack by the muslim fundamentalist group. They complained to the police. The police arrested the main conspirator, Manjurul Sikder, but the rest were not arrested. They are pressuring the Hindus to withdraw the case.
