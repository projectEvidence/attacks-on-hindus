198. Durga Temple was vandalised and looted
Date of Incident:
August 4, 2024
Division: Barishal
District: Jhalokati
Upazilla: Kathalia
Specific Location: Uttarpar, Satani Bazar, Aurabunia Union
Details
On August 5, 2024, an attack was carried out on the Sri Sri Sarbajanin Durga Temple, north of Satani Bazar, Paschim Chitki village, Aurabunia union, Kanthalia upazila, Jhalokathi. Several hundreds islamist fundamentalists led by local Manjurul Sikder (50) took part in the attack. During this time, they vandalised and looted various items. The people of the minority Hindu community were unable to resist the sudden preplanned attack by the muslim fundamentalist group. They complained to the police. The police arrested the main conspirator, Manjurul Sikder, but the rest were not arrested. They are pressuring the Hindus to withdraw the case.

202. More than 40 business establishments owned by minority Hindu was looted in Dinajpur community
Date of Incident:
August 4, 2024
Division: Rangpur
District: Dinajpur
Upazilla: Bochaganj
Specific Location: Chourongi Bazar, Ishania Union
Details
At least 40 minority shops were vandalized and looted in Chowrangi Bazar of Isania Union in Bochaganj Upazila of Dinajpur. On Monday afternoon, August 5, at least 100 people entered the market in a group and carried out an ambush. Later, when they moved towards Sahaspur village adjacent to the market, they were stopped by people from the minority community. Later, they left after threatening to leave their homes.

Eyewitness Hridoy Roy, a resident of Sahaspur village, said, "The attackers started vandalizing as soon as they entered the market. Our shops were selectively vandalized. The attackers had sticks and sharp weapons in their hands. We fled from the shop. Later, they looted the goods in front of our eyes. We were heading towards the village. At that time, when all of us in the neighborhood stood in front, they left."
