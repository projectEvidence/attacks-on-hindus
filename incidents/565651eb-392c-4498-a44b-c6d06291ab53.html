74. Under-construction idols were vandalized in Durgapur Union, Ulipur Upazila, Kurigram.
Date of Incident:
November 22, 2024
Division: Rangpur
District: Kurigram
Upazilla: Ulipur
Specific Location: Durgapur Union
Details
Several idols under construction on the veranda of a temple in Durgapur Union of Ulipur Upazila of Kurigram have been vandalized. The incident took place on Saturday (November 23) evening at the Jamuna Sarkarpara Sarvajanin Kali Temple in the union.

Police and local Sanatan Dharma followers could not identify anyone involved in the incident. Temple committee president Nitai Lal Sarkar said, "This kind of incident has never happened before. There was no threat. We do not understand who suddenly committed this kind of incident. We are shocked and speechless."
