291. Former Union Parishad Chairman Subhash Debnath Abhiram house looted and vandalised
Date of Incident:
August 4, 2024
Division: Khulna
District: Jashore
Upazilla: Bagherpara
Specific Location: Manikdoa, Dholgram union parishod
Details
A group of people attacked and vandalized the house of former Dhalgram Union Parishad (UP) chairman Subhash Debnath Abhiram in Manikdoa village. During this, they took two cows from the cowshed.
