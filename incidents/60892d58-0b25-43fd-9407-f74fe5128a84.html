56. Alokdia Shakyamuni Pagoda (শাক্যমুনি প্যাগোডা আলোকদিয়া), a Buddhist Rural Religious Center Pagoda (Bihar), in Lalmai, Comilla vandalized and looted at night
Date of Incident:
February 3, 2025
Division: Chattogram
District: Comilla
Upazilla: Lalmai
Specific Location: Alokadia Shakyamuni Pagoda, Alokdia Union
Details
During late night, miscreants broke the statue of Buddha, the donation box; stole a solar power battery, and looted the goods of Alokadia Shakyamuni Pagoda (শাক্যমুনি প্যাগোডা আলোকদিয়া), a Buddhist Rural Religious Center Pagoda (Bihar), in Lalmai, Comilla
