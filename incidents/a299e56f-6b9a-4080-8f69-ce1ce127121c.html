206. Four Hindu houses in Phulpur, Mymensingh were attacked on August 5 after the fall of Sheikh Hasina
Date of Incident:
August 4, 2024
Division: Mymensingh
District: Mymensingh
Upazilla: Phulpur
Details
Four Hindu houses in Phulpur, Mymensingh were attacked on August 5 after the fall of Sheikh Hasina. The mob killed the 1971 war crime witness Ajit Sarkar and then they attacked those houses.
