150. Radha Krishna Temple in Moulvibazar was vandalized in broad daylight
Date of Incident:
August 19, 2024
Division: Sylhet
District: Moulvibazar
Upazilla: Kulaura
Details
Around 50 people attacked the Radha Krishna Temple in Kulaura Municipality of Moulvibazar in broad daylight on August 20. Two youths named Abdul Majid and Arafat led the attack on the temple with sticks and country-made weapons. When Hindus tried to stop them, they were beaten and injured.
