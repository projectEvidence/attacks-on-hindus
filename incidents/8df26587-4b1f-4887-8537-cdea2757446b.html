77. Suspect Arrested After Idol Vandalism at Kali Mandir in Palashkanda
Date of Incident:
December 17, 2024
Division: Mymensingh
District: Mymensingh
Upazilla: Haluaghat
Specific Location: Polashkanda Kali Temple
Details
On Wednesday night, idols at the Kali Mandir in Palashkanda village, Bildora Union, were vandalized by unknown miscreants. The following morning, villagers discovered the desecrated deities and alerted authorities.

In response, police arrested Alal Uddin (27), son of Abdul Khalek, from Palashkanda village on suspicion of involvement in the attack. According to law enforcement sources, the investigation is ongoing, and more arrests may follow.

The desecration has sparked outrage among the local Hindu community and raised concerns over the growing insecurity of religious minorities in the area.

BNP Joint Secretary General Syed Emran Saleh Prince strongly condemned the incident and called for justice, urging authorities to take firm measures against those who incite communal unrest.
