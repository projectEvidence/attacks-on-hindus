41. Historic Hindu Worship Site in Gaibandha Occupied by Atoar Mandal.
Date of Incident:
March 9, 2025
Division: Rangpur
District: Gaibandha
Upazilla: Palashbari
Details
In Gaibandha, a local resident named Atoar Mandal has been accused of grabbing temple land.

According to the application and an on-site inspection, 5 decimals of land from Hal Dag No. 780 of Khas Khatian No. 1 under JL No. 11, Re S. No. 9 of Kashiabari Mouza in Kishoregari Union, was used many years ago as a place of worship for the Hindu community.

Locals alleged that when Atoar Mandal, son of the late Harez Ali Mandal of Kashiabari village, encroached upon the land and started building a house, they tried to stop him. However, Atoar Mandal continued construction without paying heed to any objections.
When contacted, the Upazila Assistant Commissioner (Land) stated, "There is no scope for occupying and using government khas land."
