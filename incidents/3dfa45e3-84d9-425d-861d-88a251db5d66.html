88. While buying medicine for his wife who is suffering from cancer Mona Das (মনা দাস) was arrested by Army and Police without any complaint or evidence. Is religious identity the crime?
Date of Incident:
November 4, 2024
Division: Chattogram
District: Chattogram
Police Station: Kotwali, CMP
Details
Mona Das, who was going to collect medicine for his wife, who was undergoing treatment for cancer at Chittagong Medical College, was also arrested and sent to jail. Later, when the media published a news report on the incident titled, "What will Mona's wife's ten-year-old son do now?", he was granted bail after 25 days. During these 25 days, the cancer-stricken wife had to go without food due to lack of money.
