283. The house of Pranayakanti Adhikari, the headmaster of Mahilara AN Secondary School in Gournadi was vandalized and looted
Date of Incident:
August 4, 2024
Division: Barishal
District: Barishal
Upazilla: Gaurnadi
Details
The house of Pranayakanti Adhikari, the headmaster of Mahilara AN Secondary School in Gournadi Upazila, was vandalized and looted on Monday evening. Pranayakanti Adhikari's family fled for their lives.
