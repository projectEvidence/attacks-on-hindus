193. The house of Sanjeev Mondal in Purba Khejuria village in Dacope, Khulna was attacked
Date of Incident:
August 4, 2024
Division: Khulna
District: Khulna
Upazilla: Dacope
Details
The house of Sanjeev Mondal, former youth and sports affairs secretary of the upazila Awami League, of Purba Khejuria village, was attacked and vandalized. Sanjeev Mondal said, "Today at around 1 pm, miscreants attacked my house. After that, the chairman vandalized the house extensively."
