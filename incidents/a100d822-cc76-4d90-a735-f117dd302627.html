99. Hussain was caught stealing betel nuts from Sudhir Dutt's tree in Mollarkul village, and after being released, he carried out a planned attack.
Date of Incident:
October 12, 2024
Division: Khulna
District: Bagerhat
Upazilla: Mollahat
Specific Location: Mollarkul
Details
Hussain (16), son of the deceased Khair Molla of the same village, was caught stealing betel nuts from the tree of Sudhir Dutta of Mollarkul village around 12 midnight on Friday. Later that night, a compromise was reached with the mediation of the elders. However, they also said that they were threatened to see him after the puja. A day after the puja ended on Saturday, Hussain's close friends carried out a planned attack on Sunday afternoon. A housewife and 4 youths were injured in the attack. The injured are Sukumay Mitra (30), Shiuli Basu (20), Joy Basu (27), Pallab Mitra (19) and Nitish Bhakta (28).
