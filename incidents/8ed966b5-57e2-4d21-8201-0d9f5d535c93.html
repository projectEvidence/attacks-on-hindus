139. The land of Bharat Das and his brother Shambhu Das was forcefully taken by local influential people in Jhenaidah in the name playground
Date of Incident:
September 6, 2024
Division: Khulna
District: Jhenaidah
Upazilla: Maheshpur
Details
Local influential people have been accused of encroaching on the land of a minority community and building a playground in Maheshpur, Jhenaidah.

The victim family held a press conference at the Jhenaidah Press Club on Saturday morning to protest the incident and demand the return of the land. They alleged in the press conference that Bharat Das and his brother Shambhu Das are the owners of land plots 104 and 103 of Khatian 2867 of Hamidpur Mouza No. 108 of Gopalpur village in Maheshpur upazila. They inherited the land and owned it. Their family has been cultivating this land for almost a hundred years. But when the land was mistakenly recorded in the government's Khatian during the field survey, they later filed a case and got the land back. After the fall of the Awami League government, some influential people tried to cut the crops planted on their land and seize the land. When they filed a case in the court regarding this, the court issued a ban. Ignoring this, on Thursday, Maheshpur Municipality Councilor Babul Hossain, Ikramul, Bipul, Khaled Hossain, Jinnah and a few others from that village destroyed the crops and occupied the land.
