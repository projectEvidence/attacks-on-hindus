104. Hindu crematorium and temple was vandalized in an attempt of forceful land grabbing in Kaliakoir, Gazipur
Date of Incident:
August 29, 2024
Division: Dhaka
District: Gazipur
Upazilla: Kaliakior
Specific Location: Safipur Bazar(Rongartek)
Details
On August 30, 2024, a local alias Lebu Kontaktar attacked the Safipur Public Crematorium and Radhagabinda Loknath Nat Mandir in the Safipur Bazar (Rongartek) area of ​​Kaliakoir Upazila of Gazipur. On Friday morning, Lebu Mia hired 50 to 60 terrorists to occupy the land. During the attack, they vandalized the Nat Mandir. When people from the Hindu community came out and tried to resist them, the terrorists also attacked them. 10-12 people were injured in this.
