36. Former youth Leader Zahid Hasan Happy Arrested Over Hindu Temple Vandalism in dilalpur, Pabna
Date of Incident:
February 21, 2025
Division: Rajshahi
District: Pabna
Upazilla: Pabna Sadar
Details
Former youth leader Zahid Hasan Happy was arrested following a case on temple vandalism in Pabna.

In the Dilalpur neighborhood of Pabna city, a former youth group leader has been arrested after allegations of vandalism at a temple on two occasions.

According to the officer in charge of the Sadar Police Station, Abdus Salam, the police arrested him from his locality on Sunday afternoon.The arrested individual, Zahid Hasan Happy (40), is the son of Abdur Rahim Khan from the Kalachandpara neighborhood of Pabna city. He is a former organizational secretary of the youth group of BNP in Pabna Sadar upazila. His elder brother, Zakir Hossain Jacky, is currently the secretary of the Nationalist Volunteer Party's Pabna Sadar upazila branch.

The president of the temple committee, Shyamal, stated that at some point on Saturday night, miscreants broke the lock of the Sri Sri Raksha Kalimata temple in the Dilalpur area of the city and vandalized the idols inside. In this incident, on Saturday night, temple committee president Shyamal Kumar Ghosh filed a case against an unidentified individual at the Sadar Police Station. On Sunday morning, administrative officials inspected the temple, and police were deployed for security.
