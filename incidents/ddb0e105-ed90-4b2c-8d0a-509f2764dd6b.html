6. Muslims have socially isolated nearly 200 Hindu houses in Banatara village of Dinajpur, citing the pretext of hurting religious sentiments
Date of Incident:
April 2, 2025
Division: Rangpur
District: Dinajpur
Upazilla: Dinajpur Sadar
Specific Location: Shankarpur, Union No. 8
Details
Muslims have socially isolated about 200 Hindu families in Bantara village of Dinajpur, citing the pretext of hurting religious sentiments. A person from Bantara village named Sabuj Das is said to have hurt the religious sentiments of Muslims through a post on Facebook. Although no evidence has been presented in his favor. In view of this, Muslims have socially isolated about 200 Hindu families in the village. Hindu businesses are being stopped, their shops are being forcibly closed. Hindus' means of earning are being blocked. The police and administration are playing a passive role.
