47. Idol of Hindu Deity Vandalized at Shri Shri Shmoshan Kalimata Temple in Jamalpur’s Sarishabari by radical Islamist ; Outrage Among Local Hindu Community
Date of Incident:
December 12, 2024
Division: Mymensingh
District: Jamalpur
Upazilla: Sarishabari
Police Station: Sarishabari
Details
Alleged radical Islamist have vandalized the idol at the Shri Shri Shmoshan Kalimata Temple in Sarishabari, Jamalpur. The incident has sparked anger and frustration among the local Sanatan (Hindu) community.

The act of vandalism took place late Thursday night (December 12) at the Kamrabad Central Maha Shmoshan Kalimata Temple under the municipal area.
