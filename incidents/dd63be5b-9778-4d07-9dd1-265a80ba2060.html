38. The Sarvajanin Sri Sri Gaur Nitai Temple in Kachua, Chandpur, was robbed on Wednesday night, November 20
Date of Incident:
November 19, 2024
Division: Chattogram
District: Chandpur
Upazilla: Kachua
Details
The Sarvajanin Sri Sri Gaur Nitai Temple adjacent to Kadla Thakur Bari in Kachua, Chandpur was robbed on Wednesday night, November 20. The miscreants broke the lock of the collapsible gate and the lock of the inner door of the temple and stole cash, brass plates, baskets, pots, baskets, bells, lamps and other necessary items for worship from the donation box. The estimated value of the cash and looted items is one lakh taka.

Swapan Kumar Roy, president of the Sri Sri Sarvajanin Gaur Nitai Temple Management Committee, said that he locked the temple at 8 pm on Wednesday and went home. On Thursday morning, he came to the temple and found the gate lock broken.
