305. House and business establishment of Bablu Kumar Saha looted and vandalised
Date of Incident:
August 4, 2024
Division: Khulna
District: Jashore
Upazilla: Bagherpara
Specific Location: Narikelbaria
Details
Bablu Kumar Saha, Chairman of Narikelbaria Union Parishad (UP), said, "The attackers attacked and vandalized my business establishment in Narikelbaria market and looted the goods in the establishment. At least 25 business establishments in the market were vandalized and goods were looted during this time. My house was attacked and vandalized in the evening."
