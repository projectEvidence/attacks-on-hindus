48. Hindu Student Leader Supta Saha Anik Arrested in Thakurgaon for Alleged Derogatory Remarks About Prophet Muhammad in facebook
Date of Incident:
March 4, 2025
Division: Mymensingh
District: Netrokona
Upazilla: Kalmakanda Upazilla
Details
A team of police arrested him from the inter-district bus stand area of the district town on Wednesday afternoon. Superintendent of Police Mirza Sayem Mahmud confirmed the arrest.

The accused has been identified as Supta Saha Anik, son of Sunil Sarkar from Mantala village in Kalmakanda Upazila of Netrokona. Supta Saha was the vice-president of the banned Chhatra League unit of Fazlul Haque Hall at Rajshahi University in 2023.

Superintendent of Police Mirza Sayem Mahmud stated that Supta Saha Anik had recently made derogatory comments about Prophet Muhammad (PBUH) from his Facebook account, which went viral on social media. In response, followers of Islam organized a human chain and demonstration, protesting the incident and demanding his punishment.

Following the backlash, Supta Saha went into hiding. Based on secret information, police later arrested him. Legal proceedings are currently underway.
