181. The house of Sujit Roy, a resident of Gariabari village in Koyra upazila, was attacked
Date of Incident: August 4, 2024
Division: Khulna
District: Khulna
Upazilla: Koyra
Specific Location: Gariabari
Details
The house of Sujit Roy, a resident of Gariabari village in Koyra upazila, was attacked.

Sujit Roy said, "I was getting news of the vandalism of the houses of Awami League leaders in Koyra upazila headquarters since yesterday afternoon. I was already scared. Then in the evening, when I heard that a group of people were coming to attack my house, I took everyone in the house and went to another house a little further away. As I was going, I heard the sound of vandalism in the house. Starting from the roof of the house, everything inside the house was broken."

182. House of Ananta Mukherjee and a temple in the nearby area of ​​Kuakata Municipality in Patuakhali were attacked
Date of Incident: August 4, 2024
Division: Barishal
District: Patuakhali
Upazilla: Kalapara
Details
The residence of Awami League Joint General Secretary Ananta Mukherjee and a temple in the nearby area of ​​Kuakata Municipality in Patuakhali were attacked. The incident took place around 4:30 pm.

Ananta Mukherjee alleged, "After hearing the news of the government's resignation, BNP-Jamaat leaders and activists took out a procession in Kuakata Municipality. The procession came towards my house. At one stage, people from the procession attacked my house. Many of the attackers entered my house. They broke and smashed every thing in the house. Nothing in my house was saved. Then the attackers attacked the temple next to my house. The doors and windows of the temple were damaged."

183. Hindu temple was demolished and looted
Date of Incident: August 4, 2024
Division: Dhaka
District: Shariatpur
Upazilla: Shariatpur Sadar -Palong
Specific Location: Dhanuka Manasa Bari
Details

The Krishna temple was constructed in March. The Krishna idol in the temple was vandalised and destroyed. The attackers had surrounded nearby Hindu houses to vandalise them.
