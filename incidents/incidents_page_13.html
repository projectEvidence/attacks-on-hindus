186. Hindu households at Andharkota and Bollamukh village were attacked and looted by Islamist mob
Date of Incident: August 4, 2024
Division: Khulna
District: Jashore
Upazilla: Bagherpara
Specific Location: Andharkota village, Bollamukh village
Details
Several Hindu houses were attacked and looted at Andharkota village. Most of them were Hindu farmers or day labourer.
Golak Chandra Biswas, one of the many victims, claimed that the assailants had vandalized the family home that day. They also looted gold, 50,000 taka in cash, and a music box. They then took out a motorcycle and a bicycle that were kept on the balcony of the house and placed them on one side of the yard. They then took out blankets, pillows, and clothes from the house, placed them on the motorcycle and bicycle, and set them on fire.

  • • House Vandalism

192. Seven Hindu temples were attacked and set on fire at night on August 5, 2024
Date of Incident: August 4, 2024
Division: Rangpur
District: Thakurgaon
Upazilla: Pirganj
Specific Location: Kosharaniganj Union, No. 2Kasha Raniganj Union
Details
On Monday night, August 5, 2024, 7 Hindu temples in Pirganj were attacked and set on fire. Satirghat Sri Sri Kali Temple, Sri Shiv Temple, Sri Sri Saraswati Temple in Kosharaniganj Union of Pirganj Upazila were attacked and set on fire. Elua Pukur Sri Kali Temple in Saidpur Union, Sri Sri Chandi Thakurani Kali Temple in Shivpur Village, Senua Tilipukur Sri Kali Temple, Sati Dah Cremation Ghat and Temple in No. 2 Kasha Raniganj Union were attacked and vandalized by the Vijaya procession. The iron stove for cremation of the body of the crematorium, tube well, tin ceiling of the temple and even the motor for bathing the body were looted. The place for chanting Krishna's name in the crematorium field was heinously damaged.

  • • Temple Vandalism
