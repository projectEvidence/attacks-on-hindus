183. Extremist Muslims attacked and looted the houses of local Hindu temple committee president and his Hindu neighbours in Lalpur, Natore
Date of Incident:
August 5, 2024
Division: Rajshahi
District: Natore
Upazilla: Lalpur
Details
A group of people attacked the house of Dwipendranath Saha, the president of the Upazila Puja Celebration Council (পূজা উদ্‌যাপন পরিষদ) and the temple, who is also a school teacher, around 7:30 PM yesterday. The attackers broke down one of the gates of his house but failed to break the second one, so they shattered the windows from outside. Following this, they attacked the houses of his brother, Naresh Chandra Saha, and their neighbor, Shimul Sarkar. The attackers broke the doors, entered the houses, and carried out extensive vandalism.

Naresh Chandra claimed that the assailants looted over a hundred thousand taka, four tolas of gold jewelry, a laptop, mobile phones, and sacks of rice from his house.

Earlier, the attackers vandalized eight CCTV cameras at the Jotdaivaki Shiva and Kali Temple. Dwipendranath Saha told Prothom Alo over the phone that his house was located next to the temple. The attackers targeted his house right after attacking the temple. At that time, he informed the Lalpur police station, but an officer there said they would not be able to send police.

187. 1971 war crime witness Ajit Sarkar was murdered on 05 August 2024
Date of Incident:
August 4, 2024
Division: Mymensingh
District: Mymensingh
Upazilla: Phulpur
Specific Location: Balia Union
Details
After the fall of Sheikh Hasina, on August 5, the house of Ajit Sarkar, a witness in a war crimes case, was attacked by the son and relatives of the accused in Domkona village of Balia Union in Phulpur, Mymensingh.
In 2016, Ajit Sarkar Penu testified in court in a war crimes case against Ghiyasuddin, a member of the 1971 Peace Committee.
The attack was led by Ghiyasuddin's son Lavlu on the afternoon of August 5. Ajit Sarkar sought refuge in the house of his neighbor Mortuza Mia to save his life. He was taken from there and dragged around Mahishaunda Bazar and forced to wear a garland made of shoes for being a witness in the case. Then, four or five people, including Lavlu, killed him on the river bank and dumped his body. On the evening of August 8, when the body was found floating downstream, Lavlu's nephew Moizuddin chopped it into pieces and dumped it a few kilometers away.

August 4, 2024
Division: Khulna

Details

We are now unemployed. We are sitting at home. I have become destitute and have sat on the streets."
