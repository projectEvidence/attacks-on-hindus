41. Khulna University student Arnab Kumar Sarkar (26) shot dead by miscreants
Date of Incident:
January 24, 2025
Division: Khulna
District: Khulna
Police Station: Khulna
Specific Location: খুলনা নগরের তেঁতুলতলা মোড় এলাকা (Tetultala intersection area of ​​Khulna city)
Details
A student of Khulna University was shot dead by miscreants. The incident took place at around 9:30 pm on Friday in the Tetultala intersection area of ​​Khulna city.

The deceased student has been identified as Arnab Kumar Sarkar (26). He is the son of Nitish Chandra Sarkar of Abu Ahmed Road, Sonadanga, Khulna.

Arnab is a Master's student of the Business Administration discipline (Department) of Khulna University. The matter was confirmed by Sheikh Mahmudul Hasan, head of the Business Administration discipline.

Eyewitnesses said that Arnab was drinking tea while leaning on a motorcycle in front of a tea shop at Tetultala intersection tonight. At that time, miscreants on several motorcycles came and shot him and fled. The bullet passed through Arnab's head. Local residents rescued him and took him to the private City Medical College Hospital, where doctors declared him dead.
