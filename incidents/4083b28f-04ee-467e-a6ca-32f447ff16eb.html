88. Seven Idols were vandalized at the Sri Sri Gopinath Jiur Akhara temple in ​​Kishoreganj on the eve of Durga Puja
Date of Incident:
October 2, 2024
Division: Dhaka
District: Kishoreganj
Upazilla: Kishoreganj Sadar
Specific Location: Hindupara, Monipurghat road
Details
Seven idols of Durga Puja were vandalized at Sri Sri Jiur Akhra in Kishoreganj district town. The incident took place early Thursday morning. The Gopinath Sangha of Manipurghat Road Hindupara in Batrish area of ​​the city is the organizer of this puja.

Members of the Sangha said that this is the first time they have taken the initiative to hold Durga Puja at this akahra. Some people were on guard until 4 am. Then sometime in the morning, miscreants vandalized a total of seven idols, including the Durga idol, and left.

District Superintendent of Police Mohammad Hasan Chowdhury visited the scene. But no visible action was taken. Only in the incident of idol vandalism in the temple, the officer-in-charge (OC) of Sadar Model Police Station Md. Tariqul Islam has been withdrawn (closed).
