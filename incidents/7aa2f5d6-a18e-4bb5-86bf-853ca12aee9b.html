224. Belongings of businessman Krishna Dutta was looted and vandalised in Dhopadi village
Date of Incident:
August 4, 2024
Division: Khulna
District: Jashore
Upazilla: Abhaynagar
Details
On Monday evening, August 5, a group of people attacked and vandalized the house of businessman Krishna Dutta in Dhopadi village of Abhaynagar upazila, looting gold ornaments and other belongings.
