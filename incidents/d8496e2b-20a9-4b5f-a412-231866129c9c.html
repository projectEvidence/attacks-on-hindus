219. Hindu house was looted before vandalizing the temple
Date of Incident:
August 4, 2024
Division: Barishal
District: Barishal
Upazilla: Bakerganj
Specific Location: Gazitola Bazar, Union No. 06, Faridpur
Details
Miscreants attacked the houses of Bipul Chandra and Binoy Das near Gazitala Bazar in Faridpur Union No. 6 of Bakerganj Upazila of Barisal district and looted their belongings before vandalizing the temple. The attack took place on the afternoon of August 5. Seven people were injured in the incident.
