85. Hindu School girl was abducted to convert to Islam with a prior public announcement at Kurigram's Phulbari
Date of Incident:
September 17, 2024
Division: Rangpur
District: Kurigram
Upazilla: Phulbari
Specific Location: Kashipur Union
Details
A 10th class Hindu student has been abducted with an announcement at Kurigram's Phulbari. The incident took place on Wednesday afternoon, September 18, in Kashipur Union of Phulbari Upazila. On the night of September 2, the miscreants pasted posters on several trees in the neighborhood. It said, 'The girl from this house will be taken away within 7 days and the girl will be converted to Islam.'

The minor Hindu girl was abducted on his way home from a coaching center around 5 pm. The incident was committed by drug dealer Alinur Rahman (32), son of the deceased Abdul Halim of the village, and his accomplices.
