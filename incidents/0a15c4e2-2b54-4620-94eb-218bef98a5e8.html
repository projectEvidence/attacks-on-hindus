262. Cash and gold ornaments were looted from the house of Mani Shankar Roy in Abhaynager, Jashore
Date of Incident:
August 4, 2024
Division: Khulna
District: Jashore
Upazilla: Abhaynagar
Specific Location: Ward No. 06, Paira Union
Details
On the afternoon of August 5, a group of people attacked and vandalized the house of Mani Shankar Roy, a member of Ward No. 6 of Payra Union Parishad (UP) in Abhaynagar Upazila, Barandi village, and took away cash and gold ornaments.
