August 22, 2024
Division: Rajshahi

Details
Rabbi Hossain, a student of a Qawmi Madrasa in Rajshahi, attacked temples in Bagha Upazila of Rajshahi and vandalized idols on the pretext that the people of Bangladesh are suffering from flood water due to the release of water by India.
