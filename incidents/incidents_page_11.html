152. Business owned by Barun Kumar Ghosh looted during broad daylight in Satkhira
Date of Incident:
August 5, 2024
Division: Khulna
District: Satkhira
Upazilla: Kaliganj
Specific Location: Bharashimla Union
Details
On August 6, at around 10:30 am, more than a hundred armed people returning from the BNP procession on the south side of Kankshiali Bridge broke the shutters of “Ma Lakshmi Bhandar” and “Ma Lakshmi Muri Bhandar” and entered. First, they vandalized 10 CCTV cameras inside the mill. Then they looted 500 bags of 50 kg Atap rice, 700 bags of 28 rice of 50 and 25 kg, 1,100 bags of 50 kg coarse rice, 300 bags of miniket, 250 bags (25 kg) Basmati, 300 bags of fresh chopped rice (for frying Muri), 16 bags of gold, 750 bags of hybrid rice, 350 bags of wheat bran, 750 bags of Atap (polished) like kudra, 900 bags of kudra, 750 bags of corn, 200 bags of 28 paddy, 300 bags of coarse paddy, 350 bags of non-basmati paddy, 250 bags of peasant feed, along with the Muri mill, crush mill, shredder mill, rice mill, a tube well, 17 peas, an iron chest, and various other items. The attackers broke open the electric meter and took various items, including wires.

155. Seven Hindu temples were attacked and set on fire at night on August 5, 2024
Date of Incident:
August 4, 2024
Division: Rangpur
District: Thakurgaon
Upazilla: Pirganj
Specific Location: Kosharaniganj Union, No. 2Kasha Raniganj Union
Details
On Monday night, August 5, 2024, 7 Hindu temples in Pirganj were attacked and set on fire. Satirghat Sri Sri Kali Temple, Sri Shiv Temple, Sri Sri Saraswati Temple in Kosharaniganj Union of Pirganj Upazila were attacked and set on fire. Elua Pukur Sri Kali Temple in Saidpur Union, Sri Sri Chandi Thakurani Kali Temple in Shivpur Village, Senua Tilipukur Sri Kali Temple, Sati Dah Cremation Ghat and Temple in No. 2 Kasha Raniganj Union were attacked and vandalized by the Vijaya procession. The iron stove for cremation of the body of the crematorium, tube well, tin ceiling of the temple and even the motor for bathing the body were looted. The place for chanting Krishna's name in the crematorium field was heinously damaged.
Involved Crimes
  • • Arson
  • • Temple Vandalism

156. House of local Hindu temple committee president and his Hindu neighbours were attacked and looted
Date of Incident:
August 4, 2024
Division: Rajshahi
District: Natore
Upazilla: Lalpur
Details
The house of school teacher Dwipendranath Saha, the president of the Upazila Puja Udjapan Parishad and the president of the temple, was attacked. They broke one gate of his house. However, since they could not break the second gate, they broke the window from outside. Then the houses of his brother Naresh Chandra Saha and neighbor Shimul Sarkar were attacked. They broke the door and entered the house and caused extensive vandalism.

Naresh Chandra claimed that miscreants looted lakhs of Taka, gold jewellery, laptop, mobile phone, bags of rice from his house.
