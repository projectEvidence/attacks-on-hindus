152. Hindu Businessman’s Fish Farm Attacked, Looted, and Illegally Occupied in Khulna’s Koyra Upazila
Date of Incident:
November 18, 2024
Division: Khulna
District: Khulna
Upazilla: Koyra
Details
In Baniakhali village, Koyra upazila, Khulna, a Hindu family’s ancestral fish farm has been violently attacked, looted, and occupied by armed miscreants, leaving the owners in fear for their lives. At a press conference held at Khulna Press Club on Tuesday, victim Chandan Roy described the ordeal his family has faced.

The Roy family has lived in this Sundarbans-adjacent village for at least four generations and owns a 100-bigha fish enclosure, of which about 60 bighas are their own property, with the rest used under lease and DC rights. On November 19, 2024, a group of 10–15 men — led by local Awami League supporter Afzal Shikari and BNP supporters Gafur Gazi and Ratan Sardar — forcibly entered the enclosure, cast nets, and looted fish worth Tk 4–5 lakh in a single attack. At the time, the enclosure contained fish worth approximately Tk 25 lakh.

Since then, the Roy family has been denied access to their own fish farm. On February 17, 2025, when the family protested, the attackers launched a violent assault on their home, beating relatives and issuing death threats. Fearing for their safety, the family initially remained silent. However, Chandan Roy said that the threats and illegal occupation have become unbearable.

The Roy family filed a written complaint with Koyra Police Station, naming the attackers. In the presence of both parties, police confirmed the Roy family’s legal ownership and ordered the attackers to stop trespassing and looting. Despite this, the perpetrators — allegedly backed by powerful “invisible forces” — continue to occupy and loot the enclosure.

Chandan Roy has appealed for the immediate return of their fish farm, the arrest of the attackers, and guarantees for the safety of his Hindu family, who remain under constant threat of further communal violence.

164. A gold shop named Dutta Jewelers was robbed in broad daylight by exploding bombs and firing bullets at Maheshwarpasha Kalibari Bazar under Daulatpur police station in Khulna city.
Date of Incident:
October 27, 2024
Division: Khulna
District: Khulna
Upazilla: Phultala
Police Station: Daulatpur
Specific Location: Maheshwarpasha Kalibari Bazar
Details
Amid the nationwide theft and robbery targeting Hindu businessmen, a brutal robbery took place in broad daylight at a gold shop named Dutta Jewelers in Maheshwarpasha Kalibari Bazar under Daulatpur police station in Khulna city. Two people were injured in the incident. Police have arrested the car used by the robbers and the driver. According to eyewitnesses and police sources, an ash-colored private car stopped in front of the Dutta Jewelers shop in Kalibari Bazar at around 1:10 pm on Monday (October 28). Ordinary pedestrians in the area thought that a customer had entered the shop. After 2-1 minutes, hand grenades started hurling from behind and in front of the car. This created a tense and frightening situation in the area.
