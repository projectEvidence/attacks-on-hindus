151. House of Hindu City Mayor was looted during wedding reception
Date of Incident:
August 4, 2024
Division: Barishal
District: Patuakhali
Upazilla: Kalapara
Details
On the afternoon of August 5, the house of Kalapara Municipal Awami League President and Municipal Mayor Bipul Chandra Howlader was attacked. His house was looted. His son's post-wedding ceremony was taking place that day. At the end of the ceremony, a group of miscreants suddenly came and attacked his house. The decorated pandals at the venue were vandalised. At one stage, 10-12 miscreants entered his house. They looted money and gold ornaments. They vandalised the furniture in the house. Some of the miscreants burned the car used by his son.

153. A group of Muslim fundamentalist youth attacked four Hindu temples
Date of Incident:
August 4, 2024
Division: Khulna
District: Jashore
Upazilla: Bagherpara
Specific Location: Rishipalli, Sultannagar
Details
Four temples in Sultannagar Rishipalli, Bagharpara upazila of Jessore have been attacked. The Basanti Temple, Harichand Temple, Kali Temple and Manasa Temple in Rishipalli were attacked on the evening of August 5, after the fall of the government. A group of Muslim fundamentalist youth from Dhalgram Bazar entered their village carrying sticks, machete and other indigenous sharp weapons.
