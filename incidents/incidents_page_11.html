Details

Criminal threaten to tie them inside home and then set fire to their home. Victim Tushar Kanti Mondol named these perpetrators: Ohidul Islam, Majid, Hadi, Khokon Chandra Mondol. Khokon is their neighbour and the others live in Asashuni.
