256. In Dhamrai near Dhaka, House of Meena Rani Das was vandalized along with Durga Temple
Date of Incident:
August 4, 2024
Division: Dhaka
District: Dhaka
Upazilla: Dhamrai
Specific Location: Barobaria, Gangutia Union
Details
On the evening of August 5, a group of 100 to 150 people marched and attacked the house of local Meena Rani Das in the Barbaria area of ​​Gangutia Union in Dhamrai, Dhaka. The attackers vandalized the main iron gate of the house. They broke the entrance gate to the tin house. The attackers broke the glass of three windows of three rooms next to the veranda of the house, a few chairs in front of the Durga temple, and all the glass of two private cars in the garage outside the house.
Involved Crimes
  • • Temple Vandalism
  • • House Vandalism

267. A Durga Temple and an under construction temple was vandalised and looted
Date of Incident:
August 4, 2024
Division: Rangpur
District: Dinajpur
Upazilla: Dinajpur Sadar
Specific Location: Fultola central crematorium ground
Details
The under construction Harishabha Ghar and Durga Temple at the Fultala Central Crematorium Ground in Dinajpur Sadar Upazila have been vandalised on August 5, 2024. During this attack, a group of 150 to 200 Muslim fundamentalists looted and vandalised the temple. People of the Hindu community are being intimidated into not speaking out about this incident. These fundamentalists had previously blocked the
temple work at the Fultala Central Crematorium ground which is situated on the bank of the Punarbhava River in Dinajpur city.
