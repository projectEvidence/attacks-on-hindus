256. Seven Hindu temples were attacked and set on fire at night on August 5, 2024
Date of Incident:
August 4, 2024
Division: Rangpur
District: Thakurgaon
Upazilla: Pirganj
Specific Location: Kosharaniganj Union, No. 2Kasha Raniganj Union
Details
On Monday night, August 5, 2024, 7 Hindu temples in Pirganj were attacked and set on fire. Satirghat Sri Sri Kali Temple, Sri Shiv Temple, Sri Sri Saraswati Temple in Kosharaniganj Union of Pirganj Upazila were attacked and set on fire. Elua Pukur Sri Kali Temple in Saidpur Union, Sri Sri Chandi Thakurani Kali Temple in Shivpur Village, Senua Tilipukur Sri Kali Temple, Sati Dah Cremation Ghat and Temple in No. 2 Kasha Raniganj Union were attacked and vandalized by the Vijaya procession. The iron stove for cremation of the body of the crematorium, tube well, tin ceiling of the temple and even the motor for bathing the body were looted. The place for chanting Krishna's name in the crematorium field was heinously damaged.
Involved Crimes
  • • Arson
  • • Temple Vandalism

269. Computer business of Partha Pratim Nath was looted by armed miscreants in Jessore
Date of Incident:
August 4, 2024
Division: Khulna
District: Jashore
Upazilla: Jessore Sadar
Specific Location: Railway road
Details
A group of miscreants armed with sticks attacked and looted the Nath Computer business of Partha Pratim Nath, son of Jessore Sadar Upazila Awami League President Mohit Kumar Nath, located on the city's railway road. The miscreants looted computers, laptops, ACs, routers and imported components from the shop and three warehouses.

Partha Pratim Nath told Prothom Alo, "I have been running the business for 24 years. The miscreants, carrying the national flag on their heads and sticks in their hands, openly looted goods worth more than four crore taka. When the army was informed, a car finally arrived. But the looting could not be stopped. My father spent a lot of money fighting the national parliament and upazila elections against the boat symbol. We owe crores of taka to the bank. We are now unemployed. We are sitting at home. I have become destitute and have sat on the streets."
