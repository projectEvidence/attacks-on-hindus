269. House of local Hindu temple committee president and his Hindu neighbours were attacked and looted
Date of Incident: August 4, 2024
Division: Rajshahi
District: Natore
Upazilla: Lalpur
Details
The house of school teacher Dwipendranath Saha, the president of the Upazila Puja Udjapan Parishad and the president of the temple, was attacked. They broke one gate of his house. However, since they could not break the second gate, they broke the window from outside. Then the houses of his brother Naresh Chandra Saha and neighbor Shimul Sarkar were attacked. They broke the door and entered the house and caused extensive vandalism.

Naresh Chandra claimed that miscreants looted lakhs of Taka, gold jewellery, laptop, mobile phone, bags of rice from his house.

  • • House Vandalism

270. A Durga Temple and an under construction temple was vandalised and looted
Date of Incident: August 4, 2024
Division: Rangpur
District: Dinajpur
Upazilla: Dinajpur Sadar
Specific Location: Fultola central crematorium ground
Details
The under construction Harishabha Ghar and Durga Temple at the Fultala Central Crematorium Ground in Dinajpur Sadar Upazila have been vandalised on August 5, 2024. During this attack, a group of 150 to 200 Muslim fundamentalists looted and vandalised the temple. People of the Hindu community are being intimidated into not speaking out about this incident. These fundamentalists had previously blocked the
temple work at the Fultala Central Crematorium ground which is situated on the bank of the Punarbhava River in Dinajpur city.
Involved Crimes

  • • Loot
