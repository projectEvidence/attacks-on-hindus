164. Swapan Biswas(60), a cultural activist from Deluti Union in Paikgachha, was murdered on the night of Thursday, August 8.
Date of Incident:
August 7, 2024
Division: Khulna
District: Khulna
Upazilla: Paikgachha
Specific Location: Paschim Para, Deluti Union
Details
Swapan Kumar Biswas (60), a leader of the Sangskritik Jot (Cultural Alliance), was killed by unidentified assailants in Khulna's Paikgachha Upazila on Thursday night.

Locals said that Swapan Kumar Biswas, a resident of Deluti Paschim Para in the upazila, was returning home from the local market around 10 pm on Thursday. On the way, the assailants hit him on the head with a hammer, leaving him seriously injured. Passersby rescued him and took him to the Upazila Health Center, where he died.
