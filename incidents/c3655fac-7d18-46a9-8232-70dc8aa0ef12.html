Robbery at gun point - Gold and cash money was taken from Liton Kundu in Narikelbaria.
Date of Incident: August 4, 2024
Division: Khulna
District: Jashore
Upazilla: Bagherpara
Specific Location: Narikelbaria
Description
On the night of August 5, 10 to 12 people armed with Ramda and country-made weapons entered the house of Liton Kundu in Narikelbaria village by cutting the grill. They held the people hostage at gunpoint and took away three bhoris of gold ornaments and 2,700 taka in cash.
