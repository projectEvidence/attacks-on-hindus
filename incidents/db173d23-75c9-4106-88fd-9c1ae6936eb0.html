147. In Cox's Bazar, extremist vandalized Hindu shops and houses and tried to convert them by forcefully feeding them beef
Date of Incident:
August 30, 2024
Division: Chattogram
District: Cox's Bazar
Upazilla: Cox's Bazar Sadar
Details
In Cox's Bazar, extremist terrorists vandalized Hindu shops and houses and tried to convert them by forcefully feeding them beef. This incident on the night of August 31 came to light the next day, September 1.
