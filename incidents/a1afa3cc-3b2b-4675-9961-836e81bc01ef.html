45. Muslim Extremists attack Moloy Malakar’s Home in Moulvibazar; assaults family members, molests Women, and vandalizes Property
Date of Incident:
December 26, 2024
Division: Sylhet
District: Moulvibazar
Upazilla: Sreemangal
Police Station: Srimangal
Specific Location: Dakhin tuk area, Sindurkhan Union
Details
In Sreemangal upazila of Moulvibazar district, local extremist youths Fayez Mia, Sakib Mia, and Mahfuz Mia carried out an attack on the house of Moloy Malakar. They vandalized valuable furniture, mobile phones, and computers, looted gold ornaments, physically assaulted family members, and committed the heinous and deplorable act of molesting the women of the household.
