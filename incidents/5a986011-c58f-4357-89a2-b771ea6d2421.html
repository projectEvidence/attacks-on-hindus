81. Md. Khurshid Alam and Akkas Ali have been arrested on charges of smuggling rare Vishnu Idol from Birganj, Dinajpur
Date of Incident:
February 4, 2025
Division: Rangpur
District: Dinajpur
Upazilla: Birganj
Specific Location: Union No. 3
Details
A rare 395 kg stone idol of Lord Vishnu was found in the courtyard of a house. The Rapid Action Battalion (RAB) has recovered a 395 kg stone idol of Lord Vishnu during a raid in Birganj, Dinajpur. A young man named Akkas Ali had been arrested during this raid. The idol of Lord Vishnu was found in the courtyard of a house in Arjanahar village of Shatagram Union No. 3, Birganj Upazila on Wednesday (February 5) morning. The arrested Akkas Ali is the son of Abed Ali of Jharbari village of Birganj Upazila. At 3 pm, Senior Assistant Director of the Public Relations Department of Dinajpur RAB-13 Camp Flight Lt. Md. This information was confirmed in a press release sent by Saifullah Naeem. He said, based on a secret tip-off in the morning, RAB members conducted a raid at the house of Md. Khurshid Alam, son of Chhamir Uddin, of Arjanahar village, Shatagram union, Birganj upazila, Dinajpur. During the raid, the huge valuable stone statue buried in the ground under a gourd tree in the courtyard of the house was recovered. The recovered statue is 66 inches long and 30 inches wide and weighs 395 kg. The value of the recovered statue has been determined at 3 crore 95 lakh taka. RAB said that a young man named Akkas Ali has been arrested on charges of being involved in the statue smuggling incident. At that time, a motorcycle of the arrested accused was seized. Sensing the presence of RAB members, the other accused Khurshid Alam fled. During the initial interrogation of the arrested Akkas Ali, he confessed that he was involved in the smuggling of these stone statues. Akkas Ali said that he and the escaped Khurshid Alam had been involved in the statue business for a long time. They had been smuggling statues for a long time, eluding the eyes of the law enforcement agencies. A case was filed against the two accused at Birganj Police Station on Wednesday afternoon under the Special Powers Act on behalf of RAB members. Arrested Akkas Ali had been handed over to that police station. Birganj Police Station Officer-in-Charge (OC) Inspector Md. Abdul Gafur said that arrested Akkas Ali was handed over to the Dinajpur Senior Judicial Magistrate Court in the evening.
