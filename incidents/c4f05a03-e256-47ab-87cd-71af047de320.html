184. Sylhet's Kishori Mohan Girls' High School principal Goura Ghosh was forced to resign
Date of Incident:
August 28, 2024
Division: Sylhet
District: Sylhet
Upazilla: Sylhet Sadar
Details
Sylhet's Kishori Mohan Girls' High School principal Goura Ghosh was forced to resign. He submitted her resignation in a letter to the Additional Divisional Commissioner (General) Sylhet on Thursday (August 29), citing personal reasons.
