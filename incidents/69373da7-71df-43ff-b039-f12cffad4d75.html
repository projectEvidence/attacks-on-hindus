174. Adris Aditya Mondal, the principal of Kopotaksha College in Koyra, resigned under pressure

Division: Khulna
District: Khulna

Details
Adris Aditya Mondal, the principal of Kopotaksha College in Koyra, Khulna, has resigned under pressure. He submitted his resignation to the college president at around 12 noon on Thursday (August 15).
