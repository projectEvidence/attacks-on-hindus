30. Physically Challenged Hindu Girl Raped in Mithapukur, Rangpur; Accused Still at Large
Date of Incident:
March 17, 2025
Division: Rangpur
District: Rangpur
Upazilla: Mithapukur
Specific Location: Boro Mirzapur( বড় মির্জাপুর)
Details
A sixth-grade student was allegedly raped in Mithapukur upazila of Rangpur on her way home after a private school.
The victim's father filed a case with Mithapukur Police Station under the Women and Children Repression Prevention Act.

The incident took place on Tuesday (March 18) at noon. Mithapukur police rushed to the spot and rescued the child and sent her to the Victim Support Center. However, the rapist is absconding till the writing of this report.
According to police and locals, the child's house is in Hindu Para of a village in Bara Mirzapur Union of the upazila.

The child studies in sixth grade at a local high school and is physically challenged. He went to school every day to study privately.
He was returning home after private lessons on Tuesday morning. On the way, a young man (40) who was hiding in a cornfield grabbed the girl and forcibly raped her inside the cornfield.
Later, hearing the girl's cries, the people of the neighborhood realized the matter and informed the police.
On information, Mithapukur police rushed to the spot and rescued the child.

Mithapukur Police Station Officer-in-Charge Abu Bakar Siddique said, "A case has been filed with the police station in this regard. The victim has been rescued and admitted to the hospital for medical examination.
The accused is absconding and a strong operation is underway to arrest him
