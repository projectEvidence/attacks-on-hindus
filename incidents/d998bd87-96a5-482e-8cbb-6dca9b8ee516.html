138. Durga temple was vanalized during night in Barguna
Date of Incident:
September 18, 2024
Division: Barishal
District: Barguna
Upazilla: Barguna Sadar
Specific Location: Galachipa, Fuljhuri union
Details
Several idols were vandalized in an attack on the public Durga temple in Galachipa village of Fuljhuri union in Sadar upazila of Barguna district. The attack took place on the night of Thursday, September 19, 2024.
