153. On the night of Mahalaya on October 1, miscreants attacked a temple in Narail and vandalized several idols, including Durga Ma
Date of Incident:
September 30, 2024
Division: Khulna
District: Narail
Upazilla: Narail-S Upazilla
Specific Location: Mirapara Bazar
Details
The idol was vandalized at the Sarvajanin Sri Sri Durga Temple in Mirapara Bazar, Sadar Upazila, Narail on Tuesday, October 1, 2024.
The idol was vandalized at the Sarvajanin Durga Temple in Mirapara Bazar, Sadar Upazila on Tuesday (October 1) on the night of Mahalaya.

According to local sources, members of the Puja Celebration Committee at the Sarvajanin Durga Temple in Mirapara Bazar, upazila, lit the temple lights on Tuesday, the night of Mahalaya, and left as they pleased. Later in the morning, when the craftsmen came to paint the idol, they found that the idol had been vandalized. Later, when the temple committee members were informed, they informed the police about the matter.
