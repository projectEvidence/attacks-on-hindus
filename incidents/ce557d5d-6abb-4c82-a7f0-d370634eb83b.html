112. Muslim fundamentalist youth vandalized idol at the Govinda Jiur temple in Gauripur, Mymensingh
Date of Incident:
September 25, 2024
Division: Mymensingh
District: Mymensingh
Upazilla: Gauripur
Details
A Muslim fundamentalist youth vandalized an idol at the Govinda Jiur temple in Gauripur municipality in the early hours of September 26. Later, he was granted a disability exemption to escape the case. It is truly ridiculous in Bangladesh that disabled people only attack temples or places of worship belonging to minorities.
