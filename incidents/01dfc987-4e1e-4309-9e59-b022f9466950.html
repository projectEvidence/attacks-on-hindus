27. 10 years old Hindu girl raped by her teacher Mozammel Haque Manik (মোজাম্মেল হক মানিক) in Auliapur, Thakurgaon
Date of Incident:
March 7, 2025
Division: Rangpur
District: Thakurgaon
Upazilla: Thakurgaon Sadar
Specific Location: Auliapur Union, Thakurgaon
Details
10 years old Hindu girl, a student of grade-V, raped by her teacher Mozammel Haque Manik (মোজাম্মেল হক মানিক) in Auliapur, Thakurgaon.
She was admitted into a hospital in a critical condition.

www.prothomalo.com news stated that: according to the police case report, the accused teacher Mozammel used to give private lessons in mathematics and English to 10 to 12 students of the fifth grade of the school during the month of Ramadan. Yesterday, the victim went to the school for private lessons at around 10 am. Mozammel gave them leave at around 11:15 am. But when she went home, Mozammel called the victim back to the school. Later, he raped the child in the school room. He threatened her not to tell anyone about the incident. When she went home, the child told her mother everything.

thedailycampus.com news reported that: before the local people could come forward due to the child's screams, acquaintances of the teacher took her to a private clinic called Sheba Hospital and Diagnostic Center in the district town. When the journalists came to know about their attempt to provide treatment secretly, the hospital authorities quickly transferred the child to the General Hospital.
