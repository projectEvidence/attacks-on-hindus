Hindu households at Andharkota and Bollamukh village were attacked and looted by Islamist mob
Date of Incident: August 4, 2024
Division: Khulna
District: Jashore
Upazilla: Bagherpara
Specific Location: Andharkota village, Bollamukh village
Details
Several Hindu houses were attacked and looted at Andharkota village. Most of them were Hindu farmers or day labourer.
Golak Chandra Biswas, one of the many victims, claimed that the assailants had vandalized the family home that day. They also looted gold, 50,000 taka in cash, and a music box. They then took out a motorcycle and a bicycle that were kept on the balcony of the house and placed them on one side of the yard. They then took out blankets, pillows, and clothes from the house, placed them on the motorcycle and bicycle, and set them on fire.

Twenty Hindu households were attacked and looted by Muslim mob
Date of Incident: August 4, 2024
Division: Khulna
District: Jashore
Upazilla: Bagherpara
Specific Location: Rishipalli, Sultannagar , Dholgram Union
Details
About 90 Hindu families and more than 500 people live in Sultannagar Rishipalli. Most of them are poor. Even so, 12 houses were looted after the attack, said Nalin Chandra Biswas, president of the Harichand Mandir Committee. They took away more than a hundred poultry, a goat, gold coins, cash, rice, pulses, oil, bamboo baskets or kulas, whatever they found in front of them from these houses. The attackers also opened the only deep tube well in the village.

On that day, after the fall of the Sheikh Hasina government, a group of 40 to 50 people attacked the Hindupalli at around 6:30 pm. Each of them had indigenous weapons like ramda, hansua, iron rods, sabals, and sticks.

Durga Temple was vandalised and looted
Date of Incident: August 4, 2024
Division: Barishal
District: Jhalokati
Upazilla: Kathalia
Specific Location: Uttarpar, Satani Bazar, Aurabunia Union
Details
On August 5, 2024, an attack was carried out on the Sri Sri Sarbajanin Durga Temple, north of Satani Bazar, Paschim Chitki village, Aurabunia union, Kanthalia upazila, Jhalokathi. Several hundreds islamist fundamentalists led by local Manjurul Sikder (50) took part in the attack. During this time, they vandalised and looted various items. The people of the minority Hindu community were unable to resist the sudden preplanned attack by the muslim fundamentalist group. They complained to the police. The police arrested the main conspirator, Manjurul Sikder, but the rest were not arrested. They are pressuring the Hindus to withdraw the case.

House of local Hindu temple committee president and his Hindu neighbours were attacked and looted
Date of Incident: August 4, 2024
Division: Rajshahi
District: Natore
Upazilla: Lalpur
Details
The house of school teacher Dwipendranath Saha, the president of the Upazila Puja Udjapan Parishad and the president of the temple, was attacked. They broke one gate of his house. However, since they could not break the second gate, they broke the window from outside. Then the houses of his brother Naresh Chandra Saha and neighbor Shimul Sarkar were attacked. They broke the door and entered the house and caused extensive vandalism.

Naresh Chandra claimed that miscreants looted lakhs of Taka, gold jewellery, laptop, mobile phone, bags of rice from his house.
