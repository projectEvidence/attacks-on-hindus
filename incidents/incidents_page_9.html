1971 war crime witness Ajit Sarkar was murdered on 05 August 2024
Date of Incident: August 4, 2024
Division: Mymensingh
District: Mymensingh
Upazilla: Phulpur
Specific Location: Balia Union
Details
After the fall of Sheikh Hasina, on August 5, the house of Ajit Sarkar, a witness in a war crimes case, was attacked by the son and relatives of the accused in Domkona village of Balia Union in Phulpur, Mymensingh.
In 2016, Ajit Sarkar Penu testified in court in a war crimes case against Ghiyasuddin, a member of the 1971 Peace Committee.
The attack was led by Ghiyasuddin's son Lavlu on the afternoon of August 5. Ajit Sarkar sought refuge in the house of his neighbor Mortuza Mia to save his life. He was taken from there and dragged around Mahishaunda Bazar and forced to wear a garland made of shoes for being a witness in the case. Then, four or five people, including Lavlu, killed him on the river bank and dumped his body. On the evening of August 8, when the body was found floating downstream, Lavlu's nephew Moizuddin chopped it into pieces and dumped it a few kilometers away.

  • • Loot
  • • House Vandalism

Miscreants looted all the belongings of Lakshmi Rani Pal of Bejpara, Jessore city
Date of Incident: August 4, 2024
Division: Khulna
District: Jashore
Upazilla: Jessore Sadar
Specific Location: Banani Road, Bejpara
Details
Lakshmi Rani Pal lives with her parents on the ground floor of Paresh Basu's house on Banani Road in Bejpara, Jessore town. Her only source of income is a sewing machine. After Sheikh Hasina's resignation, miscreants broke into the main gate of the house at around 9:15 pm last Monday and looted Lakshmi Rani's sewing machine, cooking stove, gas cylinder and 10 baht of gold jewelry belonging to her brother's newly married wife kept in the cupboard. Lakshmi Rani's family members have been leaving the area fearing for their lives. They have taken shelter in a relative's house in another area.
