121. The house of Narad Biswas was looted and attacked on August 7 in Lamaparkul village of Ichakals Union
Date of Incident:
August 6, 2024
Division: Sylhet
District: Sylhet
Upazilla: Companiganj
Specific Location: Lamaparkul, Ichakals union
Details
The attack took place at 2 pm on Wednesday (August 7) ​​at the house of Narad Biswas, son of the deceased Devendra Biswas, of Lamaparkul village in Ichakals union of the upazila. According to local sources, Narad Biswas, a prominent stone merchant of Lamaparkul village, is currently in jail in a case. Only his wife Sandhya Biswas lived in the house. Last Wednesday at 2 pm, a group of miscreants led by Sayeed Ahmed, president of the Ichakals union branch of Bangladesh Islami Chhatra Shibir, attacked Narad Biswas's house and vandalized it extensively. Fearing for his life, Sandhya Biswas fled through the back door of the house and took shelter in a neighboring house. At that time, the attackers broke the door of the house and entered, vandalized the furniture and looted gold ornaments and cash worth 43,000 taka from the cupboard. When the neighbors came out at the sound of the vandalism, the terrorists ran away. The neighbors said that Narad Biswas is a very peace-loving person. He is not involved in any kind of politics.

132. Twenty Hindu households were attacked and looted by Muslim mob
Date of Incident:
August 4, 2024
Division: Khulna
District: Jashore
Upazilla: Bagherpara
Specific Location: Rishipalli, Sultannagar , Dholgram Union
Details
About 90 Hindu families and more than 500 people live in Sultannagar Rishipalli. Most of them are poor. Even so, 12 houses were looted after the attack, said Nalin Chandra Biswas, president of the Harichand Mandir Committee. They took away more than a hundred poultry, a goat, gold coins, cash, rice, pulses, oil, bamboo baskets or kulas, whatever they found in front of them from these houses. The attackers also opened the only deep tube well in the village.

On that day, after the fall of the Sheikh Hasina government, a group of 40 to 50 people attacked the Hindupalli at around 6:30 pm. Each of them had indigenous weapons like ramda, hansua, iron rods, sabals, and sticks.

135. Durga Temple was vandalised and looted
Date of Incident:
August 4, 2024
Division: Barishal
District: Jhalokati
Upazilla: Kathalia
Specific Location: Uttarpar, Satani Bazar, Aurabunia Union
Details
On August 5, 2024, an attack was carried out on the Sri Sri Sarbajanin Durga Temple, north of Satani Bazar, Paschim Chitki village, Aurabunia union, Kanthalia upazila, Jhalokathi. Several hundreds islamist fundamentalists led by local Manjurul Sikder (50) took part in the attack. During this time, they vandalised and looted various items. The people of the minority Hindu community were unable to resist the sudden preplanned attack by the muslim fundamentalist group. They complained to the police. The police arrested the main conspirator, Manjurul Sikder, but the rest were not arrested. They are pressuring the Hindus to withdraw the case.
