Hindu households at Andharkota and Bollamukh village were attacked and looted by Islamist mob
Date of Incident: August 4, 2024
Division: Khulna
District: Jashore
Upazilla: Bagherpara
Specific Location: Andharkota village, Bollamukh village
Details
Several Hindu houses were attacked and looted at Andharkota village. Most of them were Hindu farmers or day labourer.
Golak Chandra Biswas, one of the many victims, claimed that the assailants had vandalized the family home that day. They also looted gold, 50,000 taka in cash, and a music box. They then took out a motorcycle and a bicycle that were kept on the balcony of the house and placed them on one side of the yard. They then took out blankets, pillows, and clothes from the house, placed them on the motorcycle and bicycle, and set them on fire.

  • • Arson
  • • House Vandalism

In Dhamrai near Dhaka, House of Meena Rani Das was vandalized along with Durga Temple
Date of Incident: August 4, 2024
Division: Dhaka
District: Dhaka
Upazilla: Dhamrai
Specific Location: Barobaria, Gangutia Union
Details
On the evening of August 5, a group of 100 to 150 people marched and attacked the house of local Meena Rani Das in the Barbaria area of ​​Gangutia Union in Dhamrai, Dhaka. The attackers vandalized the main iron gate of the house. They broke the entrance gate to the tin house. The attackers broke the glass of three windows of three rooms next to the veranda of the house, a few chairs in front of the Durga temple, and all the glass of two private cars in the garage outside the house.

Miscreants looted all the belongings of Lakshmi Rani Pal of Bejpara, Jessore city
Date of Incident: August 4, 2024
Division: Khulna
District: Jashore
Upazilla: Jessore Sadar
Specific Location: Banani Road, Bejpara
Details
Lakshmi Rani Pal lives with her parents on the ground floor of Paresh Basu's house on Banani Road in Bejpara, Jessore town. Her only source of income is a sewing machine. After Sheikh Hasina's resignation, miscreants broke into the main gate of the house at around 9:15 pm last Monday and looted Lakshmi Rani's sewing machine, cooking stove, gas cylinder and 10 baht of gold jewelry belonging to her brother's newly married wife kept in the cupboard. Lakshmi Rani's family members have been leaving the area fearing for their lives. They have taken shelter in a relative's house in another area.
