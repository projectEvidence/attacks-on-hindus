38. Widespread extortion and attacks on Minorities in Ishwardi, Pabna
Date of Incident:
March 11, 2025
Division: Rajshahi
District: Pabna
Upazilla: Ishwardi
Specific Location: Dashuria Bazar
Details
Hindu devotees have protested in Pabna's Ishwardi against the attack on minorities and extortion. They staged the hour-long demonstration at Dashuria Bazar area of the upazila on Wednesday afternoon. Later, the main road of Dashuria intersection ended at Hindu Para.

Earlier on Tuesday evening, some miscreants came to their homes and business establishments and demanded extortion. Later, when Hindus refused to pay the extortion money, were beaten by the miscreants. The Hindu community lodged a written complaint with Ishwardi Police Station in this regard.

Gaur Chandra Shen, Ratan Shaha, Pradib Kuman, Komal Pal, Asutash Pal, Madhok Kundu, Ashu Kundu, Jadav Kundu, Prodib Karmakar and many others were present on the occasion.
