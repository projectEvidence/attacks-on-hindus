87. The idol of Sri Sri Radha Gobind Mandir at Sahapara Tangail was attacked in the night and vandalized.
Date of Incident:
September 17, 2024
Division: Dhaka
District: Tangail
Upazilla: Basail
Specific Location: Sahapara , Karatipara
Details
The idol of Sri Sri Radha Gobind Mandir at Sahapara in Karatipara of Basail Upazila of Tangail was attacked in the night hours and vandalized. The administration has inspected the site after the incident. However no culprit was arrested.
Durga Puja has been held in the temple courtyard for the past 19 years. This is the first time that the idol of the temple has been vandalized.
