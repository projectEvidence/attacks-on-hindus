220. ISKCON temple in Meherpur was burnt to ashes by a muslim fundamentalist group
Date of Incident:
August 5, 2024
Division: Khulna
District: Meherpur
Upazilla: Meherpur-S
Details
A fundamentalist group looted and set fire to the ISKCON temple located in Hotel Bazar in Meherpur city. Maharaj Sumahan Mukunda Das Brahmachari, the head of the temple and propaganda unit, said that nothing was left of the temple, everything was burnt. Even the idol of Jagannath Dev was damaged.

  • • Temple Vandalism
  • • Loot
