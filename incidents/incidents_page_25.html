364. Hindu temple was demolished and looted
Date of Incident:
August 4, 2024
Division: Dhaka
District: Shariatpur
Upazilla: Shariatpur Sadar -Palong
Specific Location: Dhanuka Manasa Bari
Details
A temple at Dhanuka Mansa Bari in Shariatpur district town has been demolished. On the night of August 5, a Muslim mob demolished the temple and looted the furniture. The Krishna temple was constructed in March. The Krishna idol in the temple was vandalised and destroyed. The attackers had surrounded nearby Hindu houses to vandalise them. Later in the night, when the army members went there, the miscreants fled.

  • • Temple Vandalism
  • • Loot

371. House of Hindu City Mayor was looted during wedding reception
Date of Incident:
August 4, 2024
Division: Barishal
District: Patuakhali
Upazilla: Kalapara
Details
On the afternoon of August 5, the house of Kalapara Municipal Awami League President and Municipal Mayor Bipul Chandra Howlader was attacked. His house was looted. His son's post-wedding ceremony was taking place that day. At the end of the ceremony, a group of miscreants suddenly came and attacked his house. The decorated pandals at the venue were vandalised. At one stage, 10-12 miscreants entered his house. They looted money and gold ornaments. They vandalised the furniture in the house. Some of the miscreants burned the car used by his son.
