137. An extortion gang threatened and demanded money due to the Hindu identity of a businesswoman in Sylhet
Date of Incident:
August 31, 2024
Division: Sylhet
District: Sylhet
Upazilla: Sylhet Sadar
Specific Location: Mirjajangal Tanim Tower, Jamtola road
Details
A visa consultancy firm in Sylhet has been a victim of extortion. An extortion gang threatened the director of the firm, saying that the business would not be able to run unless regular extortion was received.

The incident took place on September 1 at Mirjajangal Tanim Tower on Jamtala Road. In this incident, the director of the firm, Bipasha Chowdhury, filed a general diary with the Sylhet Kotwali Police Station on September 10, seeking legal intervention. Its number is 797.

In the diary, she mentioned that on the day of the incident, at approximately 3 pm, 4/5 unknown people entered my firm named SBS Migration and demanded a cash extortion of 50,000 taka from me. When I expressed my inability to accept their offer, the extortion gang called me a minority and warned me that I would have to pay an extortion every month to run the business regularly. Otherwise, they threatened to close the firm and flee.
