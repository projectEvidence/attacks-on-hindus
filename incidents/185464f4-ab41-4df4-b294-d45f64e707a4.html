380. Kanti Lal Nath Beaten to Death Over Land Dispute in Patiya, Chattogram
Date of Incident:
July 13, 2001
Division: Chattogram
District: Chattogram
Upazilla: Patiya
Specific Location: South Vurshi
Details
Kanti Lal Nath (40), a service holder, was brutally beaten to death by unidentified assailants in the South Bhurshi area of Patiya on Saturday (14.07.2001) around 8:00 PM.

According to the Patiya correspondent, police suspect the murder was linked to a land-related dispute. The victim was reportedly returning home from his workplace in Chattogram city when the attackers assaulted him. He succumbed to his injuries on the way to the hospital.
