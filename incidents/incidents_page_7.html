Rich Hindu businessman house and business establishment was looted and vandalized
Date of Incident: August 4, 2024
Division: Rangpur
District: Gaibandha
Upazilla: Palashbari
Specific Location: Gopinathpur (Jundah) in Barisal Union
Details
On the night of August 5, the home and business of businessman Shri Gopal Chandra Roy and his younger brother Gokul Chandra Roy of Palashbari Upazila were attacked. The houses were vandalized and looted. Five tractors of the brick kiln, a joint business of the two brothers, were burned. Businessman Shri Gopal Chandra Roy said that on the night of the incident, 30 to 35 youths wearing masks attacked the home with Ram Da, sticks and sticks. To save their lives, he and his family took shelter in the darkness next to the house.

House and Business establishment of Bikash Pal was looted and vandalized
Date of Incident: August 4, 2024
Division: Khulna
District: Jashore
Upazilla: Keshabpur
Specific Location: Pajiya Bazar
Details
On Monday, August 5, at around 3:30 pm, the house of rice trader Bikash Chandra Pal in Pajia Bazar was attacked and vandalized. At the same time, his business establishment was also looted. 200 sacks of rice, 250 sacks of flour, 450 sacks of khail and 250 sacks of husk and nine lakh taka were looted from the establishment. On the same day, a man named Ripon from the Notunhat area of ​​the upazila demanded 2 lakh taka from him. Ripon also took 50 thousand taka from him.
