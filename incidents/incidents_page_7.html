91. A Hindu woman named Chumki Rani Das (50) was murdered after breaking into her home and ornaments she had on were taken away in the district town of Khagrachari.
Date of Incident:
December 4, 2024
Division: Chattogram
District: Khagrachari

Specific Location: Aparna Chowdhury Para (অপর্ণা চৌধুরী পাড়া) area of ​​Khagrachhari city
Details
A woman named Chumki Rani Das (50) was murdered after entering her house in the district town of Khagrachhari. The incident took place in the Aparna Chowdhury Para area of ​​the city on Thursday night. The deceased Chumki Das is the wife of Tapan Kanti Das of the same area. Police have arrested a young man named Md. Russel (22) as a suspect on suspicion of involvement in the incident.

It's noted on https://bengali.abplive.com/news/bangladesh-hindu-student-murdered-in-khulna-bangladesh-panic-breaks-out-1117110 (pdf saved as https://drive.google.com/file/d/11PN2tWlHllhI0zS8jHFrC5qm5X4BcF7i/view?usp=drive_link) the deceased woman's son Pranto Das is one of the conveners of the Sanatan Jagran Mancha in Khagrachari whereas some local influential person was trying to play down the communal aspect of the incident.

It is learned that miscreants entered a house in the Aparna Chowdhury Para area of ​​Khagrachhari city at 8 pm on Thursday and brutally murdered Chumki Das. At that time, the miscreants took away the victim's necklace and earrings.

Initial investigation revealed that neighboring CNG driver Md. Russel borrowed 90 thousand taka from Chumki Das's son, Pranto Das, some time ago. Russel had a dispute with Pranto when he did not return the money. Russel went to Chumki Das's house to talk to him before the murder.

A case has been registered under Section 302/380/34 of the Penal Code in Khagrachhari Sadar Police Station. Case number 03, date 06/12/2024.

After the incident, Khagrachhari Superintendent of Police Arefin Jewel, Additional Superintendent of Police of Sadar Circle Taufiqul Alam, Officer-in-Charge (OC) of Sadar Police Station Abdul Baten Mridha and other senior officials of the district police visited the scene.

Khagrachhari Sadar Circle Additional Superintendent of Police Taufiqul Alam said, "A stone (used in a baton) was found at the scene. With which the head was hit. Chumki Rani's ear was torn when the kidnapper took off her earrings."

Khagrachhari Sadar Police Station Officer-in-Charge (OC) Abdul Baten Mridha said, "Chumki Rani was brought to Sadar Hospital in a seriously injured condition and the doctor on duty declared her dead." "An investigation is underway to unravel the mystery of the murder. Details will be announced after the investigation is completed."

92. Radical Muslims Attack Hindu Community in Sunamganj: Homes, Shops, and Loknath Temple Vandalized
Date of Incident:
December 2, 2024
Division: Sylhet
District: Sunamganj
Police Station: Doarabazar
Specific Location: Manglargaon (মংলারগাও)
Details
Four people have been arrested for vandalizing and damaging homes, shops and the local Loknath temple of the Hindu community in the Doarabazar area of ​​Sunamganj, the Chief Advisor's Press Wing said. The police headquarter said that on December 3, Akash Das (20) in Monglargaon village of Doarabazar police station in Sunamganj district allegedly posted a post mocking Islam from his Facebook ID. Although the alleged post was deleted, the screenshot sparked tension among the people of the area. Upon receiving the news, the Doarabazar police station arrested Akash Das on the day of the incident, and at that time, local people tried to snatch Akash Das from the police. Later, for the sake of safety, he was taken to Sadar police station instead of Doarabazar police station. Police said that on that day, the mob vandalised and damaged the homes, shops and local Loknath temple of the Hindu community. The situation was brought under control in the presence of the district SP, DC, army and police. The police conducted an intensive investigation to identify and arrest the people involved in the incident. The police identified the accused involved in the incident and filed a case against 150 to 170 unknown people, including 12 people. Alim Hossain, (19), Sultan Ahmed Raju (20), Imran Hossain (31) and Shajahan Hossain (20) were arrested in this incident. Like previous incidents, police did not do any investigation or verification prior to the arrest of Akash das. Police just arrested him under the demand of Muslim population.

Upazilla: Shibchar

Specific Location: Bablatala area
Details

Local residents believe that despite owning the land legally, the family is being subjected to such oppression simply because they belong to a minority community.

Details

The brief statement of the case states that there is a temple called 'Shri Sri Hare Krishna Namahatta Sangha' in Ranibazar Haludpatti. The plaintiff is a member of the Sangha. Last Wednesday at around 8:30 am, some members including Pranay went to clean the temple. They returned after finishing their work at around 9:30 am. On Friday, around 5 pm, after hearing about the attack, they went to the temple and found that the door and lock were broken. When they went inside, they found that six pictures of the Lord, conch, achamanpatra, mridangam, sofa, chair, table, clock and Thakur's throne kept in the temple had been vandalized. Everything was lying on the ground. Bells, gongs, cymbals, and drums kept in a box at the temple were stolen. The complaint mentions a loss of 4 million taka.

Plaintiff Pranay Karmakar told Prothom Alo that ISKCON has many branch associations. Sri Sri Hare Krishna Namahatta Sangha is the propaganda branch of ISKCON. The Bhairav ​​Sangha was established in 2009. Temple activities are also conducted here. Regular prayers are held on Sundays. Bhairav ​​activities are being conducted from the ISKCON Narsingdi branch.

After investigation, it was learned that the activities of the Sangha are conducted in a one-story building of a businessman in Ranibazar Haludpatti. Prayers and kirtan are held in the Sangha every Sunday. Apart from that, the Sangha office is not open much on other days. It was closed yesterday as well. Around 5 pm, some people came there and started shouting slogans like 'ISKCON has no place in Bhairav'. Almost all of them had sticks in their hands. Later, they vandalized and left. Most of the attackers were young people. There were 15 to 20 people in number.

105. Police crackdown in Kotalipara, Gopalganj on Hindus demanding release of Chinmoy Krishna Das from false case
Date of Incident:
November 25, 2024
Division: Dhaka
District: Gopalganj
Upazilla: Kotalipara
Specific Location: ভাঙ্গারহাট বাজার
Details
After the bail plea of Chinmoy Krishna Das was denied, a procession was held in Kotalipara, Gopalganj. Three protesters were detained. Earlier in the afternoon, protest rally by thousands of Hindus marched from different areas of Kotalipara upazila and gathered at Bhangarhat Bazaar. At that time, protesters clashed with the Kotalipara Police Station.

পুলিশ এ সময় উপজেলার ভাঙ্গারহাট গ্রামের বিশ্বেশর হালদারের ছেলে অসীম হালদার (৩৩), কলাবাড়ি গ্রামের জতীন অধিকারীর ছেলে দিলীপ অধিকারী (৩৮), হিজলবাড়ী গ্রামের শচীন রোনাহা বাড়ৈর ছেলে তুষার বাড়ৈ (৩৭) নামের ৩ বিক্ষোভকারীকে আটক করে থানায় নিয়ে যায়।

The police arrested three protesters, Asim Halder (33), son of Vishweshar Halder of Bhangarhat village in the upazila, Dilip Adhikari (38), son of Jatin Adhikari of Kalabari village, and Tushar Baroi (37), son of Sachin Ronaha Baroi of Hijalbari village, and took them to the police station.
