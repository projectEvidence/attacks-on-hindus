A Durga Temple and an under construction temple was vandalised and looted
Date of Incident: August 4, 2024
Division: Rangpur
District: Dinajpur
Upazilla: Dinajpur Sadar
Specific Location: Fultola central crematorium ground
Description: The under construction Harishabha Ghar and Durga Temple at the Fultala Central Crematorium Ground in Dinajpur Sadar Upazila have been vandalised on August 5, 2024. During this attack, a group of 150 to 200 Muslim fundamentalists looted and vandalised the temple. People of the Hindu community are being intimidated into not speaking out about this incident. These fundamentalists had previously blocked the temple work at the Fultala Central Crematorium ground which is situated on the bank of the Punarbhava River in Dinajpur city.
Involved Crimes
  1. Temple Vandalism
  2. Loot

House of Hindu City Mayor was looted during wedding reception
Date of Incident: August 4, 2024
Division: Barishal
District: Patuakhali
Upazilla: Kalapara
Description: On the afternoon of August 5, the house of Kalapara Municipal Awami League President and Municipal Mayor Bipul Chandra Howlader was attacked. His house was looted. His son's post-wedding ceremony was taking place that day. At the end of the ceremony, a group of miscreants suddenly came and attacked his house. The decorated pandals at the venue were vandalised. At one stage, 10-12 miscreants entered his house. They looted money and gold ornaments. They vandalised the furniture in the house. Some of the miscreants burned the car used by his son.
