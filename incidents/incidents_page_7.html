97. The 'Syed Brothers' of Barisal have always been reckless in seizing the property of Hindus, including the elderly Malti Rani.
Date of Incident:
October 6, 2024
Division: Barishal
District: Barishal
Upazilla: Barisal Sadar
Details
On August 5, the councilor brothers of the BNP era became influential again after the fall of the Sheikh Hasina government.

They have tried to evict a helpless minority woman from her house by occupying land with a fake decree. Apart from this, there are allegations against this family of grabbing business establishments, shops, and land of many people in the name of justice and arbitration. They are especially reckless in grabbing the property of the minority community. They are the five children of the deceased Syed Chunnu Mia of Kathpatti area of ​​Ward No. 9 of Barisal. There are also allegations of grabbing against Chunnu Mia. Among them, the eldest son Syed Jamal Hossain Noman BNP and Syed Humayun Kabir Linku are associated with Awami politics.


In 2022, two-thirds of the land worth 5 crore taka of a 75-year-old woman named Malti Rani Nath was seized. Then, on August 6, the day after the fall of the Sheikh Hasina government, an attack was launched to seize the remaining part of the land under the leadership of Jamal Hossain Noman. The Debnath clothing store located there was attacked and looted. The money was looted.

Malti Rani's grandson Shuvo Debnath told Kalbela, since 2013, they have attacked our land at least 8 to 10 times by showing a fake degree. The last attack was on August 6 at around 9:30 pm and the shop was vandalized. They looted goods worth 4 lakh taka and 1.5 lakh taka inside. In this incident, Debashish Debnath, the grandson of Malti Rani Nath, filed a case with the Kotwali Model Police Station, and Jamal Hossain Noman was arrested and sent to jail by the court.

105. Arun Das a victim of land grabbing from Awami League leader Hasnat Abdullah, Hasnat grabbed a lot of land in the 16 years of the previous Awami League government by using influence and fear
Date of Incident:
September 25, 2024
Division: Barishal
District: Barishal
Upazilla: Gaurnadi
Specific Location: Dakhsin Palardi
Details
It is alleged that Hasanat Abdullah grabbed a lot of land in the 16 years of the previous Awami League government by using influence and fear, sometimes at a nominal price, sometimes by force. Abul Hasanat Abdullah is the President of Barisal District Awami League.

There are also allegations against Hasanat of grabbing land from minority communities. Arun Das (72), the owner of the traditional 'The Laxman Das Circus', alleged that Hasanat Abdullah had his eye on the land of their under-construction 'Leela Cinema Hall' in Dakshin Palradi Mouza, next to the Dhaka-Barisal highway. He put pressure on him to get the land deed. When he did not agree, Awami League leader Abul Hossain Mollah and local Selim Hawladar offered to sell the land, but he refused.

Later, Hasanat Abdullah used the administration to stop their circus show. This caused them financial losses. Since the circus was closed for a long time, it became difficult to bear the cost of food for the artists and animals. Then he was forced to deed 34 percent of the land, including the building under construction. Hasanat Abdullah gave only 3.6 million taka for the property worth about 1.5 crore taka. Arun Das lamented, "It didn't end here, Hasanat Abdullah again pressured me to write down the dighi on 96 percent of my land. But when I refused, Lutfar Rahman, the general secretary of Gournadi Upazila Chhatra League, threatened to kill me. After that, I stopped going to Gournadi out of fear for my life."
