100. A group of Muslim fundamentalist youth attacked four Hindu temples
Date of Incident: August 4, 2024
Division: Khulna
District: Jashore
Upazilla: Bagherpara
Specific Location: Rishipalli, Sultannagar
Details
Four temples in Sultannagar Rishipalli, Bagharpara upazila of Jessore have been attacked. The Basanti Temple, Harichand Temple, Kali Temple and Manasa Temple in Rishipalli were attacked on the evening of August 5, after the fall of the government. A group of Muslim fundamentalist youth from Dhalgram Bazar entered their village carrying sticks, machete and other indigenous sharp weapons.

105. A Durga Temple and an under construction temple was vandalised and looted
Date of Incident: August 4, 2024
Division: Rangpur
District: Dinajpur
Upazilla: Dinajpur Sadar
Specific Location: Fultola central crematorium ground
Details
The under construction Harishabha Ghar and Durga Temple at the Fultala Central Crematorium Ground in Dinajpur Sadar Upazila have been vandalised on August 5, 2024. During this attack, a group of 150 to 200 Muslim fundamentalists looted and vandalised the temple. People of the Hindu community are being intimidated into not speaking out about this incident. These fundamentalists had previously blocked the
temple work at the Fultala Central Crematorium ground which is situated on the bank of the Punarbhava River in Dinajpur city.
