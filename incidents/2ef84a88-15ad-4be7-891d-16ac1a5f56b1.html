124. Hindus were driven out of hotels and not allowed to stay in Hotels on the instructions of Police Super ahead of peaceful protest assembly
Date of Incident:
November 21, 2024
Division: Rangpur
District: Rangpur
Upazilla: Kaunia
Details
While addressing the assembly of Hindus in Rangpur, Mr. Chinmoy Krishna Das describes ordeals. Hindus were not allowed to stay in Hotels and were driven out of hotels on the instructions of the superintendent of Police.

While addressing the assembly of Hindus in Rangpur, Mr. Chinmoy Krishna Das pointed out that Hindus were not allowed to stay in Hotels and were driven out of hotels on the instructions of Police Super (Ref: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=kzTAznGw8RQ)

Moreover, Sanatan Jagran Mancha Rangpur coordinator Dipankar Bhattacharya told Prothom Alo that they have sought permission from the district administration to hold a rally at the district school ground. But they have not been given permission to use the district school ground. They have been asked to hold the rally at Mahiganj Degree College ground, four kilometers from Rangpur city.
