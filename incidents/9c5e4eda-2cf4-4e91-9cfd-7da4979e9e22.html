146. Madrasa student was caught red handed while vandalizing temple in Bagha Municipality
Date of Incident:
August 22, 2024
Division: Rajshahi
District: Rajshahi
Upazilla: Bagha
Specific Location: Pundaripara, Kaligram
Details
Rabbi Hossain, a student of a Qawmi Madrasa in Rajshahi, attacked temples in Bagha Upazila of Rajshahi and vandalized idols on the pretext that the people of Bangladesh are suffering from flood water due to the release of water by India. On Friday, August 23, 2024, attack was carried out by breaking the locks of the Pundaripara temple in Kaligram of Bagha Municipality and vandalizing various temple equipment and idols. During the attack, he was caught red-handed and handed over to the army by peace-loving Hindu minority community members.
