55. Idols were broken in a two hundreds year old Kali temple in Mirzapur, Tangail
Date of Incident:
November 29, 2024
Division: Dhaka
District: Tangail
Upazilla: Mirzapur
Specific Location: Durpasa, Banail Union
Details
An idol of the nearly two hundred year old Deepavali Kali temple was vandalized in Durpasa village of Banail Union in Mirzapur Upazila of Tangail. In the dead of night, miscreants broke the idol of the temple and dumped it in four places. The incident took place late on Friday night, November 30, 2024.
