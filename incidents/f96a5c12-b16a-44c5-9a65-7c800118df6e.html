28. Settler Muslims set fire to the house of a tribal indigenous person in Rangamati on February 20, 2025
Date of Incident:
February 20, 2025
Division: Chattogram
District: Rangamati
Upazilla: Nannerchar
Specific Location: Bagachhari Kailash Para, Ward No. 1 of Burighat Union
Details
Settler Muslims set fire to the house of a tribal indigenous person named Montu Chakma. This happened in Bagachhari Kailash Para, Ward No. 1 of Burighat Union in Naniarchar Upazila of Rangamati on February 20, 2025.
