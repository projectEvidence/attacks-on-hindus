301. A group of people with covered face, robbed half a dozen Hindu house in Shalborat
Date of Incident:
August 4, 2024
Division: Khulna
District: Jashore
Upazilla: Bagherpara
Specific Location: Shalborat
Details
On the night of August 5, 2024, a group of 10 to 12 people attacked the Hindu neighborhood of Shalborat village in the upazila. 65 Hindu families live in this neighborhood. They went to five to six houses with black cloths tied around their faces and ramadas in their hands. The people of the neighborhood said that out of fear, everyone in the neighborhood somehow spent the night in the neighboring garden. On Tuesday morning, the men left the neighborhood and went elsewhere.

302. Rich Hindu businessman house and business establishment was looted and vandalized
Date of Incident:
August 4, 2024
Division: Rangpur
District: Gaibandha
Upazilla: Palashbari
Specific Location: Gopinathpur (Jundah) in Barisal Union
Details
On the night of August 5, the home and business of businessman Shri Gopal Chandra Roy and his younger brother Gokul Chandra Roy of Palashbari Upazila were attacked. The houses were vandalized and looted. Five tractors of the brick kiln, a joint business of the two brothers, were burned. Businessman Shri Gopal Chandra Roy said that on the night of the incident, 30 to 35 youths wearing masks attacked the home with Ram Da, sticks and sticks. To save their lives, he and his family took shelter in the darkness next to the house.
