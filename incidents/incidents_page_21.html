304. House of Hindu City Mayor was looted during wedding reception
Date of Incident:
August 4, 2024
Division: Barishal
District: Patuakhali
Upazilla: Kalapara
Details
On the afternoon of August 5, the house of Kalapara Municipal Awami League President and Municipal Mayor Bipul Chandra Howlader was attacked. His house was looted. His son's post-wedding ceremony was taking place that day. At the end of the ceremony, a group of miscreants suddenly came and attacked his house. The decorated pandals at the venue were vandalised. At one stage, 10-12 miscreants entered his house. They looted money and gold ornaments. They vandalised the furniture in the house. Some of the miscreants burned the car used by his son.

305. Durga Temple was vandalised and looted
Date of Incident:
August 4, 2024
Division: Barishal
District: Jhalokati
Upazilla: Kathalia
Specific Location: Uttarpar, Satani Bazar, Aurabunia Union
Details
On August 5, 2024, an attack was carried out on the Sri Sri Sarbajanin Durga Temple, north of Satani Bazar, Paschim Chitki village, Aurabunia union, Kanthalia upazila, Jhalokathi. Several hundreds islamist fundamentalists led by local Manjurul Sikder (50) took part in the attack. During this time, they vandalised and looted various items. The people of the minority Hindu community were unable to resist the sudden preplanned attack by the muslim fundamentalist group. They complained to the police. The police arrested the main conspirator, Manjurul Sikder, but the rest were not arrested. They are pressuring the Hindus to withdraw the case.
Involved Crimes

  • • Loot
