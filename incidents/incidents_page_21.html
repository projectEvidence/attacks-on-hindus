Date of Incident:
August 4, 2024
Division: Khulna
District: Jashore
Upazilla: Jessore Sadar
Specific Location: Banani Road, Bejpara
Details
Lakshmi Rani Pal lives with her parents on the ground floor of Paresh Basu's house on Banani Road in Bejpara, Jessore town. Her only source of income is a sewing machine. After Sheikh Hasina's resignation, miscreants broke into the main gate of the house at around 9:15 pm last Monday and looted Lakshmi Rani's sewing machine, cooking stove, gas cylinder and 10 baht of gold jewelry belonging to her brother's newly married wife kept in the cupboard. Lakshmi Rani's family members have been leaving the area fearing for their lives. They have taken shelter in a relative's house in another area.

305. Seven Hindu temples were attacked and set on fire at night on August 5, 2024
Date of Incident:
August 4, 2024
Division: Rangpur
District: Thakurgaon
Upazilla: Pirganj
Specific Location: Kosharaniganj Union, No. 2Kasha Raniganj Union
Details
On Monday night, August 5, 2024, 7 Hindu temples in Pirganj were attacked and set on fire. Satirghat Sri Sri Kali Temple, Sri Shiv Temple, Sri Sri Saraswati Temple in Kosharaniganj Union of Pirganj Upazila were attacked and set on fire. Elua Pukur Sri Kali Temple in Saidpur Union, Sri Sri Chandi Thakurani Kali Temple in Shivpur Village, Senua Tilipukur Sri Kali Temple, Sati Dah Cremation Ghat and Temple in No. 2 Kasha Raniganj Union were attacked and vandalized by the Vijaya procession. The iron stove for cremation of the body of the crematorium, tube well, tin ceiling of the temple and even the motor for bathing the body were looted. The place for chanting Krishna's name in the crematorium field was heinously damaged.
Involved Crimes

  • • Temple Vandalism
