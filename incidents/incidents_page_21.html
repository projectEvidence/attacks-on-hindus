310. More than 40 business establishments owned by minority Hindu was looted in Dinajpur community
Date of Incident:
August 4, 2024
Division: Rangpur
District: Dinajpur
Upazilla: Bochaganj
Specific Location: Chourongi Bazar, Ishania Union
Details
At least 40 minority shops were vandalized and looted in Chowrangi Bazar of Isania Union in Bochaganj Upazila of Dinajpur. On Monday afternoon, August 5, at least 100 people entered the market in a group and carried out an ambush. Later, when they moved towards Sahaspur village adjacent to the market, they were stopped by people from the minority community. Later, they left after threatening to leave their homes.

Eyewitness Hridoy Roy, a resident of Sahaspur village, said, "The attackers started vandalizing as soon as they entered the market. Our shops were selectively vandalized. The attackers had sticks and sharp weapons in their hands. We fled from the shop. Later, they looted the goods in front of our eyes. We were heading towards the village. At that time, when all of us in the neighborhood stood in front, they left."

315. 1971 war crime witness Ajit Sarkar was murdered on 05 August 2024
Date of Incident:
August 4, 2024
Division: Mymensingh
District: Mymensingh
Upazilla: Phulpur
Specific Location: Balia Union
Details
After the fall of Sheikh Hasina, on August 5, the house of Ajit Sarkar, a witness in a war crimes case, was attacked by the son and relatives of the accused in Domkona village of Balia Union in Phulpur, Mymensingh.
In 2016, Ajit Sarkar Penu testified in court in a war crimes case against Ghiyasuddin, a member of the 1971 Peace Committee.
The attack was led by Ghiyasuddin's son Lavlu on the afternoon of August 5. Ajit Sarkar sought refuge in the house of his neighbor Mortuza Mia to save his life. He was taken from there and dragged around Mahishaunda Bazar and forced to wear a garland made of shoes for being a witness in the case. Then, four or five people, including Lavlu, killed him on the river bank and dumped his body. On the evening of August 8, when the body was found floating downstream, Lavlu's nephew Moizuddin chopped it into pieces and dumped it a few kilometers away.

  • • House Vandalism
