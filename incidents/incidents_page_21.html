Date of Incident:
August 4, 2024
Division: Dhaka
District: Dhaka
Upazilla: Dhamrai
Specific Location: Barobaria, Gangutia Union
Details
On the evening of August 5, a group of 100 to 150 people marched and attacked the house of local Meena Rani Das in the Barbaria area of ​​Gangutia Union in Dhamrai, Dhaka. The attackers vandalized the main iron gate of the house. They broke the entrance gate to the tin house.
