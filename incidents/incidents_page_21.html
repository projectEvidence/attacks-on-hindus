Date of Incident:
August 4, 2024
Division: Dhaka
District: Dhaka
Upazilla: Dhamrai

Details

305. Hindu temple was demolished and looted
Date of Incident:
August 4, 2024
Division: Dhaka
District: Shariatpur
Upazilla: Shariatpur Sadar -Palong

Details
A temple at Dhanuka Mansa Bari in Shariatpur district town has been demolished. On the night of August 5, a Muslim mob demolished the temple and looted the furniture.
