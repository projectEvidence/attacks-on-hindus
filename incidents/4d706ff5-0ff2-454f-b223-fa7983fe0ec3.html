179. Hindu house was looted in Shantihar village of Swarupkathi on the night of August 6
Date of Incident:
August 5, 2024
Division: Barishal
District: Pirojpur
Upazilla: Nesarabad
Specific Location: Shantihar, Swarupkathi
Details
An attack was reported on a Hindu house in Santihar village of Swarupkathi, Pirojpur on the night of August 6. The victims said that out of the four men in the house, three were tied up and beaten, while one managed to escape. 15 thousand taka was looted. All of them were tortured overnight and left unconscious.
