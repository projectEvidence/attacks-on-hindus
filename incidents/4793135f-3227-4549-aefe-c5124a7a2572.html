101. Hindu Widow Arati Mondol’s Home in Madaripur Demolished and Seized by Force
Date of Incident:
December 1, 2024
Division: Dhaka
District: Madaripur
Upazilla: Shibchar
Police Station: Shivchar
Specific Location: Bablatala area
Details
On Monday, December 2, at around 11:00 AM, Raihan Molla and his hired goon Biplob attacked the home of poor and helpless widow Arati Mondol. During the attack, they vandalized the house—breaking doors and windows, cutting down trees, and even removing the water tap from the premises.

Arati Mondol, a 55-year-old widow, has been living on this land since her marriage to her late husband, Gouranga Mondol. With tears in her voice, she said, “Raihan Molla and his family had threatened us several times before. They tried to demolish the house and attack us earlier too. Even though the police had come and removed some of their encroachments, today they have forcefully taken over my home again.”
Arati Mondol’s son, Swapan Mondol, added, “This land belongs to us. Today a large group of people attacked our house, broke down our doors, and destroyed our trees. We are now living in great fear. The attackers could launch a bigger assault at any moment.”
Local residents believe that despite owning the land legally, the family is being subjected to such oppression simply because they belong to a minority community.
