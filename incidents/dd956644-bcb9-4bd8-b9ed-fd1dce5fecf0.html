172. In Faridpur, under-construction Durga idols were vandalized in two temples within 100 meters of Bhanga Police station
Date of Incident:
September 14, 2024
Division: Dhaka
District: Faridpur
Upazilla: Bhanga
Specific Location: Gurpatti
Details
Two Durga idols under construction at two temples within 100 meters of Bhanga police station in Faridpur were vandalized. Eight Durga idols under construction at the Bhanga Bazar Sarvajanin Hari Temple and the Bhanga Sarvajanin Kali Temple in Ward No. 8 (Gurpatti) of the municipality were vandalized.
