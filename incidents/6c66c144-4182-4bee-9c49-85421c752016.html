40. Zakir Hossain Babu entered the Maheshpur Shri Kali Temple in Sadar Upazila of Jamalpur and vandalized the idols by hacking them with a sharp weapon.
Date of Incident:
November 13, 2024
Division: Mymensingh
District: Jamalpur
Upazilla: Jamalpur Sadar
Specific Location: Maheshpur
Details
Zakir Hossain Babu entered the Maheshpur Shri Kali Temple in Sadar Upazila of Jamalpur and vandalized the idols by randomly hacking them with country-made weapons. He is a resident of Kasharupara village in Sadar. Locals have arrested a young man red-handed and handed him over to the police. Three country-made weapons have been recovered from him.
