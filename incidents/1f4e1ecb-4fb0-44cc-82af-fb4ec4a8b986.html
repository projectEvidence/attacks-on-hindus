261. The house of Khalilnagar UP Chairman Pranab Ghosh Bablu in Tala Upazila was vandalized and looted
Date of Incident:
August 4, 2024
Division: Khulna
District: Satkhira
Upazilla: Tala
Specific Location: Khalilnagar union parishad
Details
The house of Khalilnagar UP Chairman Pranab Ghosh Bablu in Tala Upazila was vandalized and looted on August 5, following the fall of Sheikh Hasina's government. He was an active supporter of the Awami League.
