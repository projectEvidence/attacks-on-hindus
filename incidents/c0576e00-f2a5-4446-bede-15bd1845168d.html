384. Hindu bride Sheuli Rani Paul from Zakiganj abducted four days before marriage by Muslim goons; parent severely injured
Date of Incident:
July 20, 2001
Division: Sylhet
District: Sylhet
Upazilla: Zakiganj
Specific Location: Biroshree(বিরশ্রী) Union
Details
Sheuli Rani Paul from Zakiganj was abducted by local muslim goons four days before marriage. These goons attacked the parents of Sheuli Rani paul. They were severly injured and admitted to Sylhet Osmani Medical college for treatment.
This incident happened on Saturday mid night at Borochalia village of Biroshree Union of Zakiganj.
The brother of the bride filed a case in local police station with 8/9 suspects. Four people were named in the police case as abductors. Some of the culprits are Abdur Rob, Achar Uddin, and Shibbir Ahmed. Shibbir Ahmed is from Ujirpur and Abu Bokor is from Majhergaon.
The marriage between Sheuli Rani Paul and Biplob Paul from Baniarchar Village, Tahirpur Upazilla of Sunamganj was already set and arrangement was going on full pace.
