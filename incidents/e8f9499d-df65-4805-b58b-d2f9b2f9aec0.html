95. Idol vandalism at the Sarbojanin Kali Temple in Nasibpur village of Laksmiganj Union Netrokona.
Date of Incident:
December 4, 2024
Division: Mymensingh
District: Netrokona
Upazilla: Netrakona-S Upazilla
Specific Location: Sarbojanin Kali Temple, Lakshmiganj Union
Details
An incident of idol vandalism has occurred at the Sarbojanin Kali Temple in Nasibpur village of Lakshmiganj Union, Netrokona Sadar Upazila. On Thursday night (December 5), this incident took place. After learning about the incident on Friday morning, the police detained one person for questioning.

It has been reported that 8-10 Hindu families in Nasibpur village have been celebrating the annual Kali Puja and regular worship by establishing a community temple in a tin-roofed structure for a long time. At some point on Thursday night, miscreants entered the temple and partially broke the hands of the Kali idol and the heads of the Dakini and Yogini idols.

Additionally, they attempted to set fire to the hair of the Kali idol, which was found on the floor. Although some parts of the hair burned, the fire did not spread. On Friday morning, the residents of the village noticed the incident.

Following the news, Additional Police Superintendent Saheb Ali Pathan, the Executive Officer (UNO) of the Sadar Upazila Asma Binte Rafiq, the president of the district Puja Celebration Committee Jnanesh Sarker, and local police responded.
