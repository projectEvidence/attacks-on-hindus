3. Bangladesh Medical University Associate Professor Dr. Anindita Dutta blocked and harassed on campus
Date of Incident:
March 15, 2025
Division: Dhaka
District: Dhaka
Upazilla: Dhaka City
Details
Bangladesh Medical University (formerly BSMMU) Associate Professor Dr. Anindita Dutta has been reportedly locked up on the campus. After a long 6 hours, she was rescued with the help of the university administration, army and police and sent home.

Dr. Anindita Dutta is the daughter of former MP and former Vice-Chancellor of Bangladesh Medical University, Professor Pran Gopal Dutta. She is an associate professor in the university's Radiology and Imaging Department. She is not known to have any political affiliation.

Investigations revealed that a group of people had locked up Anindita Dutta in her office in the Cancer Building in F Block of Bangladesh Medical University since Sunday (March 16) morning.
