129. On November 6, an attack was carried out at Sri Sri Kali temple and on the public Mahashanshan(crematory ground) in Sirajdikhan upazila of Munshiganj
Date of Incident:
November 5, 2024
Division: Dhaka
District: Munshiganj
Upazilla: Sirajdikhan
Specific Location: Kanainagar, Chitrakot
Details
On November 6, an attack was carried out on the public Mahashanshan(crematory) and Sri Sri Kali temple in Chitrakot Kanainagar village of Sirajdikhan upazila of Munshiganj. During this, the boundary wall under construction at the temple and Mahashanshan was demolished.
