16. A sixth-grade Hindu student was raped on her way home from school in Rangpur
Date of Incident:
March 17, 2025
Division: Rangpur
District: Rangpur
Upazilla: Mithapukur
Specific Location: বড় মির্জাপুর ইউনিয়ন/বড় হযরতপুর ইউনিয়নের চিথলী পশ্চিমপাড়া এলাকা
Details
A sixth-grade student was raped on her way home from private school in Mithapukur, Rangpur. The student's father has filed a case with the police station in this incident. The incident took place on Tuesday (March 18) afternoon. The police could not arrest the rapist.

According to police and local sources, the student, the daughter of a farmer from a village in Boro Mirzapur Union of the upazila, is studying in sixth grade at a local high school. The school is now closed. However, the girl used to go to school every day to study privately. She was returning home after finishing her private studies in the morning. Alam Mia (40), son of the deceased Abu Mia of the area, who was lying in wait in a corn field on the way, grabbed the girl and took her inside the corn field and raped her.

Later, when the girl heard her crying, people around the spot rescued her and admitted her to the hospital. The rapist fled. Mithapukur Police Station OC Abu Bakkar Siddique said that a case has been filed with the police station in this incident. The girl has been rescued and admitted to the hospital for a health check-up.

17. Bangladesh Medical University Associate Professor Dr. Anindita Dutta blocked and harassed on campus by Chhatra Dal leaders and activists and local mob
Date of Incident:
March 15, 2025
Division: Dhaka
District: Dhaka
Upazilla: Dhaka City
Police Station: Shahbag
Specific Location: PG Hospital
Details
Bangladesh Medical University (formerly BSMMU) Associate Professor Dr. Anindita Dutta was reportedly locked up on the campus. After a long 6 hours, she was rescued with the help of the university administration, army and police and sent home.

Dr. Anindita Dutta is the daughter of former MP and former Vice-Chancellor of Bangladesh Medical University, Professor Pran Gopal Dutta. She is an associate professor in the university's Radiology and Imaging Department. She is not known to have any political affiliation.

Investigations revealed that a group of people (some leaders of Shahbagh Thana Chhatra Dal and students) had locked up Anindita Dutta in her office in the Cancer Building in F Block of Bangladesh Medical University since Sunday (March 16) morning.

In this regard, Shahbagh Thana Chhatra Dal leader Rubel Ahmed said that there is a case against her for attacking the students before August 5. Based on the information of the plaintiff in the case, we came here and blocked her.

Bangladesh Medical University Proctor Professor Dr. Sheikh Farhad said that Pran Gopal sir's daughter works here. She has been detained here. We are also detained here with her. The army has also arrived at the scene.

University Vice-Chancellor Professor Dr. Shahinur Alam said, I heard that a group of students have come to the Cancer Building and taken up position. After informing the law enforcement agencies, the police came and took up position.

Shahbagh Police Station OC Khalid Mansur said, "So far, the Bangladesh Medical University authorities have not informed us anything about this. They have some internal rules and regulations. I don't know if there is any case against that person."

19. Two Hindu students, Bikarna Das Divya and Pranay Kundu, were expelled from the university for allegedly insulting Islam
Date of Incident:
March 15, 2025
Division: Rajshahi
District: Pabna
Upazilla: Pabna Sadar
Details
The university administration has temporarily expelled two students of Pabna Science and Technology University on charges of insulting Islam. The names of the two students are Bikarna Das Divya and Pranay Kundu. They are studying in the Department of Urban and Regional Planning. The matter was learned from the university's proctor's office on Sunday (March 16).

University Proctor Professor Dr. Kamruzzaman Khan said, "Last Friday, allegations of insulting religion were made against two students of the Department of Urban and Regional Planning. Their department gave them a show cause notice. Although they responded to the show cause notice, it was reported that it was not satisfactory. An emergency meeting of the Disciplinary Board was held today on this issue.

In the meeting, it was decided to temporarily expel the two students from the Department of Urban and Regional Planning."

On Friday night (March 14), the Facebook posts and comments of Bikarna Das Divya and Pranay Kundu about insulting Islam went viral on social media. That night, general students protested on campus demanding the expulsion of the two students. Later, show cause notices were given to the two students.

The administration decided to temporarily expel the two students in an emergency disciplinary board meeting today.

Meanwhile, screenshots of the Messenger group of members of the Sanatan Vidyarthi Sangsad of the Pharmacy Department were leaked on social media last night. In that group, the students alleged that Bidyut Sarkar, Suborna Sarkar of the 2017-18 academic year, Dipu Biswas of the 2018-19 academic year, Tanoy Sarkar of the 2019-20 academic year, and Ankan Ghosh of the 2022-23 academic year had insulted Islam. The students started the protest at 11:30 am demanding the expulsion of these five new students.

Date of Incident:
March 15, 2025
Division: Dhaka
District: Narsingdi
Upazilla: Raipura, Narsingdi
Details
A mother of three (40) of Sanatan Dharma was raped in her house in Raipura, Narsingdi. The accused threatened to kill her husband and wife when the incident came to light. Raipura Police Station Officer-in-Charge Adil Mahmud said that on Monday afternoon, the victim had appeared at the Narsingdi Police Superintendent's office and narrated the incident to Additional Police Superintendent (Administration and Finance) Md. Kalimullah. Later, the woman went to the police station but did not immediately file a written complaint with the police station.

Earlier, the incident took place around 9 pm on Sunday. The accomplices in the rape recorded the video of the rape on their mobile phones and threatened to kill the husband and wife if they told anyone about the incident.

The housewife told the police that on Sunday night, during the Tarawih prayers, Ratan Mia's son Rakib Mia entered her house and raped her during the rain. Rakib's two accomplices present at the time recorded the video of the rape on their mobile phones. Rakib and his accomplices snatched the gold ornaments from the victim's ears and neck. While leaving, they threatened to slaughter the couple if they told anyone about the incident.

Police said that accused Rakib is a drug dealer and user. He is an accused in multiple drug cases. Multiple cases are pending against him in different police stations.

On Monday afternoon, the victim himself appeared at the Narsingdi Superintendent of Police's office and narrated the incident to Additional Superintendent of Police (Administration and Finance) Md. Kalimullah. After hearing the incident, the Additional Superintendent of Police ordered the officer-in-charge of Raipura Police Station to take legal action.

In this regard, Raipura Police Station Officer-in-Charge Adil Mahmud said, "The Additional Superintendent of Police called and informed me about the matter. I have instructed the Inspector Investigation to take necessary action. The woman has not filed a written complaint with the police station yet. A drive has been launched to arrest the accused."

22. Robbers hacked Hindu gold trader Dilip Das to death in front of his wife and looted about 25 bhori of gold in Savar on March 09, 2025 as they were about to close their shop
Date of Incident:
March 8, 2025
Division: Dhaka
District: Dhaka
Upazilla: Savar
Specific Location: Nayarhat Bazaar Swarnapotti area of ​​Ashulia
Details
Robbers hacked Hindu gold trader Dilip Das to death in front of his wife and looted about 25 bhori of gold in Savar as they were about to close their shop.

The incident happened around 9:00pm at Nayarhat Bazaar Swarnapotti, known for its numerous gold jewellery shops. According to witnesses, three armed men arrived in a white car and detonated crude bombs to create panic. They then approached Dilip, who was about to close his shop, and stabbed him indiscriminately before snatching the bag of gold he was carrying. His wife was with him at the time. The criminals detonated more crude bombs before fleeing the scene in their vehicle. Dilip was rushed to Enam Medical College Hospital in critical condition, but he succumbed to his injuries during treatment.

His wife, Saraswati Das, said, "The armed men hacked my husband to death and looted 20 to 25 bhori of gold in his bag right in front of me."

Dr Merajur Rayhan, emergency medical officer at Enam Medical College Hospital, told The Daily Star that Dilip had suffered severe injuries to his chest, face, and multiple parts of his body. "We started treatment immediately, but he died due to excessive bleeding," he said.

Details

thedailycampus.com news reported that: before the local people could come forward due to the child's screams, acquaintances of the teacher took her to a private clinic called Sheba Hospital and Diagnostic Center in the district town. Specific Location: মৌলভীবাজারের বড়লেখা উপজেলা
Details
The incident happened on the morning Monday, 03 March 2025, the child, who lives with her family at a rented house, was given Tk 30 to buy candy from a shop below their residence. When the child did not return home promptly, the mother went downstairs and found the shopkeeper undressing and assaulting the child. Upon witnessing the incident, the mother screamed and rescued the child.

The victim's mother filed a case with the Barlekha Police Station last night, and police subsequently arrested the accused — the 17-year-old shopkeeper — in connection to the case, said Abul Kashem Sarkar, OC of Barlekha Police Station.

Officer-in-charge of Baralekha Police Station Abul Kashem Sarkar said that the child was first taken to Baralekha Upazila Health Complex and later transferred to Sylhet MAG Osmani Medical College Hospital for advanced treatment.

The child's physical condition became more complicated as he was suffering from thalassemia. The child is currently undergoing treatment at a private hospital in Sylhet.

The child's mother said that she has been suffering from insecurity since the incident. She said, "The owner of the house is threatening me because I told the police about the evidence of torture. On the day of the incident, they did not even let the child go to the hospital."

28. Islamic militants of Hizb-ut-Tahrir threaten to burn community centers if musical instruments are played at Hindu weddings
Date of Incident:
February 27, 2025
Division: Chattogram
District: Chattogram
Police Station: Kotwali, CMP
Specific Location: Various Community Centers in Chittagong City
Details
In the wake of the threat from the banned militant organization Hizb-ut-Tahrir, complications have arisen regarding the community centers in Chittagong city where weddings of traditional religious followers were booked. The militant organization has threatened through a handwritten letter that no musical instruments will be used at weddings of traditional religious followers. If this is not agreed to, the community centers will be set on fire.

It is worth mentioning that in late January, all community centers around the parade ground in Chittagong were forced to cancel advance bookings for Hindu, Buddhist and non-Muslim weddings on the occasion of the waz mahfil of Jamaat-e-Islami speaker Mizanur Rahman Azhari. As a result, those families who had already printed wedding cards and sent out invitations were affected.

Many were forced to change the wedding date, and many were forced to book the ceremony at a far-off community center at a much higher rent. They were financially affected and harassed.

Meanwhile, there has been widespread excitement across the city over the threat of Hizb ut Tahrir. However, no statement has been received from any responsible person from the police or administration regarding this matter.
