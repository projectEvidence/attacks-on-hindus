Date of Incident: October 27, 2024
Division: Khulna
District: Khulna
Upazilla: Phultala
Police Station: Daulatpur
Specific Location: Maheshwarpasha Kalibari Bazar
Details
Amid the nationwide theft and robbery targeting Hindu businessmen, a brutal robbery took place in broad daylight at a gold shop named Dutta Jewelers in Maheshwarpasha Kalibari Bazar under Daulatpur police station in Khulna city. Ordinary pedestrians in the area thought that a customer had entered the shop. After 2-1 minutes, hand grenades started hurling from behind and in front of the car. This created a tense and frightening situation in the area.
