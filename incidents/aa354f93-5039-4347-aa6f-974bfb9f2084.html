72. Police and BGB attacked Chinmoy's followers peacefully staying on the ground floor of the court building
Date of Incident:
November 26, 2024
Division: Chattogram
District: Chattogram
Police Station: Kotwali, CMP
Details
Chinmoy Krishna's lawyer Swarup Kanti Nath said, "After the magistrate court rejected bail, we filed a case and applied for bail in the Metropolitan Sessions Judge's Court." The court has put the application for hearing on Wednesday (today). Chinmoy's followers had been peacefully staying on the ground floor of the court building since morning. In the afternoon, the police and BGB attacked ordinary Sanatanis without provocation. Such an attack under the court building is unprecedented.

It was seen on the spot that tension was seen when the police tried to remove Chinmoy's followers around 2 pm. Then, an old mini truck and a microbus from Malkhana were brought to the ground floor of the court premises and a blockade was created in front of the prison van. Many of the blockaders were then seen lying on the ground. As tension increased in the court premises, Metropolitan Sessions Judge Md. Saiful Islam's chamber, Metropolitan PP Mofizul Haque Bhuiyan and General Secretary of the Chittagong Bar Association Ashraf Hossain Chowdhury Razzaj were seen entering. At that time, Chinmoy's lawyer was waiting outside the Metropolitan Sessions Judge's court to hear the case. At that time, several hundred police, SWAT and BGB members stationed on the ground floor of the court building blew whistles simultaneously and first exploded sound grenades. After that, they dispersed the blockaders by beating them with batons. Everyone dispersed after 15 to 20 minutes of action by the three forces.

Six Chinmoy followers were admitted to the hospital after being injured in the police baton-charge. They are Srivas Das, Sharku Das, Choton, Sujit Ghosh, Utpal and Enamul Haque.
