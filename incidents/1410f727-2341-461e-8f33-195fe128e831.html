198. The house of Asim Baran Sushil at Rangunia - vandalized and looted
Date of Incident:
August 5, 2024
Division: Chattogram
District: Chattogram
Upazilla: Rangunia
Specific Location: Mughaler Hat, Hosnabad Union
Details
On 06 August, miscreants broke the lock of the house of Union Awami League President Asim Baran Sushil at Mughaler Hat in Hosnabad Union of Rangunia, Chittagong Upazila, vandalizing and looting his belongings.
