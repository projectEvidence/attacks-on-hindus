296. Debnath Bastralay in Chak Bazar, Barishal was looted
Date of Incident:
August 4, 2024
Division: Barishal
District: Barishal
Upazilla: Barisal Sadar
Specific Location: Chak Bazar
Details
Kajal Das, coordinator of the Barisal Anti-Oppression Forum, said, "There have been attacks to loot and seize Hindu businesses in Barisal city. Such incidents have occurred at Mahadev Bhandar on Bazar Road and Debnath Bastralay in Chak Bazar."
