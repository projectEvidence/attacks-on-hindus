271. The house and the pharmacy of Rural doctor Uttam Kumar Roy was attacked and vandalized
Date of Incident:
August 4, 2024
Division: Dhaka
District: Faridpur
Upazilla: Madhukhali
Specific Location: Bamundi Bazar, Meghchami union
Details
On August 5, at around 8 pm, the house of rural doctor Uttam Kumar Roy, a resident of Bamundi village in Meghchami union of Madhukhali, was attacked. The doctor's pharmacy and the Kali temple at his house were also vandalized.

Rural doctor Uttam Kumar Roy told Prothom Alo that he was at his drug store in Bamundi Bazar at around 8 pm yesterday. At that time, miscreants aged 18-35 came to beat him up.

281. A Durga Temple and an under construction temple was vandalised and looted
Date of Incident:
August 4, 2024
Division: Rangpur
District: Dinajpur
Upazilla: Dinajpur Sadar
Specific Location: Fultola central crematorium ground
Details
The under construction Harishabha Ghar and Durga Temple at the Fultala Central Crematorium Ground in Dinajpur Sadar Upazila have been vandalised on August 5, 2024. During this attack, a group of 150 to 200 Muslim fundamentalists looted and vandalised the temple. People of the Hindu community are being intimidated into not speaking out about this incident. These fundamentalists had previously blocked the
temple work at the Fultala Central Crematorium ground which is situated on the bank of the Punarbhava River in Dinajpur city.
Involved Crimes
  • • Temple Vandalism
  • • Loot
