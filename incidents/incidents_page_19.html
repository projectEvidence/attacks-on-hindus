274. Seven Hindu temples were attacked and set on fire at night on August 5, 2024
Date of Incident:
August 4, 2024
Division: Rangpur
District: Thakurgaon
Upazilla: Pirganj
Specific Location: Kosharaniganj Union, No. 2Kasha Raniganj Union
Details
On Monday night, August 5, 2024, 7 Hindu temples in Pirganj were attacked and set on fire. Satirghat Sri Sri Kali Temple, Sri Shiv Temple, Sri Sri Saraswati Temple in Kosharaniganj Union of Pirganj Upazila were attacked and set on fire. Elua Pukur Sri Kali Temple in Saidpur Union, Sri Sri Chandi Thakurani Kali Temple in Shivpur Village, Senua Tilipukur Sri Kali Temple, Sati Dah Cremation Ghat and Temple in No. 2 Kasha Raniganj Union were attacked and vandalized by the Vijaya procession. The iron stove for cremation of the body of the crematorium, tube well, tin ceiling of the temple and even the motor for bathing the body were looted. The place for chanting Krishna's name in the crematorium field was heinously damaged.
Involved Crimes
  • • Arson
  • • Temple Vandalism
Proofs

  • News on amader-protidin.com

Date of Incident:
August 4, 2024
Division: Khulna
District: Jashore
Upazilla: Bagherpara
Specific Location: Shalborat
Details
On the night of August 5, 2024, a group of 10 to 12 people attacked the Hindu neighborhood of Shalborat village in the upazila. 65 Hindu families live in this neighborhood. They went to five to six houses with black cloths tied around their faces and ramadas in their hands. The people of the neighborhood said that out of fear, everyone in the neighborhood somehow spent the night in the neighboring garden. On Tuesday morning, the men left the neighborhood and went elsewhere.
