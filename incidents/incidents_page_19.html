Division: Khulna

Upazilla: Bagherpara
Specific Location: Shalborat
Details

They went to five to six houses with black cloths tied around their faces and ramadas in their hands. The people of the neighborhood said that out of fear, everyone in the neighborhood somehow spent the night in the neighboring garden.
