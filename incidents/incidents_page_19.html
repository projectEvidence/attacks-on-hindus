272. More than 40 business establishments owned by minority Hindu was looted in Dinajpur community
Date of Incident:
August 4, 2024
Division: Rangpur
District: Dinajpur
Upazilla: Bochaganj
Specific Location: Chourongi Bazar, Ishania Union
Details
At least 40 minority shops were vandalized and looted in Chowrangi Bazar of Isania Union in Bochaganj Upazila of Dinajpur. On Monday afternoon, August 5, at least 100 people entered the market in a group and carried out an ambush. Later, when they moved towards Sahaspur village adjacent to the market, they were stopped by people from the minority community. Later, they left after threatening to leave their homes.

Eyewitness Hridoy Roy, a resident of Sahaspur village, said, "The attackers started vandalizing as soon as they entered the market. Our shops were selectively vandalized. The attackers had sticks and sharp weapons in their hands. We fled from the shop. Later, they looted the goods in front of our eyes. We were heading towards the village. At that time, when all of us in the neighborhood stood in front, they left."
Involved Crimes
  • • Shop Vandalism
  • • Loot

276. House of Ananta Mukherjee and a temple in the nearby area of ​​Kuakata Municipality in Patuakhali were attacked
Date of Incident:
August 4, 2024
Division: Barishal
District: Patuakhali
Upazilla: Kalapara
Details
The residence of Awami League Joint General Secretary Ananta Mukherjee and a temple in the nearby area of ​​Kuakata Municipality in Patuakhali were attacked. The incident took place around 4:30 pm.

Ananta Mukherjee alleged, "After hearing the news of the government's resignation, BNP-Jamaat leaders and activists took out a procession in Kuakata Municipality. The procession came towards my house. At one stage, people from the procession attacked my house. Many of the attackers entered my house. They broke and smashed every thing in the house. Nothing in my house was saved. Then the attackers attacked the temple next to my house. The doors and windows of the temple were damaged."
