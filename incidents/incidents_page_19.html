276. Computer business of Partha Pratim Nath was looted by armed miscreants in Jessore
Date of Incident:
August 4, 2024
Division: Khulna
District: Jashore
Upazilla: Jessore Sadar
Specific Location: Railway road
Details
A group of miscreants armed with sticks attacked and looted the Nath Computer business of Partha Pratim Nath, son of Jessore Sadar Upazila Awami League President Mohit Kumar Nath, located on the city's railway road. The miscreants looted computers, laptops, ACs, routers and imported components from the shop and three warehouses.

Partha Pratim Nath told Prothom Alo, "I have been running the business for 24 years. The miscreants, carrying the national flag on their heads and sticks in their hands, openly looted goods worth more than four crore taka. When the army was informed, a car finally arrived. But the looting could not be stopped. My father spent a lot of money fighting the national parliament and upazila elections against the boat symbol. We owe crores of taka to the bank. We are now unemployed. We are sitting at home. I have become destitute and have sat on the streets."

284. 1971 war crime witness Ajit Sarkar was murdered on 05 August 2024
Date of Incident:
August 4, 2024
Division: Mymensingh
District: Mymensingh
Upazilla: Phulpur
Specific Location: Balia Union
Details
After the fall of Sheikh Hasina, on August 5, the house of Ajit Sarkar, a witness in a war crimes case, was attacked by the son and relatives of the accused in Domkona village of Balia Union in Phulpur, Mymensingh.
In 2016, Ajit Sarkar Penu testified in court in a war crimes case against Ghiyasuddin, a member of the 1971 Peace Committee.
The attack was led by Ghiyasuddin's son Lavlu on the afternoon of August 5. Ajit Sarkar sought refuge in the house of his neighbor Mortuza Mia to save his life. He was taken from there and dragged around Mahishaunda Bazar and forced to wear a garland made of shoes for being a witness in the case. Then, four or five people, including Lavlu, killed him on the river bank and dumped his body. On the evening of August 8, when the body was found floating downstream, Lavlu's nephew Moizuddin chopped it into pieces and dumped it a few kilometers away.

  • • Loot
  • • House Vandalism
