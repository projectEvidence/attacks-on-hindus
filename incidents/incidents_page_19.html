271. Homeopathic doctor Manoj Kumar Biswas was beaten up, his shop was looted, house temple was vandalized
Date of Incident:
August 4, 2024
Division: Dhaka
District: Faridpur
Upazilla: Madhukhali
Specific Location: Bamundi Bazar, Meghchami Union
Details
The attackers attacked and vandalized the shop of homeopathic doctor Manoj Kumar Biswas in Bamundi Bazar. Manoj was beaten up. Later, he went into hiding with his family.
