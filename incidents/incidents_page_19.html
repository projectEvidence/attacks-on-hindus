271. House and Business establishment of Bikash Pal was looted and vandalized
Date of Incident:
August 4, 2024
Division: Khulna
District: Jashore
Upazilla: Keshabpur
Specific Location: Pajiya Bazar
Details
On Monday, August 5, at around 3:30 pm, the house of rice trader Bikash Chandra Pal in Pajia Bazar was attacked and vandalized. At the same time, his business establishment was also looted. 200 sacks of rice, 250 sacks of flour, 450 sacks of khail and 250 sacks of husk and nine lakh taka were looted from the establishment. On the same day, a man named Ripon from the Notunhat area of ​​the upazila demanded 2 lakh taka from him. Ripon also took 50 thousand taka from him.

275. House of Hindu City Mayor was looted during wedding reception
Date of Incident:
August 4, 2024
Division: Barishal
District: Patuakhali
Upazilla: Kalapara
Details

His son's post-wedding ceremony was taking place that day. At the end of the ceremony, a group of miscreants suddenly came and attacked his house. The decorated pandals at the venue were vandalised.
