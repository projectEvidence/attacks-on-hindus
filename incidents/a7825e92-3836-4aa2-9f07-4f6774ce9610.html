6. Two Hindu students, Bikarna Das Divya and Pranay Kundu, were expelled from the university for allegedly insulting Islam
Date of Incident:
March 15, 2025
Division: Rajshahi
District: Pabna
Upazilla: Pabna Sadar
Details
The university administration has temporarily expelled two students of Pabna Science and Technology University on charges of insulting Islam. The names of the two students are Bikarna Das Divya and Pranay Kundu. They are studying in the Department of Urban and Regional Planning. The matter was learned from the university's proctor's office on Sunday (March 16).

University Proctor Professor Dr. Kamruzzaman Khan said, "Last Friday, allegations of insulting religion were made against two students of the Department of Urban and Regional Planning. Their department gave them a show cause notice. Although they responded to the show cause notice, it was reported that it was not satisfactory. An emergency meeting of the Disciplinary Board was held today on this issue.

In the meeting, it was decided to temporarily expel the two students from the Department of Urban and Regional Planning."

On Friday night (March 14), the Facebook posts and comments of Bikarna Das Divya and Pranay Kundu about insulting Islam went viral on social media. That night, general students protested on campus demanding the expulsion of the two students. Later, show cause notices were given to the two students.

The administration decided to temporarily expel the two students in an emergency disciplinary board meeting today.

Meanwhile, screenshots of the Messenger group of members of the Sanatan Vidyarthi Sangsad of the Pharmacy Department were leaked on social media last night. In that group, the students alleged that Bidyut Sarkar, Suborna Sarkar of the 2017-18 academic year, Dipu Biswas of the 2018-19 academic year, Tanoy Sarkar of the 2019-20 academic year, and Ankan Ghosh of the 2022-23 academic year had insulted Islam. The students started the protest at 11:30 am demanding the expulsion of these five new students.
