316. In Dhamrai near Dhaka, House of Meena Rani Das was vandalized along with Durga Temple
Date of Incident:
August 4, 2024
Division: Dhaka
District: Dhaka
Upazilla: Dhamrai
Specific Location: Barobaria, Gangutia Union
Details
On the evening of August 5, a group of 100 to 150 people marched and attacked the house of local Meena Rani Das in the Barbaria area of ​​Gangutia Union in Dhamrai, Dhaka. The attackers vandalized the main iron gate of the house. They broke the entrance gate to the tin house. The attackers broke the glass of three windows of three rooms next to the veranda of the house, a few chairs in front of the Durga temple, and all the glass of two private cars in the garage outside the house.

320. Twenty Hindu households were attacked and looted by Muslim mob
Date of Incident:
August 4, 2024
Division: Khulna
District: Jashore
Upazilla: Bagherpara
Specific Location: Rishipalli, Sultannagar , Dholgram Union
Details
About 90 Hindu families and more than 500 people live in Sultannagar Rishipalli. Most of them are poor. Even so, 12 houses were looted after the attack, said Nalin Chandra Biswas, president of the Harichand Mandir Committee. They took away more than a hundred poultry, a goat, gold coins, cash, rice, pulses, oil, bamboo baskets or kulas, whatever they found in front of them from these houses. The attackers also opened the only deep tube well in the village.

On that day, after the fall of the Sheikh Hasina government, a group of 40 to 50 people attacked the Hindupalli at around 6:30 pm. Each of them had indigenous weapons like ramda, hansua, iron rods, sabals, and sticks.
Involved Crimes
  • • Physical Assault
  • • Loot
  • • House Vandalism
Proofs
  • News on www.prothomalo.com
  • News on www.ajkerpatrika.com
  • Proof on bengalihindu.github.io
Victims
  • Name: Shashadhar Biswas
    Suffered Crimes
    • • Physical Assault
    • • Loot
    • • House Vandalism
    Notes: Shashadhar Biswas said that the attackers entered the house and started abusing him. Then they entered the house and looted everything. And what they could not take, they broke and scattered. They broke the tin door of his house with a hammer. At one point, they broke the door and entered the house and looted the belongings.
  • Name: Manimala Biswas
    Suffered Crimes
    • • Physical Assault
    • • Loot
    Notes: Pashupati Biswas (65) had fled the house after hearing about the attack. But his wife Manimala Biswas (55) could not leave the house; she was standing in the yard. Scared by the suddenness of the attack, she ran into the house. But when she went up to the balcony, she was hit in the waist with a club. She fell there. Then, the miscreants broke the door and entered the house and looted gold ornaments, cash and belongings.
