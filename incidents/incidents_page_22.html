326. Hindu temple was demolished and looted
Date of Incident:
August 4, 2024
Division: Dhaka
District: Shariatpur
Upazilla: Shariatpur Sadar -Palong
Specific Location: Dhanuka Manasa Bari
Details
A temple at Dhanuka Mansa Bari in Shariatpur district town has been demolished. On the night of August 5, a Muslim mob demolished the temple and looted the furniture. Later in the night, when the army members went there, the miscreants fled.

327. In Dhamrai near Dhaka, House of Meena Rani Das was vandalized along with Durga Temple
Date of Incident:
August 4, 2024
Division: Dhaka
District: Dhaka
Upazilla: Dhamrai
Specific Location: Barobaria, Gangutia Union
Details
On the evening of August 5, a group of 100 to 150 people marched and attacked the house of local Meena Rani Das in the Barbaria area of ​​Gangutia Union in Dhamrai, Dhaka. The attackers vandalized the main iron gate of the house. They broke the entrance gate to the tin house. The attackers broke the glass of three windows of three rooms next to the veranda of the house, a few chairs in front of the Durga temple, and all the glass of two private cars in the garage outside the house.
