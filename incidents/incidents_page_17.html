243. Rich Hindu businessman house and business establishment was looted and vandalized
Date of Incident:
August 4, 2024
Division: Rangpur
District: Gaibandha
Upazilla: Palashbari
Specific Location: Gopinathpur (Jundah) in Barisal Union
Details
On the night of August 5, the home and business of businessman Shri Gopal Chandra Roy and his younger brother Gokul Chandra Roy of Palashbari Upazila were attacked. The houses were vandalized and looted. Five tractors of the brick kiln, a joint business of the two brothers, were burned. Businessman Shri Gopal Chandra Roy said that on the night of the incident, 30 to 35 youths wearing masks attacked the home with Ram Da, sticks and sticks. To save their lives, he and his family took shelter in the darkness next to the house.

254. Durga Temple was vandalised and looted
Date of Incident:
August 4, 2024
Division: Barishal
District: Jhalokati
Upazilla: Kathalia
Specific Location: Uttarpar, Satani Bazar, Aurabunia Union
Details
On August 5, 2024, an attack was carried out on the Sri Sri Sarbajanin Durga Temple, north of Satani Bazar, Paschim Chitki village, Aurabunia union, Kanthalia upazila, Jhalokathi. Several hundreds islamist fundamentalists led by local Manjurul Sikder (50) took part in the attack. During this time, they vandalised and looted various items. The people of the minority Hindu community were unable to resist the sudden preplanned attack by the muslim fundamentalist group. They complained to the police. The police arrested the main conspirator, Manjurul Sikder, but the rest were not arrested. They are pressuring the Hindus to withdraw the case.
