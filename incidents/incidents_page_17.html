242. House of Ananta Mukherjee and a temple in the nearby area of ​​Kuakata Municipality in Patuakhali were attacked
Date of Incident:
August 4, 2024
Division: Barishal
District: Patuakhali
Upazilla: Kalapara
Details
The residence of Awami League Joint General Secretary Ananta Mukherjee and a temple in the nearby area of ​​Kuakata Municipality in Patuakhali were attacked. The incident took place around 4:30 pm.

Ananta Mukherjee alleged, "After hearing the news of the government's resignation, BNP-Jamaat leaders and activists took out a procession in Kuakata Municipality. The procession came towards my house. At one stage, people from the procession attacked my house. Many of the attackers entered my house. They broke and smashed every thing in the house. Nothing in my house was saved. Then the attackers attacked the temple next to my house. The doors and windows of the temple were damaged."

255. Rich Hindu businessman house and business establishment was looted and vandalized
Date of Incident:
August 4, 2024
Division: Rangpur
District: Gaibandha
Upazilla: Palashbari
Specific Location: Gopinathpur (Jundah) in Barisal Union
Details
On the night of August 5, the home and business of businessman Shri Gopal Chandra Roy and his younger brother Gokul Chandra Roy of Palashbari Upazila were attacked. The houses were vandalized and looted. Five tractors of the brick kiln, a joint business of the two brothers, were burned. Businessman Shri Gopal Chandra Roy said that on the night of the incident, 30 to 35 youths wearing masks attacked the home with Ram Da, sticks and sticks. To save their lives, he and his family took shelter in the darkness next to the house.
