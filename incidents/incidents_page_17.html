Date of Incident:
August 4, 2024
Division: Barishal
District: Patuakhali
Upazilla: Kalapara
Details
The residence of Awami League Joint General Secretary Ananta Mukherjee and a temple in the nearby area of ​​Kuakata Municipality in Patuakhali were attacked. The incident took place around 4:30 pm.

Ananta Mukherjee alleged, "After hearing the news of the government's resignation, BNP-Jamaat leaders and activists took out a procession in Kuakata Municipality. Nothing in my house was saved. Then the attackers attacked the temple next to my house. The doors and windows of the temple were damaged."
