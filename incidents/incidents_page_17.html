Date of Incident:
August 4, 2024
Division: Barishal
District: Jhalokati
Upazilla: Kathalia
Specific Location: Uttarpar, Satani Bazar, Aurabunia Union
Details
On August 5, 2024, an attack was carried out on the Sri Sri Sarbajanin Durga Temple, north of Satani Bazar, Paschim Chitki village, Aurabunia union, Kanthalia upazila, Jhalokathi. Several hundreds islamist fundamentalists led by local Manjurul Sikder (50) took part in the attack. During this time, they vandalised and looted various items. The people of the minority Hindu community were unable to resist the sudden preplanned attack by the muslim fundamentalist group. They complained to the police. The police arrested the main conspirator, Manjurul Sikder, but the rest were not arrested. They are pressuring the Hindus to withdraw the case.

251. Seven Hindu temples were attacked and set on fire at night on August 5, 2024
Date of Incident:
August 4, 2024
Division: Rangpur
District: Thakurgaon
Upazilla: Pirganj
Specific Location: Kosharaniganj Union, No. 2Kasha Raniganj Union
Details
On Monday night, August 5, 2024, 7 Hindu temples in Pirganj were attacked and set on fire. Satirghat Sri Sri Kali Temple, Sri Shiv Temple, Sri Sri Saraswati Temple in Kosharaniganj Union of Pirganj Upazila were attacked and set on fire. Elua Pukur Sri Kali Temple in Saidpur Union, Sri Sri Chandi Thakurani Kali Temple in Shivpur Village, Senua Tilipukur Sri Kali Temple, Sati Dah Cremation Ghat and Temple in No. 2 Kasha Raniganj Union were attacked and vandalized by the Vijaya procession. The iron stove for cremation of the body of the crematorium, tube well, tin ceiling of the temple and even the motor for bathing the body were looted. The place for chanting Krishna's name in the crematorium field was heinously damaged.

  • • Temple Vandalism
