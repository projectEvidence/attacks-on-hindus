281. Nine Hindu women from a house in Syedkathi Union spent the night in a pond to escape rape
Date of Incident:
August 4, 2024
Division: Barishal
District: Barishal
Upazilla: Banaripara
Specific Location: Syedkathi Union
Details
Nine Hindu women from a house in Syedkathi Union spent the whole night into a pond to escape rape. Finding no one in the house, they looted furniture and money.
