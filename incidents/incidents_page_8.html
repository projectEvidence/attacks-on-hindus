Date of Incident: August 4, 2024

District: Gaibandha
Upazilla: Palashbari

Details
On the night of August 5, the home and business of businessman Shri Gopal Chandra Roy and his younger brother Gokul Chandra Roy of Palashbari Upazila were attacked.
