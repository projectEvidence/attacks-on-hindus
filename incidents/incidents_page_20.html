Date of Incident:
August 4, 2024
Division: Dhaka
District: Dhaka
Upazilla: Dhamrai
Specific Location: Barobaria, Gangutia Union
Details

The attackers vandalized the main iron gate of the house. They broke the entrance gate to the tin house. The attackers broke the glass of three windows of three rooms next to the veranda of the house, a few chairs in front of the Durga temple, and all the glass of two private cars in the garage outside the house.
