288. Computer business of Partha Pratim Nath was looted by armed miscreants in Jessore
Date of Incident:
August 4, 2024
Division: Khulna
District: Jashore
Upazilla: Jessore Sadar
Specific Location: Railway road
Details
A group of miscreants armed with sticks attacked and looted the Nath Computer business of Partha Pratim Nath, son of Jessore Sadar Upazila Awami League President Mohit Kumar Nath, located on the city's railway road. The miscreants looted computers, laptops, ACs, routers and imported components from the shop and three warehouses.

Partha Pratim Nath told Prothom Alo, "I have been running the business for 24 years. The miscreants, carrying the national flag on their heads and sticks in their hands, openly looted goods worth more than four crore taka. When the army was informed, a car finally arrived. But the looting could not be stopped. My father spent a lot of money fighting the national parliament and upazila elections against the boat symbol. We owe crores of taka to the bank. We are now unemployed. We are sitting at home. I have become destitute and have sat on the streets."

298. More than 40 business establishments owned by minority Hindu was looted in Dinajpur community
Date of Incident:
August 4, 2024
Division: Rangpur
District: Dinajpur
Upazilla: Bochaganj
Specific Location: Chourongi Bazar, Ishania Union
Details
At least 40 minority shops were vandalized and looted in Chowrangi Bazar of Isania Union in Bochaganj Upazila of Dinajpur. On Monday afternoon, August 5, at least 100 people entered the market in a group and carried out an ambush. Later, when they moved towards Sahaspur village adjacent to the market, they were stopped by people from the minority community. Later, they left after threatening to leave their homes.

Eyewitness Hridoy Roy, a resident of Sahaspur village, said, "The attackers started vandalizing as soon as they entered the market. Our shops were selectively vandalized. The attackers had sticks and sharp weapons in their hands. We fled from the shop. Later, they looted the goods in front of our eyes. We were heading towards the village. At that time, when all of us in the neighborhood stood in front, they left."
Involved Crimes
  • • Shop Vandalism
  • • Loot
