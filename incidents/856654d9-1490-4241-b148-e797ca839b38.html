294. On August 05, targeted attacks happened on Hindus in Pirojpur Indurkani Upazila
Date of Incident:
August 4, 2024
Division: Barishal
District: Pirojpur
Upazilla: Zianagar
Details
Hindus have been attacked, vandalized, arsoned and looted in Pirojpur Indurkani Upazila. The houses of freedom fighters and ordinary Hindus in West Balipara Board School, Pather Hat, Battala Bazar, Khaigo Hat and Balipara Bazar of Balipara Union No. 3 of Indurkani have been attacked. Many shops have been looted and vandalized.

  • • House Vandalism
  • • Loot
  • • Shop Vandalism
