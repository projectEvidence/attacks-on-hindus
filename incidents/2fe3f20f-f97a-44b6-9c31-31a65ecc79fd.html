139. Various parts of five idols have been vandalized at the "Sajjankanda District Road Transport Owners Unity Council Temple", located next to the Rajbari-Faridpur regional highway
Date of Incident:
October 8, 2024
Division: Dhaka
District: Rajbari
Upazilla: Rajbari Sadar
Details
Various parts of five idols were vandalized at the Sajjankanda District Road Transport Owner Unity Council temple on the Rajbari-Faridpur regional highway. Some parts of the faces of some idols were broken at any time between 10 and 11 am. Police have inspected the scene.
