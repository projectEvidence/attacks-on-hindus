195. House of Chaiti Kundu in Swarupkathi was vandalized and looted
Date of Incident:
August 4, 2024
Division: Barishal
District: Pirojpur
Upazilla: Nesarabad
Details
Chaiti Kundu, a resident of Swarupkathi Upazila Sadar in Pirojpur, said, "As soon as the government fell, some people from the area entered our house, vandalized and looted. They took everything that was in the house. They took cash, gold ornaments, furniture. My parents saw the horror and went into hiding to save their lives."
