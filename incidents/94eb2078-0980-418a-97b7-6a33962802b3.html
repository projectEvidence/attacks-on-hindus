194. In Iluhar Union of Banaripara, Hindu family was robbed and threatened to leave the country
Date of Incident:
August 5, 2024
Division: Barishal
District: Barishal
Upazilla: Banaripara
Specific Location: Iluhar Union
Details
In Iluhar Union of Banaripara Upazila of Barisal, Hindu man was beaten up and robbed of three lakh taka and two bhoris of gold ornaments. They were threatened with life and asked to leave the country. Although the family members were not willing to open their mouths out of fear, they know that they could not stay in this village anymore.
