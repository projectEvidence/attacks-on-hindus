215. More than 40 business establishments owned by minority Hindu was looted in Dinajpur community
Date of Incident:
August 4, 2024
Division: Rangpur
District: Dinajpur
Upazilla: Bochaganj
Specific Location: Chourongi Bazar, Ishania Union
Details
At least 40 minority shops were vandalized and looted in Chowrangi Bazar of Isania Union in Bochaganj Upazila of Dinajpur. On Monday afternoon, August 5, at least 100 people entered the market in a group and carried out an ambush. Later, when they moved towards Sahaspur village adjacent to the market, they were stopped by people from the minority community. Later, they left after threatening to leave their homes.

Eyewitness Hridoy Roy, a resident of Sahaspur village, said, "The attackers started vandalizing as soon as they entered the market. Our shops were selectively vandalized. The attackers had sticks and sharp weapons in their hands. We fled from the shop. Later, they looted the goods in front of our eyes. We were heading towards the village. At that time, when all of us in the neighborhood stood in front, they left."

  • • Loot

225. House of local Hindu temple committee president and his Hindu neighbours were attacked and looted
Date of Incident:
August 4, 2024
Division: Rajshahi
District: Natore
Upazilla: Lalpur
Details
The house of school teacher Dwipendranath Saha, the president of the Upazila Puja Udjapan Parishad and the president of the temple, was attacked. They broke one gate of his house. However, since they could not break the second gate, they broke the window from outside. Then the houses of his brother Naresh Chandra Saha and neighbor Shimul Sarkar were attacked. They broke the door and entered the house and caused extensive vandalism.

Naresh Chandra claimed that miscreants looted lakhs of Taka, gold jewellery, laptop, mobile phone, bags of rice from his house.
