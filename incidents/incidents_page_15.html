211. ISKCON temple in Meherpur was burnt to ashes by a muslim fundamentalist group
Date of Incident:
August 5, 2024

212. Business owned by Barun Kumar Ghosh looted during broad daylight in Satkhira
Date of Incident:
August 5, 2024
Division: Khulna
District: Satkhira
Upazilla: Kaliganj
Specific Location: Bharashimla Union
Details
On August 6, at around 10:30 am, more than a hundred armed people returning from the BNP procession on the south side of Kankshiali Bridge broke the shutters of “Ma Lakshmi Bhandar” and “Ma Lakshmi Muri Bhandar” and entered. First, they vandalized 10 CCTV cameras inside the mill. Then they looted 500 bags of 50 kg Atap rice, 700 bags of 28 rice of 50 and 25 kg, 1,100 bags of 50 kg coarse rice, 300 bags of miniket, 250 bags (25 kg) Basmati, 300 bags of fresh chopped rice (for frying Muri), 16 bags of gold, 750 bags of hybrid rice, 350 bags of wheat bran, 750 bags of Atap (polished) like kudra, 900 bags of kudra, 750 bags of corn, 200 bags of 28 paddy, 300 bags of coarse paddy, 350 bags of non-basmati paddy, 250 bags of peasant feed, along with the Muri mill, crush mill, shredder mill, rice mill, a tube well, 17 peas, an iron chest, and various other items. The attackers broke open the electric meter and took various items, including wires.
Involved Crimes
  • • Shop Vandalism
  • • Loot
  • • Arson

222. A Durga Temple and an under construction temple was vandalised and looted
Date of Incident:
August 4, 2024
Division: Rangpur
District: Dinajpur
Upazilla: Dinajpur Sadar
Specific Location: Fultola central crematorium ground
Details
The under construction Harishabha Ghar and Durga Temple at the Fultala Central Crematorium Ground in Dinajpur Sadar Upazila have been vandalised on August 5, 2024. During this attack, a group of 150 to 200 Muslim fundamentalists looted and vandalised the temple. People of the Hindu community are being intimidated into not speaking out about this incident. These fundamentalists had previously blocked the
temple work at the Fultala Central Crematorium ground which is situated on the bank of the Punarbhava River in Dinajpur city.

  • • Loot
