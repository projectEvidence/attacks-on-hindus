220. Seven Hindu temples were attacked and set on fire at night on August 5, 2024
Date of Incident:
August 4, 2024
Division: Rangpur
District: Thakurgaon
Upazilla: Pirganj
Specific Location: Kosharaniganj Union, No. 2Kasha Raniganj Union
Details
On Monday night, August 5, 2024, 7 Hindu temples in Pirganj were attacked and set on fire. Satirghat Sri Sri Kali Temple, Sri Shiv Temple, Sri Sri Saraswati Temple in Kosharaniganj Union of Pirganj Upazila were attacked and set on fire. Elua Pukur Sri Kali Temple in Saidpur Union, Sri Sri Chandi Thakurani Kali Temple in Shivpur Village, Senua Tilipukur Sri Kali Temple, Sati Dah Cremation Ghat and Temple in No. 2 Kasha Raniganj Union were attacked and vandalized by the Vijaya procession. The iron stove for cremation of the body of the crematorium, tube well, tin ceiling of the temple and even the motor for bathing the body were looted. The place for chanting Krishna's name in the crematorium field was heinously damaged.

223. 1971 war crime witness Ajit Sarkar was murdered on 05 August 2024
Date of Incident:
August 4, 2024
Division: Mymensingh
District: Mymensingh
Upazilla: Phulpur
Specific Location: Balia Union
Details
After the fall of Sheikh Hasina, on August 5, the house of Ajit Sarkar, a witness in a war crimes case, was attacked by the son and relatives of the accused in Domkona village of Balia Union in Phulpur, Mymensingh.
In 2016, Ajit Sarkar Penu testified in court in a war crimes case against Ghiyasuddin, a member of the 1971 Peace Committee.
The attack was led by Ghiyasuddin's son Lavlu on the afternoon of August 5. Ajit Sarkar sought refuge in the house of his neighbor Mortuza Mia to save his life. He was taken from there and dragged around Mahishaunda Bazar and forced to wear a garland made of shoes for being a witness in the case. Then, four or five people, including Lavlu, killed him on the river bank and dumped his body. On the evening of August 8, when the body was found floating downstream, Lavlu's nephew Moizuddin chopped it into pieces and dumped it a few kilometers away.

  • • House Vandalism
