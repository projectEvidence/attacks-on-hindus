216. Durga Temple was vandalised and looted
Date of Incident: August 4, 2024
Division: Barishal
District: Jhalokati
Upazilla: Kathalia
Specific Location: Uttarpar, Satani Bazar, Aurabunia Union
Details
On August 5, 2024, an attack was carried out on the Sri Sri Sarbajanin Durga Temple, north of Satani Bazar, Paschim Chitki village, Aurabunia union, Kanthalia upazila, Jhalokathi. Several hundreds islamist fundamentalists led by local Manjurul Sikder (50) took part in the attack. During this time, they vandalised and looted various items. The people of the minority Hindu community were unable to resist the sudden preplanned attack by the muslim fundamentalist group. They complained to the police. The police arrested the main conspirator, Manjurul Sikder, but the rest were not arrested. They are pressuring the Hindus to withdraw the case.

Date of Incident: August 4, 2024
Division: Barishal
District: Patuakhali
Upazilla: Kalapara
Details
The residence of Awami League Joint General Secretary Ananta Mukherjee and a temple in the nearby area of ​​Kuakata Municipality in Patuakhali were attacked. The incident took place around 4:30 pm.

Ananta Mukherjee alleged, "After hearing the news of the government's resignation, BNP-Jamaat leaders and activists took out a procession in Kuakata Municipality. The procession came towards my house. At one stage, people from the procession attacked my house. Many of the attackers entered my house. They broke and smashed every thing in the house. Nothing in my house was saved. Then the attackers attacked the temple next to my house. The doors and windows of the temple were damaged."
