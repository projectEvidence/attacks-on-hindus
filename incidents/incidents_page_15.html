213. Durga Temple was vandalised and looted
Date of Incident:
August 4, 2024
Division: Barishal
District: Jhalokati
Upazilla: Kathalia
Specific Location: Uttarpar, Satani Bazar, Aurabunia Union
Details
On August 5, 2024, an attack was carried out on the Sri Sri Sarbajanin Durga Temple, north of Satani Bazar, Paschim Chitki village, Aurabunia union, Kanthalia upazila, Jhalokathi. Several hundreds islamist fundamentalists led by local Manjurul Sikder (50) took part in the attack. During this time, they vandalised and looted various items. The people of the minority Hindu community were unable to resist the sudden preplanned attack by the muslim fundamentalist group. They complained to the police. The police arrested the main conspirator, Manjurul Sikder, but the rest were not arrested. They are pressuring the Hindus to withdraw the case.

217. Miscreants looted all the belongings of Lakshmi Rani Pal of Bejpara, Jessore city
Date of Incident:
August 4, 2024
Division: Khulna
District: Jashore
Upazilla: Jessore Sadar
Specific Location: Banani Road, Bejpara
Details
Lakshmi Rani Pal lives with her parents on the ground floor of Paresh Basu's house on Banani Road in Bejpara, Jessore town. Her only source of income is a sewing machine. After Sheikh Hasina's resignation, miscreants broke into the main gate of the house at around 9:15 pm last Monday and looted Lakshmi Rani's sewing machine, cooking stove, gas cylinder and 10 baht of gold jewelry belonging to her brother's newly married wife kept in the cupboard. Lakshmi Rani's family members have been leaving the area fearing for their lives. They have taken shelter in a relative's house in another area.
