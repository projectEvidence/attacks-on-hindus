102. A muslim man named Ali Noor was caught red handed while vandalizing Kalimandir idols in Kushtia
Date of Incident:
November 20, 2024
Division: Khulna
District: Kushtia
Upazilla: Kushtia Sadar
Specific Location: Uttar Magura, ​​Goswami Durgapur Union
Details
The incident took place at around 7:30 pm at the Mahasmashan Kali Mandir in Uttar Magura area of ​​Goswami Durgapur Union of Sadar Upazila.

According to local resident Madhav Saha, while passing in front of the temple last night, he saw a young man vandalizing the idols by inserting a bamboo stick into the grill of the Kali Mandir. The young man first pulled the Kali idols near the grill with a bamboo stick. Then he continued vandalizing. Madhav Saha first recorded the scene on his mobile phone camera. Then, with the help of local people, he arrested him and informed the police.
