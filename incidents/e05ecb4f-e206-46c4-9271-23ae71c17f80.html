118. Muslim Bengali settlers destroyed and looted Banarupa Maitree Vihar (বনরুপা মৈত্রী বিহার) in Banarupa, Kathal Toli, Rangamati
Date of Incident:
September 20, 2024
Division: Chattogram
District: Rangamati
Upazilla: Rangamati Sadar
Specific Location: Banarupa, Kathal Toli (বনরুপা, কাঠাল তলী)
Details
Muslim Bengali settlers destroyed and looted Banarupa Maitree Vihar (বনরুপা মৈত্রী বিহার) in Banarupa, Kathal Toli, Rangamati (বনরুপা, কাঠাল তলী, রাংগামাটি). They stole the donation box because they were unable to break it.

On September 20, clashes broke out between tribals and settler Bengalis in Rangamati. In this, Anik Chakma (17) was beaten to death while sitting in a tea shop 200 yards from the DC office. Although the killers' clear faces and identities were confirmed in the video footage, they have not been arrested yet. Vandalism was carried out in Banarupa Maitree Vihar during the incident. The office of the Chittagong Hill Tracts Regional Council was burned. No one has been charged or arrested in these incidents as of the publishing date of https://drive.google.com/file/d/1WATrOCOBYteGnKWZgBM548VAVJCwBe_3/view?usp=drive_link
