30. Muslims robbed Paul Majumder Khokon's house in Kotalipara, Gopalganj in broad daylight and killed his son Piyas Majumder
Date of Incident:
March 10, 2025
Division: Dhaka
District: Gopalganj
Upazilla: Kotalipara
Specific Location: Lakirpara, Kushla Union (লাকিরপাড়া, কুশলা ইউনিয়ন)
Details
A robbery incident took place in the afternoon at the house of a man named Paul Majumder Khokon in Kotalipara, Gopalganj. At that time, the robbers tied Paul Majumder Khokon's son Piyas Majumder (22) and killed him, and looted cash and gold ornaments.

The robbery took place on Tuesday afternoon, March 11, in Lakirpara village of Kushla Union of the upazila.

It is learned that Paul Majumder Khokon is a dentist and his wife Anita Baidya is a nurse. As usual, the two went to work on Tuesday morning. In the afternoon, Paul Majumder Khokon came to his house and saw his son Piyas Majumder tied up on the bed and screamed. At that time, people around rushed to take Piyas Majumder to the Kotalipara Upazila Health Center, where the doctor on duty declared him dead.
