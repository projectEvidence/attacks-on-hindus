62. Hindu Family Temple Set on Fire in Dhaka’s Turag Area
Date of Incident:
December 6, 2024
Division: Dhaka
District: Dhaka
Police Station: Turag
Details
A complaint has been filed alleging that a family temple in the Turag area of the capital was set on fire. A case has been filed at the Turag Police Station against unidentified miscreants. According to the case, the incident occurred late Friday night.

Babul Chandra Ghosh, the plaintiff, said today that on Friday around 11:30 PM, after having dinner, the family went to sleep. Adjacent to their bedroom is the Shri Shri Mahabhagya Lakshmi Narayan Temple, which is their family temple. Around 2:30 AM, they woke up to the sound of footsteps. Upon checking, they saw that the temple was on fire. With the help of locals, they managed to extinguish the fire.
