144. Pakuria Palpara Temple in Pakuria Union of Bagha Upazila was vandalized
Date of Incident:
August 22, 2024
Division: Rajshahi
District: Rajshahi
Upazilla: Bagha
Specific Location: Palpara, Pakuria Union
Details
Rabbi Hossain, a student of a Qawmi Madrasa in Rajshahi, attacked temples in Bagha Upazila of Rajshahi and vandalized idols on the pretext that the people of Bangladesh are suffering from flood water due to the release of water by India. On Friday, August 23, 2024, in the early morning, attack was carried out by breaking the locks of the Pakuria Palpara Temple in Pakuria Union of Bagha Upazila.
