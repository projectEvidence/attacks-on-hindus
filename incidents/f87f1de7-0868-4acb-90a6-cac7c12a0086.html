49. Temple vendalism after Friday Jumma Namaz at Chittagong's Shantaneshwari Kali Mandir
Date of Incident:
November 28, 2024
Division: Chattogram
District: Chattogram
Police Station: Kotwali, CMP
Specific Location: Patharghata
Details
On Friday, around 2:30 pm, the Shantaneshwari Kali Mandir was vandalized in Harish Chandra Munsef Lane in Patharghata, a part of the city.
On that day, after Friday prayers at Andarkilla Mosque, Hefazat-e-Islam took out a procession demanding the ban on ISKCON. Hundreds of people from the procession, chanting various slogans, pelted bricks and vandalized the temple.
