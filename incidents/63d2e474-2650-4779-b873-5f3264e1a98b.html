41. Three Hindu families were forcibly evicted by Akhtaruzzaman Sohaag, the nephew of former Awami League supported MP Zakir Hossain Sarkar in Mithapukur, Rangpur. Court Injunction ignored.
Date of Incident:
February 14, 2025
Division: Rangpur
District: Rangpur
Upazilla: Mithapukur
Details
Allegations of land grabbing against a former MP's nephew by Hindus have emerged. A press conference was held at the Mithapukur Press Club's hall room at 4 PM. Members of three victimized families were present at the event.

The nephew of former MP Zakir Hossain Sarkar, who is a member of the current advisory committee and a former general secretary of Mithapukur Upazila Awami League, has been accused of seizing land belonging to the Hindu community. In protest, the victims held a press conference on Wednesday.During the conference, victim Profulla Saha stated in a written statement that Akhtaruzzaman Sohaag, the nephew of former MP Zakir Hossain Sarkar from the Awami League government, has forcefully and unjustly occupied our ancestral property by exerting influence.

After Zakir Hossain Sarkar became the MP from the Upazila Chairman position, Sohaag has perpetrated oppression and torture on the minorities. He forcibly occupied 9 decimal (0.09 acres) of my ancestral homestead and built a wall, evicting our three families, in violation of a court injunction.
