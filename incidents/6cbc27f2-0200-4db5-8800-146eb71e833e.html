65. A woman devotee was attacked and injured by stones and bricks during a program of Bangladesh Sevashram in the presence of the police in Debidwar, Comilla,
Date of Incident:
February 19, 2025
Division: Chattogram
District: Comilla
Upazilla: Debidwar
Details
During a program of Bangladesh Sevashram in Debidwar, Comilla, a woman devotee was attacked and injured by stones and bricks. The miscreants also threw bricks at the congregation's program in the presence of the police. The incident took place on February 20 during a program at the house of Manik Dutta in Debidwar upazila.
