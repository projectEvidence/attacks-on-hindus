197. Six Hindu households in Malopara , Meherpur was attacked and vandalised
Date of Incident:
August 4, 2024
Division: Khulna
District: Meherpur
Upazilla: Meherpur-S
Specific Location: Malopara
Details
On Monday evening, August 5, miscreants attacked and vandalized six houses in Malopara. They beat up family members while leaving.

The miscreants have been on the rampage since Monday afternoon. They have not been able to get any help from the police.
