112. Neighbour Muslims raped and killed a Hindu Housewife; and dumped her dead body in septic tank
Date of Incident:
September 23, 2024
Division: Khulna
District: Jashore
Upazilla: Abhaynagar
Specific Location: Devpara area of ​​Bhatpara village in Baghutia union of Abhaynagar upazila
Details
Housewife Sabita Rani Dey (50) went missing in September last year while collecting cow fodder in Abhaynagar, Jessore. Her body was recovered from a neighbor's septic tank 33 hours later. The matter came to light only recently when the police received the viscera report after about five months. The report states that the housewife was raped and strangled to death.

On the evening of September 24, 2024, the police recovered the body of housewife Sabita Rani Dey from the Devpara area of ​​Bhatpara village in Baghutia union of Abhaynagar upazila. The body was sent to the morgue of the 250-bed Jessore Hospital for autopsy. Samples were collected for viscera testing and sent to the CID forensic lab. The police recently received the viscera report.

A day after the body was recovered, on September 25, housewife Sabita Rani's husband Milan Kumar Dey filed a case with Abhaynagar police station. The case named Niyamul Sheikh (26), his father Ramzan Sheikh (49) and uncle Yunus Sheikh (41) of Devpara area of ​​neighboring Bhatpara village. The police have also arrested three people. Among the three, Niyamul Sheikh is in jail. The other two have been released on bail.

According to the case sources, the housewife went to the bamboo garden near her house on the morning of September 23 to collect cow feed. After that, when she did not return home, her family members and neighbors searched for her. About 33 hours after she went missing, her body was recovered from the septic tank of Niyamul Sheikh's toilet adjacent to the bamboo garden near her house.

Locals said that the husband of the traditional housewife is a shingara, puri and tea shop owner. He has a shop in the local Bhatpara market. Their two daughters are married. A husband and wife lived in the house.

When asked about this, the investigating officer of the case, Bhatpara Police Investigation Center Sub-Inspector (SI) Nazrul Islam, told Ajker Patrika that the viscera report of the housewife's body was received a few days ago. The report states that she was raped and strangled to death. After the investigation, the charge sheet of the case will be sent to the court.
