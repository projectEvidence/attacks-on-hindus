175. The house of Laxman Das in Patuakhali Sadar Upazilla was looted by the locals
Date of Incident:
August 4, 2024
Division: Barishal
District: Patuakhali
Upazilla: Patuakhali Sadar
Specific Location: Laukathi Union
Details
