11. Suspicious Death of Hindu Student at Notre Dame College Raises Questions of Foul Play
Date of Incident:
May 11, 2025
Division: Dhaka
District: Dhaka
Police Station: Motijheel
Specific Location: 6th floor, Father Tim Building, Notre Dame College
Details
On the afternoon of May 12, 2025, 18-year-old Dhruvabrata Das, a Hindu HSC examinee from the science stream of Notre Dame College in Dhaka, tragically died after falling from the sixth floor of the college’s Father Tim building in Motijheel. While authorities have so far treated the incident as a fall, the victim’s mother, Tama Rani Singh, has strongly alleged that her son was pushed and murdered, dismissing the idea that it was an accident.

Dhruvabrata, the eldest of three siblings, originally hailed from Madhyapara in Gaibandha Sadar but had been living with his family in a rented flat in Tikatuli, Dhaka. He had gone to the college that day to collect his test exam result card, accompanied by his father. Since guardians were not allowed into the building, his father waited outside. When Dhruvabrata didn’t return after more than an hour, his parents tried to call him repeatedly, but received no answer. Soon after, some students brought his unconscious body outside, stating he had fallen from a high floor. Though initially rushed to Islami Bank Hospital and then to Dhaka Medical College Hospital, he was declared dead on arrival.

Tama Rani Singh tearfully stated, “My son was gentle, disciplined, and not the kind of boy to accidentally fall. Someone must have pushed him off that building.” She also pointed out that there were no apparent behavioral or academic issues that could suggest suicidal intent. Her plea for justice reflects a growing concern among minority communities in Bangladesh regarding unexplained and suspicious deaths, particularly when they involve members of the Hindu community.

Police have confirmed the incident and the body is currently at the emergency department of DMCH. The family and broader Hindu community are demanding a thorough and impartial investigation to determine whether Dhruvabrata's death was a tragic accident or a deliberate act of communal violence masked as misfortune.
