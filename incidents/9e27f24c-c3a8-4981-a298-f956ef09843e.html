89. The idols of Maa Bhabani Durga and Kalimata temples in Battalahat area of ​​Chapainawabganj Sadar Upazila were vandalized.
Date of Incident:
October 4, 2024
Division: Rajshahi
District: Chapai Nawabganj
Upazilla: Nawabganj Sadar
Specific Location: Bottolahat(বটতলাহাট)
Details
The idols of Maa Bhabani Durga and Kalimata temples in Battalahat area of ​​Chapainawabganj Sadar Upazila were vandalized. Although the administration inspected the site, such incidents are happening again and again in the society due to the lack of strong action. Never before has such idols been vandalized in Chapainawabganj. However, the Hindu community of the district is in fear due to the vandalization of the idols this year.
