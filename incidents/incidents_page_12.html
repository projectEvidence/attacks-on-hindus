House and Business establishment of Bikash Pal was looted and vandalized
Date of Incident: August 4, 2024
Division: Khulna
District: Jashore
Upazilla: Keshabpur
Specific Location: Pajiya Bazar
Details
On Monday, August 5, at around 3:30 pm, the house of rice trader Bikash Chandra Pal in Pajia Bazar was attacked and vandalized. At the same time, his business establishment was also looted. 200 sacks of rice, 250 sacks of flour, 450 sacks of khail and 250 sacks of husk and nine lakh taka were looted from the establishment. On the same day, a man named Ripon from the Notunhat area of ​​the upazila demanded 2 lakh taka from him. Ripon also took 50 thousand taka from him.

A group of people with covered face, robbed half a dozen Hindu house in Shalborat
Date of Incident: August 4, 2024
Division: Khulna
District: Jashore
Upazilla: Bagherpara
Specific Location: Shalborat
Details
On the night of August 5, 2024, a group of 10 to 12 people attacked the Hindu neighborhood of Shalborat village in the upazila. 65 Hindu families live in this neighborhood. They went to five to six houses with black cloths tied around their faces and ramadas in their hands. The people of the neighborhood said that out of fear, everyone in the neighborhood somehow spent the night in the neighboring garden. On Tuesday morning, the men left the neighborhood and went elsewhere.
