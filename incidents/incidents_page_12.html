166. The house of former chairman of Maheshwaripur in Koyra, Bijoy Kumar Sardar, was attacked
Date of Incident: August 4, 2024
Division: Khulna
District: Khulna
Upazilla: Koyra
Specific Location: Maheshwaripur Union
Details
The house of former chairman of Maheshwaripur UP in Koyra, Bijoy Kumar Sardar, was attacked in the morning. Bijoy Kumar said that when the miscreants attacked in the morning, no one was at home. The miscreants looted all the belongings in the house and set the house on fire.

Eyewitnesses said that at around 10 am today, a group of people attacked Bijoy Kumar's house with sticks, rods, iron pipes and petrol. They first broke the lock of the entrance gate of the house and looted it. Then they gathered the furniture in the house in one place and set it on fire.

171. Twenty Hindu households were attacked and looted by Muslim mob
Date of Incident: August 4, 2024
Division: Khulna
District: Jashore
Upazilla: Bagherpara
Specific Location: Rishipalli, Sultannagar , Dholgram Union
Details
About 90 Hindu families and more than 500 people live in Sultannagar Rishipalli. Most of them are poor. Even so, 12 houses were looted after the attack, said Nalin Chandra Biswas, president of the Harichand Mandir Committee. They took away more than a hundred poultry, a goat, gold coins, cash, rice, pulses, oil, bamboo baskets or kulas, whatever they found in front of them from these houses. The attackers also opened the only deep tube well in the village.

On that day, after the fall of the Sheikh Hasina government, a group of 40 to 50 people attacked the Hindupalli at around 6:30 pm. Each of them had indigenous weapons like ramda, hansua, iron rods, sabals, and sticks.

  • • Loot
  • • House Vandalism

175. Hindu temple was demolished and looted
Date of Incident: August 4, 2024
Division: Dhaka
District: Shariatpur
Upazilla: Shariatpur Sadar -Palong
Specific Location: Dhanuka Manasa Bari
Details
A temple at Dhanuka Mansa Bari in Shariatpur district town has been demolished. On the night of August 5, a Muslim mob demolished the temple and looted the furniture. The Krishna temple was constructed in March. The Krishna idol in the temple was vandalised and destroyed. The attackers had surrounded nearby Hindu houses to vandalise them. Later in the night, when the army members went there, the miscreants fled.
