177. The house of Narad Biswas was looted and attacked on August 7 in Lamaparkul village of Ichakals Union
Date of Incident:
August 6, 2024
Division: Sylhet
District: Sylhet
Upazilla: Companiganj
Specific Location: Lamaparkul, Ichakals union
Details
The attack took place at 2 pm on Wednesday (August 7) ​​at the house of Narad Biswas, son of the deceased Devendra Biswas, of Lamaparkul village in Ichakals union of the upazila. According to local sources, Narad Biswas, a prominent stone merchant of Lamaparkul village, is currently in jail in a case. Only his wife Sandhya Biswas lived in the house. Last Wednesday at 2 pm, a group of miscreants led by Sayeed Ahmed, president of the Ichakals union branch of Bangladesh Islami Chhatra Shibir, attacked Narad Biswas's house and vandalized it extensively. Fearing for his life, Sandhya Biswas fled through the back door of the house and took shelter in a neighboring house. At that time, the attackers broke the door of the house and entered, vandalized the furniture and looted gold ornaments and cash worth 43,000 taka from the cupboard. When the neighbors came out at the sound of the vandalism, the terrorists ran away. The neighbors said that Narad Biswas is a very peace-loving person. He is not involved in any kind of politics.
