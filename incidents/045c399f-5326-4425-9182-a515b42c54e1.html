106. An attack was carried out in broad daylight at the Sri Sri Loknath Brahmachari Temple near the Kotwali Police Station in Chittagong city
Date of Incident:
November 25, 2024
Division: Chattogram
District: Chattogram
Police Station: Kotwali, CMP
Specific Location: Firingibazar(ফিরিঙ্গীবাজার)
Details
A broad daylight attack was carried out on the Sri Sri Loknath Brahmachari Temple near the Kotwali Police Station in Chittagong city. On the afternoon of November 26, 100-150 people carrying sticks and bricks and stones carried out the attack. The temple authorities have informed that the police played a silent role when they sought help from the police station during the attack. The police have not filed any case in this incident either.
