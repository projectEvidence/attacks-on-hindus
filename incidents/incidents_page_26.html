382. Schoolgirl Mukti Rani Barman hacked to death for refusing love proposal
Date of Incident:
May 1, 2023
Division: Mymensingh
District: Netrokona
Upazilla: Barhatta Upazilla
Details
A student named Mukti Rani Barman (16) was hacked to death by a miscreant on her way back from school in Barhatta upazila of Netrokona. She was taken to Mymensingh Medical College Hospital around 5 pm on Tuesday, where doctors declared her dead.

The deceased Mukti was the daughter of Nikhil Barman of Premnagar village in the upazila and a class 10 student of Premnagar Chalipura High School. The accused Kausar Mia (21) is the son of Shamsu Mia of the same village.

Liton Barman, uncle of the deceased Mukti, said that Kausar had been harassing his niece for a long time on her way to and from school. The matter was also informed to her family. Today, Kausar injured Mukti on her way back from school. Later, she was rescued and taken to Barhatta Upazila Health Complex, where doctors sent her to Netrokona Modern Sadar Hospital after providing primary treatment. From there, she was declared dead after being taken to Mymensingh Medical College Hospital.

Mukti's elder sister Rujina Rani Barman has claimed that Kausar used to harass Mukti Rani. She said, "Kausar used to harass me too. Due to his threats, I got admitted to Netrokona Adarsh ​​High School after passing fifth grade. I tried to take legal action against Kausar's father Shamsur, but it did not work. After I left, he started harassing my sister. Kausar is killing my sister because he did not listen to her. I want justice for this."

Mukti's classmates said that they were returning home from school together at around 2:45 pm today. After reaching the Chalipura area, about two kilometers away from the school, Mukti suddenly started hitting her with a crowbar. At this time, they got scared and started screaming. Local people rushed to rescue Mukti and took her to Barhatta Hospital.

Abul Khair Akand, headmaster of Premnagar Chalipura High School, said that Mukti's family is very poor. The girl was calm and intelligent. She was a student of the humanities branch. The matter is very tragic. We demand the immediate arrest of the scoundrel and exemplary punishment.

Barhatta Police Station Officer-in-Charge Khokon Kumar Saha said that the police are conducting raids at various places to arrest the accused. The body of the deceased has been kept in the morgue of Mymensingh Medical College.

383. Schoolgirl from Hindu Businessman’s Family Abducted in Barguna — Journalist Arrested
Date of Incident:
October 1, 2020
Division: Barishal
District: Patuakhali
Upazilla: Kalapara
Specific Location: Kuakata tourist area
Details
A shocking incident of abduction has rattled the Hindu community in Barguna district, where a ninth-grade schoolgirl, the daughter of a Hindu cloth merchant from Barguna municipal area, was kidnapped. The prime accused is Md. Abdul Azim, the Barguna correspondent of Somoy TV.

According to the victim’s family, the abduction took place on October 2, 2025, when the girl went missing under suspicious circumstances. Her father, suspecting a premeditated crime, filed a case at Barguna Sadar Police Station against Abdul Azim and two other accomplices. Acting on a tip-off, police from Mohipur Police Station launched an early morning operation at Kuakata tourist area on October 3, where they arrested Abdul Azim and successfully rescued the abducted schoolgirl.

Law enforcement confirmed the arrest, stating that the accused would be handed over to Barguna Sadar Police along with the rescued minor. The Hindu community in Barguna has expressed deep concern over the targeting of a young girl from a minority family, fearing both communal hostility and abuse of influence by powerful individuals. They are demanding a swift trial, maximum punishment for the perpetrators, and stronger safeguards for Hindu girls, who remain vulnerable to such targeted crimes in Bangladesh.

386. Terror Attack on Kali Puja Mandap in Atrai: Worship Disrupted, Women Assaulted, Four Arrested
Date of Incident:
July 6, 2001
Division: Rajshahi
District: Naogaon
Upazilla: Atrai
Specific Location: Kali Puja Mandap
Details
Four individuals have been arrested in connection with a violent attack on a Hindu religious gathering in Dangapara village of Atrai upazila, which disrupted a Kali Puja celebration and left several worshippers injured.

According to eyewitnesses and police sources, the incident took place on the night of July 7, when members of the minority Hindu community were engaged in a Kali Puja at the local temple. A group of local miscreants allegedly approached Haripada Prang, president of the Dangapara Kali Mandir Puja Committee, demanding extortion money. When he refused, the attackers began assaulting him and others with punches and sticks, forcing the puja to come to a halt.

The attackers also reportedly vandalized nearby houses belonging to members of the minority community and issued death threats to Haripada Prang.

On July 8, Haripada Prang filed a case with Atrai Police Station, naming 12 individuals as accused. Police have since arrested four of the suspects, who have been sent to jail following preliminary legal proceedings.
