38. Hamidul Islam Arif arrested for robbing a Hindu temple in Belkuchi, Sirajganj, Rajshahi: stolen idol recovered.
Date of Incident:
February 20, 2025
Division: Rajshahi
District: Sirajganj
Upazilla: Belkuchi
Specific Location: Sri Sri Ramakrishna Seva Ashram temple, Delua
Details
Robbery at a temple in Belkuchi, thief arrested from in-laws' house. Police have arrested a person named Hamidul Islam alias Arif in connection with the theft of three brass idols and gold last month from the Belkuchi Delua Sri Sri Ramakrishna Seva Ashram temple.

The arrest was made during a raid on Friday night (February 21) at Arif's in-laws' house in Bolgati village. Stolen items were recovered from him at that time. Arrested Arif is the son of Dadu Sheikh from Rupnai village under Enayetpur police station.

Officer in Charge (OC) of Belkuchi police station, Mohammad Zakeriya Hossain, stated that brass idols and gold were stolen from the temple last month. Since a case was filed regarding this incident, we have been active in recovering the stolen items. Based on secret information, we conducted a raid on Arif's in-laws' house last night, recovering the stolen items and arresting him. He was sent to court today (February 22).
