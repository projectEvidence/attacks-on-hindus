183. Muslim fundamentalists attacked and looted Jotdaivaki Shiva and Kali Temple (জোতদৈবকী শিব ও কালী মন্দির) in Lalpur, Natore
Date of Incident:
August 5, 2024
Division: Rajshahi
District: Natore
Upazilla: Lalpur
Specific Location: Jotdaivaki Shiva and Kali Temple
Details
The attack on the Jotdaivaki Shiva and Kali Temple in Lalpur, Natore took place on 05 August 2024. A Muslim fundamentalist group attacked the Jotdaivaki Shiva and Kali Temple and broke 8 CCTV cameras in the temple. Then they vandalized and looted the temple.
