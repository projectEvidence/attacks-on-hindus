219. Muslim fundamentalist attacked and looted Shiva-Kali Temple
Date of Incident:
August 4, 2024
Division: Rajshahi
District: Natore
Upazilla: Lalpur
Details
The attack on the Jotdaiwaki Shiva and Kali Temple in Lalpur, Natore took place on 05 August 2024. A Muslim fundamentalist group attacked the Jotdaiwaki Shiva and Kali Temple and broke 8 CCTV cameras in the temple. Then they vandalized and looted the temple.
