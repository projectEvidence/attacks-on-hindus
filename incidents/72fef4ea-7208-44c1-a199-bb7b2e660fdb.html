51. 75 year old Hindu woman killed with hands and legs tied in Pirojpur on February 07, 2025
Date of Incident:
February 6, 2025
Division: Barishal
District: Pirojpur
Upazilla: Nazirpur
Specific Location: Shakhmatia Union (শেখমাটিয়া ইউনিয়নের মৌখালী গ্রাম)
Details
Miscreants have killed a 75 year old Hindu woman tying her hands and legs. Miscreants entered her home cutting a ditch under her house in moukhali village of Shakhmatia Union of Pirojpur district. The reason for the murder is not initially known, but the family claims that the murder may have been caused by a land dispute with a local group.
