37. Unknown Youth Attacked the temple at Raipur Mahamaya Mandir in Laxmipur
Date of Incident:
March 12, 2025
Division: Chattogram
District: Lakshmipur
Upazilla: Raipur
Details
An incident of deity vandalism has occurred at a temple in Raipur upazila of Lakshmipur. The incident took place around 9 PM on Thursday night at the Shri Shri Mahamaya Temple in Raipur municipality of the district. Following the incident, the army, police, and leaders of various political parties visited the site.

Rabindra Karmakar, the president of the temple committee, said that the deity of Goddess Saraswati was vandalized. The temple priest had gone to a nearby house to conduct a ritual. During that time, someone entered the temple and vandalized the deity . Temple priest Haradhan Chakraborty said, “I was out for a short while, and when I returned, I saw that someone had broken the deity in the temple.”

Police said CCTV footage from around the temple has been collected. The footage shows a young man entering the temple with a cloth covering his face. He then vandalized the deity and fled. Police officials stated that the act was carried out with the intention of creating unrest in the country.

Jamilul Haque, Assistant Superintendent of Police (Raipur Circle), told Prothom Alo that the incident appears to be deliberate. Efforts are underway to identify the masked youth. Those involved in the incident will be identified and brought under the law.

Milon Mondal, General Secretary of the Lakshmipur District Hindu-Buddhist-Christian Unity Council, said the incident must be investigated promptly and those responsible brought to justice. He urged strong intervention from the administration on this matter.
