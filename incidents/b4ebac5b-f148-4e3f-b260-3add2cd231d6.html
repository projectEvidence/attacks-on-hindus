65. Hindu youth murdered and hanged from tree
Date of Incident:
January 24, 2025
Division: Khulna
District: Satkhira
Police Station: Asashuni
Details
Animesh Sarkar (Cycle mechanic), the only son of Niranjan Sarkar of Langaldaria village under Ashashuni police station in Satkhira district, was murdered and his body was left hanging.

From the news on timestodaybd.com: The body of a bicycle mechanic named Animesh Sarkar was found hanging from a branch of a neem tree in Sriula Union of Asashuni Upazila of Satkhira, far from his house. Animesh's family has claimed that he was hanged from a tree branch after being killed. Police reached the spot on Saturday morning and recovered the body. Animesh, son of Niranjan Sarkar of Langaldaria village of the union, used to work as a bicycle mechanic in the market next to his house. His mother Shefali Sarkar and wife Sanjita Sarkar said that neighbors Ahid Mallick (অহিদ মল্লিক), son of deceased Omar Mallick, and A. Malek Mallick (আঃ মালেক মল্লিক) (convener of Sriula Union "শ্রীউলা ইউনিয়ন" BNP) had been mentally torturing, beating and threatening Animesh in various ways.
