82. A heinous theft had taken place at 4 temples in Boalkhali,Chattogram
Date of Incident:
February 4, 2025
Division: Chattogram
District: Chattogram
Upazilla: Boalkhali
Details
A heinous theft has taken place at 4 temples in Boalkhali, Chattogram.The thief broke the lock of the grill at the Sri Sri Siddheshwari Matri Temple, Navodaya Club in Purba Shakpura, Boalkhali, and broke the lock of the main temple, stealing items worth about one lakh taka. This thief also stole from the others puja mandap of 3 other houses in the vicinity this morning.
Involved Crimes
  • • Loot
  • • Robbery
  • • Temple Vandalism
