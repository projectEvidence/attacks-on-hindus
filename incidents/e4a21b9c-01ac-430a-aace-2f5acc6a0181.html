66. Hindus protesting unjustified arrest of Chinmoy Krishna Das were physically assaulted. Women leaders of Hindu organizations were also injured.
Date of Incident:
November 26, 2024
Division: Dhaka
District: Dhaka
Upazilla: Dhaka City
Specific Location: Shahbagh
Details
The Hindu Buddhist Christian Unity Council has alleged that several people including Kushal Baran Chakraborty and Advocate Ramen Roy were attacked and left bleeding during the protest in Shahbagh. Three or four women leaders of Hindu organizations were also injured in the incident. Some of the injured are undergoing treatment at various hospitals.
