49. Under pressure from muslim fundamentalist students Anupam Sen was forced to step down from his position as Vice-Chancellor of Premier University
Date of Incident:
December 6, 2024
Division: Chattogram
District: Chattogram
Police Station: Kotwali, CMP
Specific Location: Premier University, Prabartak Circle, 1/A O.R. Nizam Rd, Chittagong 4203
Details
In the Premier University of Chattogram muslim fundamentalist students organised protest and and asked for resignation of Dr. Anupam Sen. Under immense pressure Dr. Sen was forced to resign. In Bangladesh there is widespread incidents of forceful resignations. Hindu officials are taking the much of the blunts of those activities. We have seen Hindu police officers, doctors and teachers were forcefully removed from their positions in past few months. Dr. Anupam Sen is another victim.
On Friday night, he sent his resignation letter to the Chancellor of the university, the President of the country. Since Wednesday, a group of students had been staging demonstrations demanding the resignation of the Vice-Chancellor, Pro-Vice-Chancellor, and Treasurer of Premier University.
