51. Students and Coordinator of "বৈষম্যবিরোধী ছাত্র আন্দোলন" incited hatred against ISKCON at Dhaka University Campus
Date of Incident:
November 26, 2024
Division: Dhaka
District: Dhaka
Upazilla: Dhaka City
Specific Location: Dhaka University Campus
Details
DU students from Lohagara and Satkania protested on the campus. They demanded the arrest of Alif's killers within 24 hours and the banning of ISKCON. Tahmid Al Muddassir, coordinator of the student movement against discrimination, said at the protest rally, "There is no precedent in the history of this country for the killing of a government lawyer. ISKCON did just that. ISKCON is banned and action must be taken to find out their source of funding."

The date of the incident is either November 26 or November 27, 2024 (the news was published on Nov 27) Source: https://samakal.com/bangladesh/article/267302/চিন্ময়ের-গ্রেপ্তার-ঘিরে-সংঘাত-চট্টগ্রামে-আইনজীবী-নিহত
