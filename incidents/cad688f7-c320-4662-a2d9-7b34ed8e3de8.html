93. Many of the 10 minority families who were attacked and looted on or after August 5 in Patharghata Upazila of Barguna have not been able to return home, BNP leaders are demanding extortion
Date of Incident:
October 24, 2024
Division: Barishal
District: Barguna
Upazilla: Patharghata
Specific Location: Kathaltoli, Chorduani
Details
Many of the 10 minority families who were attacked and looted in Patharghata upazila of Barguna on or after August 5 have not been able to return home. The head of some families has left their homes, while the entire family has left the area. Some have alleged that BNP leaders are demanding extortion to return home. However, BNP leaders have denied the allegations.
It is learned from the spot that Anjan Bhattacharya, a member of Patharghata's Kathaltoli Union Parishad, and Sujan Roy, a resident of the same union, have not been able to return home with their entire families. The families of Arjun Hawlader, Shailen Hawlader and Debashish Sengupta of Charduani village of the taluk are in the area, but they are on the run.
