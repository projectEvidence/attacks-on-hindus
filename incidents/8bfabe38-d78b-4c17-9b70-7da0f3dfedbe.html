260. Miscreants attacked the house of Shusen Chandra Shil in Banshpara
Date of Incident:
August 4, 2024
Division: Chattogram
District: Feni
Upazilla: Feni Sadar
Details
Miscreants attacked the house of Feni Sadar Upazila Parishad Chairman and Sadar Upazila Awami League General Secretary Shusen Chandra Shil in Banshpara. The house was set on fire by the fire service, who arrived and brought the fire under control.
